## Detangling Brush vs Standard Brush

**Standard brush on wet Indian hair:** Rigid bristles catch tangles and snap hair at the weakest point — wet hair is 30% weaker than dry. Indian women with long hair lose significant length annually to wet-hair detangling breakage.

**Detangling brush:** Flexible, wide-set bristles bend around tangles rather than ripping through them. Detangle from tip to root (not root to tip) — work through ends first, progressively moving up.

**Electric detangler:** Motorised paddle that vibrates or oscillates — continuously adjusts bristle angle to tangles, further reducing force needed. Best for children's hair and very knotted Indian hair.
