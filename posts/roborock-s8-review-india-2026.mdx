## Detailed Reviews

### 1. Roborock S8 — Best Entry-Level S8

**Price:** ₹44,999–₹49,999

The base S8 introduces the DuoRoller brush system — Roborock's biggest engineering leap in recent years. Two rubber rollers replace the traditional bristle brush, which means hair wrapping (a constant headache with older robot vacuums) is almost entirely eliminated. For Indian families with long hair or pets, this alone justifies the upgrade from older models. At 6,000 Pa suction, it handles the fine dust and debris common in Indian homes with ease.

The S8 includes sonic mopping that vibrates 3,000 times per minute. On tile and marble, this delivers a noticeably cleaner finish compared to static mop pads. The mop automatically lifts when the robot detects a carpet or rug, so you can run a full vacuum-and-mop cycle without manual intervention.

Navigation uses PreciSense LiDAR with ReactiveAI 2.0 obstacle avoidance — it maps accurately and handles common Indian floor obstacles like slippers, charging cables, and furniture legs. The Roborock app is exceptional by any standard: granular zone controls, scheduled cleaning, room-specific settings, and cleaning history all work smoothly on Indian networks.

**Pros:** DuoRoller eliminates hair tangling, 6,000 Pa handles Indian dust, excellent app, sonic mopping works well on tile
**Cons:** Requires manual dustbin emptying, mop pad needs manual washing after use, no auto-refill for water tank

### 2. Roborock S8+ — Best Value S8 Variant

**Price:** ₹59,999–₹64,999

The S8+ adds an auto-empty station that uses suction to transfer debris from the robot's dustbin into a sealed bag in the dock. The dock holds approximately 60 days of dust before the bag needs replacing. For Indian homes where daily cleaning means frequent debris, this reduces maintenance to a monthly task rather than after every run. The auto-empty process is noisy — about 70 dB for a few seconds — but it is brief and typically happens at the dock rather than in the middle of a room.

Everything else is identical to the base S8 — same DuoRoller, same 6,000 Pa suction, same sonic mop, same app. The mop pad still needs manual washing after use, which is the one maintenance step that remains. If you run daily mop cycles in an Indian kitchen, plan to rinse the mop pad every few days.

**Pros:** All S8 strengths plus 60-day auto-empty, significantly reduces daily maintenance burden
**Cons:** Auto-empty bags are an ongoing cost, mop still requires manual washing, no hot water mop wash

### 3. Roborock S8 Pro Ultra — Best Premium Robot Vacuum in India

**Price:** ₹74,999–₹84,999

The S8 Pro Ultra adds the self-wash station — the most significant upgrade in the S8 line. After a mopping cycle, the robot returns to dock, and the station automatically washes the mop pad and dries it with warm air. This means the mop stays hygienically clean without any user intervention. For Indian kitchens where mopping picks up oil residue, masala stains, and fine dirt, having a self-cleaning mop is a meaningful quality-of-life improvement.

The station also auto-refills the robot's water tank from a larger reservoir, meaning you can set a full clean-and-mop schedule and not think about it for days. Debris auto-empty is included. Combined, these three features — auto-empty, mop wash, water refill — make the S8 Pro Ultra genuinely hands-off in a way that cheaper models cannot match.

Suction, navigation, and mopping performance are identical to the base S8. The mop wash uses room temperature water (a contrast to Ecovacs T20 Omni's hot water wash at 55°C), which is a minor drawback for deep hygiene, but the convenience factor more than compensates for most buyers.

**Pros:** Fully automated maintenance, mop auto-wash and dry, water auto-refill, best-in-class app, excellent Indian tile and marble performance
**Cons:** Expensive, mop wash uses cold water, station is large and needs floor space near a power outlet

## Buying Guide / Comparison Analysis

**Performance on Indian Floors:** The DuoRoller is a game-changer for Indian homes. Marble and tile collect hair faster than carpet, and traditional bristle brushes tangle constantly. The S8's rubber rollers glide over smooth Indian floors without snagging. Suction at 6,000 Pa is more than adequate for the dust loads in most Indian cities, even during summer months when dust levels are highest.

**Mopping Quality:** Sonic mopping on the S8 series outperforms static mop pads significantly. In real-world use on Indian kitchen tile, it removes dried stains that single-pass mopping misses. The mop pad lifts automatically over fabric surfaces (durries, rugs) so you do not need to remove it before vacuuming carpeted areas.

**Pet Hair Pickup:** The DuoRoller makes the S8 one of the best robot vacuums for pet hair in India. No tangling, consistent pickup on tile, and strong suction for embedded hair near furniture legs and under beds.

**App Performance in India:** Roborock's app is the best in the robot vacuum category. Multi-floor maps, no-go zones, selective room cleaning, cleaning reports, and real-time robot tracking all function reliably on Indian mobile networks and Wi-Fi. No VPN workarounds needed.

**Noise Levels:** The S8 runs at approximately 65–67 dB on max suction — audible but not disruptive for most Indian households during daytime. At standard suction it is quieter. The auto-empty function is louder at about 70 dB but lasts only a few seconds per emptying cycle.

**Service Centres in India:** Roborock has official India service through authorised partners in major cities. Most user reports from Indian forums note fast turnaround times and good parts availability. The 1-year India warranty is processed locally without sending products overseas.
