## Buying Guide: Projectors in India 2026

**Lumens guide (how bright a room can be):**
- 50–200 ANSI Lumens: Completely dark room only
- 500–1000 ANSI Lumens: Dim room (lights off, windows covered)
- 1500–2500 ANSI Lumens: Moderate room light (evenings)
- 3000+ ANSI Lumens: Semi-lit room (daytime with curtains)

**DLP vs 3LCD:**
- DLP: Small, portable, high contrast — but some people see "rainbow effect" (colour flashes)
- 3LCD: No rainbow effect, accurate colours — typically larger projectors

**Throw ratio:** Most projectors need 2-3m distance to project a 100" screen. Ultra-short throw (UST) projectors work from 20-30cm away — ideal for small rooms.
