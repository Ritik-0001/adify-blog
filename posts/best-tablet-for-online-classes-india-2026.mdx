Online classes require a tablet that can run Zoom or Google Meet reliably for 6+ hours, has a display large enough for reading and annotation, and a front camera good enough for video calls. Here are the best options for Indian students in 2026.

---

## What Online Classes Need from a Tablet

**Minimum requirements for online classes:**
- **Display:** 10+ inches (smaller screens strain eyes during 2-hour sessions)
- **RAM:** Minimum 4GB (for stable Zoom + background apps)
- **Battery:** 6,000mAh+ (survives a full 8-hour school day)
- **Front camera:** Minimum 5MP for video call quality
- **Wi-Fi:** 802.11ac (Wi-Fi 5) or better for stable video calls
- **Storage:** 32GB minimum (install apps + save recordings)

## 4G LTE vs Wi-Fi for Online Classes

**When 4G LTE matters:**
- College hostel Wi-Fi is unreliable (very common in India)
- Home broadband is shared across multiple family members during class hours
- Travel to coaching classes requires internet on commute

**4G LTE variants are available** for most tablets above at ₹1,000–₹1,500 premium. For students in hostels or areas with unreliable broadband, the LTE version is strongly recommended.
