## What to Expect Under ₹1500

**No ANC at this price.** Active noise cancellation requires more expensive hardware. Under ₹1500, passive noise isolation (from ear tip seal) is the only noise reduction available. Look for earbuds with multiple ear tip sizes to achieve a proper seal.

**Battery reality check.** Advertised battery life is always tested at 50% volume. At typical Indian listening volumes (70–80%), expect 20–25% less than claimed. boAt's 42 hours realistically delivers 30–35 hours total use.

**Touch controls consistency.** Budget touch controls sometimes register accidental taps (from adjusting fit, rain, sweat). This triggers unintended skips or pauses. Look for earbuds where touch sensitivity can be adjusted in an app.
