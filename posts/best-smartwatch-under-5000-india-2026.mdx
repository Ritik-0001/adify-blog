## What to Look For Under ₹5,000

**AMOLED vs LCD display:** AMOLED has better contrast, true blacks, and is more readable in direct sunlight (India's outdoor conditions). LCD is acceptable; AMOLED is meaningfully better.

**Bluetooth calling:** Many Indian users want to take calls directly from the watch — useful for driving, exercise, and hands-free situations. Not all budget smartwatches include a speaker/microphone.

**SpO2 and heart rate:** Now standard even at entry prices — verify continuous monitoring vs on-demand (continuous is better for fitness tracking).

**Battery life:** 7 days minimum for practical use. Under ₹5,000, 10–14 day battery is achievable.

**IP rating:** IP68 minimum for India's monsoon season and gym sweat.
