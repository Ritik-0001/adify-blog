The Oral-B iO Series 3 and iO Series 4 are the two most commonly confused models in the iO lineup — they share the same core magnetic drive technology, the same app connectivity, and nearly identical cleaning performance. The differences are smaller than the ₹1,500 price gap suggests.

---

## 1. Oral-B iO Series 3 — The Budget iO Entry

**Price:** ₹4,499–₹4,999
**Why it's great:** The iO Series 3 delivers the full iO magnetic drive experience at the lowest iO price point. The magnetic drive motor produces smooth, quiet cleaning with combined oscillating and micro-vibration action — fundamentally different from the Pro 3 3000's buzzy oscillating motor. Five cleaning modes (Daily Clean, Sensitive, Gum Care, Whitening, Intense), LED pressure ring in three colors, AI brushing via Oral-B app, and 14-day battery. The Series 3 does not include a travel case — only a basic head cover — and is available in fewer color options than the Series 4. For home-based users who never travel with their toothbrush, the Series 3 is the rational iO purchase: same technology, lower price.

**Full iO Technology at Lowest Price:** Identical cleaning performance to the Series 4 — the price gap buys the travel case and more colors, not better cleaning.
**Best for:** Home users who don't travel with their toothbrush, buyers on a tight budget who want the iO experience, users prioritizing cleaning modes over accessories (5 modes vs Series 4's matching 5)

## 2. Oral-B iO Series 4 — The Travel-Ready iO

**Price:** ₹5,999–₹6,499
**Why it's great:** The iO Series 4 adds the single most practical upgrade over the Series 3: a travel case with integrated USB-C magnetic charging. The case holds the handle and one brush head and charges via any USB-C adapter — a laptop charger, phone adapter, or power bank works. This eliminates packing the bulky inductive charging stand for trips. The Series 4 also offers expanded color options (Rose Quartz, Quite White, and others not available on Series 3) and marginally refined motor calibration. Cleaning performance, app connectivity, AI brushing, and battery life are identical to the Series 3.

**USB-C Travel Case:** Charges the brush via any USB-C cable from any power source — the practical differentiator that justifies the ₹1,500 premium for anyone who travels more than 4 times a year with their toothbrush.
**Best for:** Frequent domestic or international travellers, buyers who want travel charging without packing the stand, those who want Rose Quartz or additional color options

## The Travel Case — Is It Worth ₹1,500?

The Series 4's travel case is the defining difference. Here's how to decide if it's worth ₹1,500 to you:

**Pay the extra ₹1,500 if:**
- You travel with a toothbrush more than 3–4 times per year (business travel, family visits, holidays)
- You travel internationally where bringing the charging stand is inconvenient (voltages, plug adapters, bulk)
- You prefer USB-C charging so you can charge from a single cable on a trip
- Your partner or travel companion has a different brand and you want your own standalone travel charging solution

**Save the ₹1,500 if:**
- You never or rarely travel with your toothbrush (charging at a destination or buying disposable travel brushes)
- You travel by car to destinations where bringing the stand is no inconvenience
- ₹1,500 meaningfully impacts your purchase decision

**Honest bottom line:** Most Indian buyers who travel for work or family more than monthly will use the travel case enough to justify it. Buyers who rarely travel or travel infrequently should save the money.

## Build Quality Differences — Are There Any?

Both Series 3 and Series 4 use the same handle materials and construction quality. The grip pattern is identical. The weight is the same (approximately 145g). The button layout is identical. The waterproofing rating is the same.

In teardowns and user testing, no meaningful internal hardware difference has been identified between the two beyond the travel case accessory. The "upgraded motor" description from Oral-B for the Series 4 is not reflected in any measurable cleaning difference in independent tests.

## Cleaning Mode Comparison

Both models offer exactly 5 modes in the same configuration:

There is no mode available on the Series 4 that is not on the Series 3, or vice versa. Mode performance is identical between models.

## Buying Guide: Series 3 vs Series 4

**Key consideration — honest travel assessment:**
Be honest about how often you actually travel with a toothbrush and pack a charger. Many buyers overestimate this. A 2-week summer holiday once a year doesn't justify ₹1,500 — a reusable ₹300 travel brush handles that trip. Monthly business travel does justify it.

**Key consideration — the replacement trap:**
If your current brush breaks and you're considering Series 3 vs 4, buy whichever is on sale. The two models go on sale at different times on Amazon India — sometimes Series 4 drops to Series 3 pricing. Set a price alert for both and buy whichever hits your target first.

**Key consideration — second brush in a household:**
For a household where two people share an Oral-B charging stand (common with iO stands that hold multiple heads), buying one Series 3 and one Series 4 helps visually distinguish whose head is whose through color — a practical household organization benefit.
