## Buying Guide: Kids Electric Toothbrushes India 2026

**Age recommendations:**
- Ages 2–3: Manual soft-bristle brush (electric too powerful)
- Ages 3–5: Oral-B Stages Power or Colgate Kids Sonic (low power, short timer)
- Ages 5–8: Oral-B Kids or Philips Sonicare For Kids (2-minute timer, pressure sensor)
- Ages 8–12: Can use same brush as adults (standard handles)

**Parental supervision:** Always supervise children under 8 using electric toothbrushes. Children under 6 should have parents apply toothpaste — a rice-grain amount for under 3, pea-size for 3–6.

**Brush head size:** Children's brush heads are smaller than adult heads — never use adult brush heads on children under 8. The smaller head fits developing mouths and reaches all surfaces.

**Fluoride toothpaste for India:** Use 500–1,000 ppm fluoride toothpaste for children 3–6, and 1,000–1,450 ppm for ages 6+. Available at Indian pharmacies. Fluoride remains the most evidence-based cavity prevention tool.

**Replacing brush heads:** Every 3 months or when bristles fray — same rule as adults. Children often fray bristles faster due to inconsistent pressure.
