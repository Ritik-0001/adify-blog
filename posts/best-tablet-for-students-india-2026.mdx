## Student Tablet Buying Guide

**What matters for students:**
1. **Battery life** — survives a full school/college day without charging (minimum 6,000mAh)
2. **Display size** — 10+ inches for comfortable reading; 8-inch is too small for textbooks
3. **Note-taking** — stylus support for PDF annotation (critical for medical/engineering students)
4. **Apps** — Google Classroom, Microsoft 365, Zoom, Google Meet all need to run smoothly
5. **LTE/SIM** — if college hostel Wi-Fi is unreliable, a SIM slot is essential

**Accessories that complete the student setup:**
- **Keyboard case:** ₹1,500–₹3,000 — transforms tablet into laptop for essay writing
- **Stylus:** Included with Tab S9 FE; ₹2,000–₹4,000 for others
- **Screen protector + case:** ₹500–₹1,000 — student tablets take drops
