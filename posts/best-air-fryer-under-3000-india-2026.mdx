## Budget Air Fryer Reality for India

**What you get under ₹3,000:** Mechanical timer (not digital), single temperature preset or basic dial, 2–3L capacity, and basic non-stick basket. Sufficient for samosa, pakora, french fries, and chicken pieces.

**What you miss under ₹3,000:** Digital display, preset programs, large 5–6L capacity, and dual-basket options. These matter for large families or frequent use.

**Verdict:** For 2–4 person households who want occasional air frying of Indian snacks — under ₹3,000 is perfectly adequate.
