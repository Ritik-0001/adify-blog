## Buying Guide: Dash Cams for India 2026

**Why a dash cam is essential in India:**
- Insurance companies increasingly accept dash cam footage as primary evidence
- Traffic police disputes resolved with clear video proof
- Hit-and-run incidents captured on parking mode
- Over-speeding challan disputes — GPS-tagged footage proves your actual speed

**Resolution guide:**
- 1080p FHD: Reads plates at up to 10m — adequate for most use
- 2.5K/2K: Plates readable up to 15m — recommended minimum for India
- 4K: Plates readable 20m+ — best for highway use

**MicroSD card:** Always use a Class 10 / U3 rated card (Samsung Endurance or SanDisk High Endurance) — standard cards fail quickly under continuous recording heat. Budget ₹500–₹800 extra for a reliable card.

**Hardwiring vs cigarette lighter:** Hardwired connections enable parking mode (continuous power when ignition is off). Cigarette lighter is plug-and-play but no parking protection.
