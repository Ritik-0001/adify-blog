## Apple AirPods Max — ₹54,990

**Best for Apple ecosystem users.** AirPods Max's deep iOS integration is unmatched — instant pairing with iPhone (just hold next to phone), automatic switching between all Apple devices, Siri hands-free, Personalised Spatial Audio (maps audio to your head shape for individualised 3D sound), and transparency mode that sounds more natural than any competitor. Lossless audio support (with Lightning cable).

The aluminium mesh headband and knit ear cushions are luxurious and long-wearing. 20-hour battery. For Indian iPhone users who want the definitive Apple audio experience, AirPods Max justifies its ₹54,990 price.

## AirPods Max Features iPhone Users Get That Android Users Don't

**Instant pairing.** Place AirPods Max near iPhone — done in 2 seconds without Bluetooth menu navigation. Pairs with all Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac) using the same Apple ID simultaneously.

**Personalised Spatial Audio.** The TrueDepth camera scans your ear shape to customise the 3D audio experience specifically for your head dimensions. Not available on any non-Apple device.

**Headphone Accommodations.** Accessibility feature in iOS amplifies certain frequencies based on your hearing profile — useful for Indian users with age-related high-frequency hearing reduction.
