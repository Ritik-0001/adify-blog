## What Is True Ceramic?

Most budget "ceramic" straighteners use aluminium plates with a thin ceramic coating — the coating wears off within 12–18 months, leaving metal plates that heat unevenly. True ceramic straighteners (CHI, GHD) use solid ceramic or thick-infused ceramic that maintains consistent performance for 3–5 years.
