## What Makes a Chair Good for Back Pain?

Before diving into specific models, it helps to understand what features actually relieve back pain. Adjustable lumbar support is the most important — it should press gently into the curve of your lower back when you are seated upright. Seat depth adjustment matters because a seat that is too deep forces you to slouch. Armrests at the right height keep your shoulders relaxed and reduce upper back strain. A chair that allows slight recline (around 100–110 degrees) is often better than sitting bolt upright.
