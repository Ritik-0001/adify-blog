## 4K TV Technology Guide

**OLED:** Each pixel generates its own light — true black, infinite contrast, perfect viewing angles, 1ms response time. Best picture quality available. Higher price.

**QLED (Samsung) / QNED (LG):** LCD with quantum dot enhancement — brighter than OLED (better in lit Indian rooms), good colour volume. Cannot match OLED contrast in dark rooms.

**Mini-LED:** Many small LEDs behind the panel for precise backlight control (local dimming) — significantly better than standard LED, approaching OLED in dark scene performance. Mid-range price.

**Standard LED:** Edge or direct-lit LCD — adequate for casual viewing, limited local dimming. Best value for budget buyers.
