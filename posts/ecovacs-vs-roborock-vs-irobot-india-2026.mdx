## Overview

The robot vacuum market in India has grown dramatically over the last two years, and three brands dominate the conversation: Ecovacs, Roborock, and iRobot. Each has carved a distinct identity — Ecovacs for mopping innovation, Roborock for all-round performance and app quality, and iRobot for smart obstacle avoidance. But which one actually makes sense for Indian homes in 2026?

Indian floors present a unique challenge. We predominantly live with marble, vitrified tile, and stone flooring rather than carpets. Dust accumulation is higher due to dry summers, open windows, and construction activity. Mopping is not optional — it is part of the daily cleaning routine. These factors change how you should evaluate a robot vacuum, and they are exactly what this comparison is built around.

All three brands are now officially available in India with authorised service networks, Amazon.in listings, and localised warranties. But their value propositions differ sharply once you factor in Indian pricing, floor types, and after-sales realities.

## Detailed Reviews

### 1. Ecovacs DEEBOT T20 Omni — Best Mopping Robot for India

**Price:** ₹44,999–₹54,999

Ecovacs makes one of the most compelling cases for Indian buyers specifically because of mopping. The DEEBOT T20 Omni uses OZMO Turbo 2.0 — a high-speed rotating mop that scrubs tile and marble rather than just wiping it. The All-in-One station automatically empties the dustbin, washes the mop pads with hot water (up to 55°C), dries them, and refills the water tank. For Indian kitchens where cooking oil and masala spills are daily events, hot water mop washing is a genuine hygiene advantage.

TrueMapping 2.0 LiDAR navigation creates accurate floor maps, and AIVI 3D obstacle avoidance handles cables and slippers reasonably well. Suction at 6,000 Pa handles Indian dust loads effectively on tile and stone. The Ecovacs app works well in India and supports multi-floor mapping.

**Pros:** Hot water mop washing, excellent tile cleaning, competitive pricing for feature set
**Cons:** Obstacle avoidance not as refined as iRobot, carpet performance limited for homes with rugs, app can be slower than Roborock's

### 2. Roborock S8 Pro Ultra — Best Premium Robot Vacuum in India

**Price:** ₹74,999–₹84,999

The Roborock S8 Pro Ultra is the most capable robot vacuum you can buy in India right now. The DuoRoller brush system uses two rubber rollers (no bristles) which dramatically reduces hair tangling — a real win for families with long hair or pets. Sonic mopping vibrates the mop at 3,000 times per minute, delivering noticeably cleaner tile and marble than a simple dragging mop. The self-wash station cleans and dries mop pads automatically.

ReactiveAI 2.0 obstacle avoidance is solid, though not quite at iRobot's level for identifying specific object types.

## Buying Guide / Comparison Analysis

**Price Range:** Roborock wins with the widest range — from ₹24,999 (Q5 Pro) to ₹84,999 (S8 Pro Ultra). Ecovacs is well-priced at the flagship level. iRobot offers fewer models and less value at each price point for Indian buyers.

**Cleaning on Indian Tile and Marble:** All three perform well, but Roborock's DuoRoller has an edge for homes with long hair. Ecovacs OZMO Turbo 2.0 provides the best actual scrubbing action for floor cleaning.

**Mopping Quality:** Ecovacs wins clearly. Hot water mop washing is a unique feature that matters for Indian hygiene standards. Roborock sonic mopping is excellent but uses cold water. iRobot's mopping is an afterthought at every price point.

**App Support in India:** Roborock app is the best — fast, feature-rich, and reliable on Indian networks. Ecovacs app is good and fully functional. iRobot app is solid but offers fewer granular controls and has a US-first design philosophy.

**Service Centres in India:** All three have official India presence. Roborock and Ecovacs have expanded their service networks significantly in 2025–26, with centres in metro cities and courier-based support for tier-2 cities.

**Indian Warranty Support:** All three offer a standard 1-year warranty with India-based claims. Roborock has been notably responsive based on user reports from Indian forums and communities.

## FAQ

**Which brand is best for Indian tile and marble floors?**
Roborock and Ecovacs are both excellent. For pure cleaning power, Roborock S8 Pro Ultra edges ahead. For mopping quality, Ecovacs T20 Omni is superior with its hot water mop washing system.

**Does the iRobot app work properly in India?**
Yes, the iRobot app works in India. However, it offers fewer customisation options than Roborock's app and is considered less intuitive by most Indian users who have compared both.

**Which brand has the best after-sales service in India?**
All three have official India service. Roborock has the most user-reported positive experiences for responsiveness and replacement parts availability in Indian metro cities.

**Is Ecovacs or Roborock better value in India?**
At the flagship level, Ecovacs T20 Omni at ₹44,999–54,999 offers exceptional value. Roborock Q5 Pro at ₹24,999 is unbeatable at the mid-range. Overall, Roborock has more options across price points, making it the more flexible brand for Indian buyers.

For most Indian buyers, **Roborock offers the best combination of performance, app quality, and value** — particularly the Q5 Pro for budget buyers and the S8 Pro Ultra for premium. **Ecovacs is the clear winner if mopping is your priority**, especially with the T20 Omni's hot water mop wash setting a hygiene standard no competitor matches at the price. **iRobot is the right pick only if obstacle avoidance is your non-negotiable** — think homes with curious pets, toddler toys scattered on floors, or charging cable clutter everywhere.
