## What the Smart 4000 Adds Over the Pro 3

### Bluetooth App Coaching
The Smart 4000 connects to the Oral-B app and provides real-time zone coverage tracking — showing which mouth areas have been brushed and how long was spent in each. This is the core value proposition over the Pro 3.

**For users who consistently miss the same areas:** If you regularly get cavities in the same location despite brushing twice daily, the zone coaching identifies the pattern within a week of use.

### App-Displayed Pressure Feedback
While the handle has an LED pressure indicator (same as Pro 3), the app displays a pressure history — showing how often you over-pressed during the session. The pattern analysis helps users correct chronic over-pressing habits.

The Smart 4000 is only preferable if you are committed to the classic oscillating feel and want app coaching without the iO premium.

## Should You Buy the Smart 4000?

**Buy it if:**
- You want app-coached zone coverage at under ₹5,000
- Classic oscillating feel is preferred over iO
- Brushing habit data tracking is specifically desired

**Skip it if:**

**The positioning problem:** The Smart 4000 is caught between the Pro 3 (₹1,500 cheaper, same cleaning) and iO Series 3 (same price, genuinely better technology). It's a logical choice primarily for users with a specific preference for classic oscillating + app coaching.
