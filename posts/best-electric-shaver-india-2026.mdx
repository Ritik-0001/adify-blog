## Rotary vs Foil Shavers — Which for Indian Men?

**Rotary shavers (Philips):** Circular rotating cutters under a perforated shield. Best for longer hair growth (2–3 days beard) and contoured faces. Circular motion adapts to jaw angles. Less irritating for sensitive skin.

**Foil shavers (Braun, Panasonic):** Back-and-forth cutting blades under a thin foil mesh. Best for daily shaving (shorter stubble), closer shave on flat areas (upper lip, cheeks), and more precise finishing.

**For Indian men specifically:** Most Indian men prefer alternate-day or every-2-day shaving. Philips rotary handles longer growth more comfortably. For daily shavers with sensitive skin — Braun foil series with skin-soothing technology.
