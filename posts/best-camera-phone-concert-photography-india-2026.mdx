## Concert Photography Tips for Smartphone Users

**Use burst mode.** Performers move constantly — burst mode (hold shutter button) captures 10–30 frames per second, giving you the sharpest frame from each motion. All phones above support high-speed burst.

**Manual shutter speed.** Pro modes allow setting shutter speed to 1/500s or faster — freezing performers mid-motion. Auto mode often chooses slower shutter speeds to compensate for low light, resulting in motion blur.

**Zoom range for Indian venues.** At typical Indian concert distances (10–30 metres), 3x optical zoom captures waist-up shots. 5x optical reaches face-level portraits. Beyond 5x, digital zoom degrades quality in low concert lighting.
