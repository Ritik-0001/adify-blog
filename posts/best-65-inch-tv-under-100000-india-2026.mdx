## Size and Viewing Distance

**65 inches** is appropriate for:
- Viewing distance: 2.5–4 metres
- Room size: 15x12 feet or larger
- Typical Indian living room: 3–3.5 metre viewing distance

**Sitting too close (under 2 metres) to a 65" 4K TV is actually fine** — at 4K resolution, pixel density is high enough that you don't see individual pixels even at close range.
