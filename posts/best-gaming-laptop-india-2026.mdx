## GPU Performance Guide for Indian Games

**RTX 3050/4050:** Entry gaming — Valorant 144fps+ (High settings), CS2 100fps+ (Medium), GTA V 60fps+ (High). Adequate for esports titles at competitive frame rates.

**RTX 4060:** Mid-range gaming — Elden Ring 60fps (Ultra), Cyberpunk 2077 60fps (Medium-High), all esports titles 200fps+. The sweet spot for 2026 gaming.

**RTX 4070/4080:** Premium gaming — 1080p/1440p max settings, AAA titles at High-Ultra. For serious PC gamers who want maximum fidelity.

**Thermal India note:** Gaming laptop GPUs throttle when hot. India's ambient temperatures (30–40°C) mean cooling is harder than in European climates. Look for laptops with dual-fan + vapour chamber cooling.
