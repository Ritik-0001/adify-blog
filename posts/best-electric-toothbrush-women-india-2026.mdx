## Women's Oral Health Context

**Hormonal gum sensitivity:** Oestrogen and progesterone fluctuations during menstruation, pregnancy, and menopause directly affect gum tissue. Pregnant women in particular are at significantly higher risk of gingivitis (pregnancy gingivitis) — gums become engorged and more sensitive. Sonic brushes with gentle modes and physical pressure sensors are particularly important during these periods.

**Whitening priority:** Surface stain removal from chai, coffee, and turmeric (common in Indian cuisine) is a high priority for many Indian women. Whitening mode electric brushes make a visible difference on these stains.

**Consistent routine:** Studies show women brush more consistently and for longer durations than men on average — making quality brushing features (multiple modes, intensity settings) more relevant since they will actually be used.
