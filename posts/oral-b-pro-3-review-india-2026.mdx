## Cleaning Performance — The Main Reason to Buy

The Pro 3's 3D cleaning action — oscillating (rotates clockwise + counter-clockwise) + pulsating (in/out motion) — removes plaque more effectively than any manual brushing technique.

**In everyday use:** Teeth feel noticeably cleaner after the first week. The smooth, polished feeling after brushing with the Pro 3 is distinctly different from manual brushing — a feeling dentists describe as "professionally clean."

**Plaque removal:** Oral-B's clinical studies show 100% more plaque removal vs manual brushing. Third-party studies consistently confirm oscillating-rotating brushes outperform manual.

**3 cleaning modes:**
- **Daily Clean:** Standard mode for everyday use — full oscillating + pulsating power
- **Sensitive:** Reduced power — noticeably gentler, good for morning brushing when gums are most tender
- **Gum Care:** Slower oscillations focused on the gumline — specialized for users with gingivitis or gum recession

The mode switching is genuine — Sensitive mode is meaningfully different from Daily Clean, not just a marketing label.

## Pressure Sensor — The Most Important Feature

The Pro 3's pressure sensor is the upgrade that matters most over cheaper Oral-B models (Pro 500, Vitality).

When you press too hard against teeth or gums, a red light on the handle illuminates immediately. The brush doesn't stop — it just warns you visually.

**Why this matters:** 60–70% of people brush too hard with manual brushes, causing slow gum recession over years. The Pro 3's pressure sensor creates an immediate feedback loop that corrects this habit within weeks. Users report their dentist noticing reduced gum bleeding at the next checkup.

---

## Timer — Exactly Right

2-minute timer with 30-second quadrant pulses (4 pulses total, one per quarter of the mouth). The timer is non-intrusive — the brush gives one brief pulse to signal a zone change, then continues brushing.

Most manual brushers clean for 45–60 seconds. The 2-minute timer consistently extends brushing duration, which directly improves plaque removal.

---

## Battery Life

The Pro 3 3000 provides approximately 14 days of battery on a full charge (2 brushing sessions per day, 2 minutes each). Charging takes approximately 12 hours on the inductive dock.

The dock requires a power outlet — it doesn't have a travel-friendly USB cable. The included travel case is plastic and functional but not premium.

**Complaint:** No battery level indicator. You'll discover it's dead when it stops mid-brush. Not a dealbreaker but a notable omission at ₹3,000.

---

## Design and Build

The handle is plastic with a rubberised grip section — comfortable during use, easy to hold when wet. The round brush head is designed to clean one tooth at a time, which takes 1–2 days to get used to if you're coming from a flat-head manual brush.

The charging dock is basic but functional. It sits on a bathroom shelf stably.

**No travel charging:** The charging dock is not travel-friendly. On multi-week trips, the 14-day battery is usually sufficient, but for longer trips you need to pack the dock.
