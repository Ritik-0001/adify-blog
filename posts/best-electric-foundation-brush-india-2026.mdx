## Foundation Application for Indian Skin

**Indian skin texture:** Indian skin frequently has enlarged pores, textured T-zone, and combination skin. Electric vibration presses foundation into pores rather than sitting on top — creating more even coverage for Indian skin texture.

**Indian foundation shades:** Indian skin tones require accurate undertone matching (yellow, golden, olive) — electric application with controlled vibration reduces blending variation that causes foundation mismatching at jaw and neck.

**Longevity in Indian climate:** India's heat (35–45°C summers) and humidity (60–90% monsoon) cause foundation breakdown. Electric brush application creates better product-to-skin adhesion — longer-lasting coverage in Indian climate vs sponge or manual brush.
