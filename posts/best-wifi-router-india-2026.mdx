---

## Router Considerations for India

**ISP compatibility:** Most Indian ISPs use GPON fiber terminating in an ONT (Optical Network Terminal) — your router connects via Ethernet to the ONT. Any router with a WAN port works. BSNL ADSL connections require a DSL modem-router.

**Thick concrete walls:** Indian construction uses reinforced concrete walls significantly thicker than Western drywall — WiFi signal attenuation is higher. Routers with stronger antennas (8dBi+) or mesh systems handle Indian construction better.

**High device density:** 8–12 devices in an average Indian household (phones, laptops, Smart TVs, streaming sticks, IoT devices). WiFi 6 (802.11ax) handles high device density significantly better than WiFi 5.
