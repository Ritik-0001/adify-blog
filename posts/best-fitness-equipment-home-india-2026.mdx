## Apartment vs House Considerations

**Apartment:** Exercise cycle or elliptical (no floor impact). Avoid treadmill if downstairs neighbours complain. Resistance training with dumbbells is always apartment-safe.

**Independent house/villa:** Full freedom — treadmill, barbell, power rack, all viable.
