## Square vs Round Basket: Why It Matters More Than Capacity Numbers

Most buyers compare air fryers on the litre number. But the basket shape matters as much as the volume.

**Round basket (common in ₹3,000–₹5,000 models):**
- Food piles in the centre more easily
- Harder to lay flat items (fish, flatbreads) without overlapping
- 4L round basket: roughly 22cm diameter, 200cm² surface area

**Square basket (Cosori Pro II and premium models):**
- Food distributes more evenly across the basket
- Flat items (fish fillets, flatbreads, sandwiches) fit without overlapping
- 5.8L square basket: roughly 22cm × 22cm, 484cm² surface area

In practice, the Cosori Pro II's square 5.8L basket fits more food than a 6L round basket — and the food cooks more evenly because there are no curved sides creating uneven airflow.
