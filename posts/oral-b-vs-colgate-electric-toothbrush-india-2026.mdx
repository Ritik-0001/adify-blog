Oral-B and Colgate are the two most recognised oral care brands in India — both sell electric toothbrushes, but at very different scales of commitment to the category. Here is a direct comparison of both brands' electric toothbrush ecosystems in India.

---

## Brand Overview

**Oral-B (Procter & Gamble):** The world's most clinically studied electric toothbrush brand. Oral-B invented the oscillating-rotating electric toothbrush category. In India, Oral-B sells the full range from entry (₹699 D12) to flagship (₹18,999 iO Series 9). Deep investment in clinical evidence — hundreds of peer-reviewed studies.

**Colgate (Colgate-Palmolive):** Primarily a toothpaste company that sells electric toothbrushes as a category extension. Limited model range in India — mostly the ProClinical series (sonic, battery-powered) and Slim Soft Electric. No premium rechargeable ecosystem comparable to Oral-B's iO platform.

---

## Technology Comparison

| Technology | Oral-B | Colgate |
|---|---|---|
| Oscillating-rotating | Yes (all models from D12 up) | No |
| Sonic vibration | Yes (limited models) | Yes (ProClinical range) |
| iO Magnetic Drive | Yes (Series 3–9) | No equivalent |
| AI coaching | Yes (iO Series 7+) | No |
| Pressure sensor | Yes (Pro 3 and above) | No (in India models) |
| App connectivity | Yes (iO Series 4+) | No |

**The technology gap is significant.** Oral-B's iO platform has no equivalent in Colgate's Indian lineup. Colgate's electric toothbrushes are essentially entry-level sonic brushes — functional but without the clinical investment, accessory ecosystem, or smart features of Oral-B.

## Brush Head Ecosystem

**Oral-B:** One of the most extensive brush head ranges globally. The Oral-B round head is universal across all Oral-B handles (D12 to iO Series 9). Available heads include: CrossAction, Sensitive, 3D White, Precision Clean, Gum & Enamel, iO Ultimate Clean, iO Gentle Care, iO Radiant White. Replacement heads widely available on Amazon India (₹200–₹1,000 per head or in multi-packs).

**Colgate:** Very limited replacement head availability in India. The ProClinical heads have limited ecosystem depth and are harder to source outside major cities.

---

## Clinical Evidence

**Oral-B:** Over 200 published clinical studies. The oscillating-rotating technology was the subject of the 2014 Cochrane systematic review that found it superior to manual brushing for plaque and gingivitis reduction.

**Colgate:** Colgate toothpaste has extensive clinical evidence, but Colgate electric toothbrushes specifically have limited independent clinical study coverage compared to Oral-B.

---

## Which Brand to Choose?

**Choose Oral-B if:**
- Budget is above ₹1,500
- You want the best-evidenced electric toothbrush technology
- Long-term use with brush head replacement is the plan
- App connectivity, AI coaching, or multiple modes matter

**Consider Colgate ProClinical if:**
- Budget is strictly under ₹900
- You want a brand-name sonic option at entry price
- You are already comfortable in the Colgate brand ecosystem

**The honest recommendation:** Oral-B dominates the electric toothbrush category in India above ₹1,000. Colgate electric toothbrushes are entry products for brand-loyalty buyers, not serious competition to Oral-B's technology or ecosystem above ₹1,500.
