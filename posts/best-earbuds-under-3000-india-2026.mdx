## Under ₹3000: What You Get That ₹1500 Doesn't Offer

**Meaningful ANC.** Sub-₹2000 ANC is barely noticeable. At ₹2500–3000, ANC becomes genuinely useful — cutting office AC noise, metro ambience, and traffic hum by a substantial amount. Nothing Ear (a) and realme Buds Air 5 Pro both deliver real-world ANC effectiveness.

**Better driver technology.** Dual-driver setups (separate drivers for bass and treble) appear in this range (Nothing Ear (a)). Single large drivers (12.4mm+) from OnePlus also outperform the 8–10mm drivers in budget earbuds.

**LDAC/LHDC support.** High-resolution audio codecs require earbuds in the ₹2500+ range. If you have a hi-res audio library or a Snapdragon-based Android phone, LDAC/LHDC delivers noticeably better audio quality versus SBC/AAC.
