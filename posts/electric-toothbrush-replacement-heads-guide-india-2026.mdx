## When to Replace Your Brush Head

### The 3-Month Rule

The ADA's three-month guideline exists because bristles begin to lose their effective cleaning shape well before they look visibly worn. Frayed or bent bristles cannot clean between teeth properly, and they may actually irritate gum tissue rather than massaging it.

### Visual Signs of Wear

- **Bristle splay:** The tips spread outward beyond the head's original diameter
- **Color fade:** Many heads include blue or pink indicator bristles that fade to white at the halfway point — a visual reminder built into the head itself
- **Reduced feel:** The brush no longer feels as "grippy" against tooth surfaces

### BrushSync Indicator (Philips Sonicare)

Philips Sonicare's BrushSync heads contain a microchip that counts brush sessions and signals the handle when replacement is due. The handle's LED changes color or flashes. This removes all guesswork — one of the most useful features for Indian households where tracking replacement schedules is easy to forget.

## Types of Replacement Heads

### CrossAction (Oral-B)

The CrossAction head has angled bristles arranged in a cross pattern designed to clean multiple tooth surfaces simultaneously. It is Oral-B's most popular head type and suitable for everyday cleaning for most adults. Compatible with all Oral-B oscillating handles (Vitality, Pro series, Smart series, and iO Series with adaptor).

### Sensitive (Oral-B / Sonicare)

Sensitive heads use extra-soft bristles at lower density, designed for users with gum recession, post-scaling sensitivity, or orthodontic work. Oral-B Sensitive heads apply less pressure per bristle; Philips Sonicare Sensitive heads reduce sonic amplitude by default when attached to compatible handles.

### Whitening (Oral-B / Sonicare)

Whitening heads use a combination of cup-shaped bristles and polishing zones to remove surface stains from tea, coffee, and spices — common in Indian diets. Not a substitute for professional whitening, but effective for extrinsic stain maintenance.

### Interdental (Oral-B)

Conical pointed bristles that reach between teeth, functioning similarly to a floss pick. Recommended as a supplement to standard CrossAction brushing, not a replacement. Particularly useful for users with wider interdental spaces.

### Ortho (Oral-B)

Designed for users wearing fixed braces. The head has a V-shaped cut-out in the centre to straddle the wire, with outer bristles cleaning brackets and teeth simultaneously. Available on Amazon India for users undergoing orthodontic treatment.

## Compatibility: Which Heads Fit Which Handles?

**Oral-B Universal Fit:** CrossAction, Sensitive, Whitening, Interdental, and Ortho heads fit all Oral-B oscillating handles — Vitality 100, Vitality Plus, Pro 1, Pro 2, Pro 3, Smart 4000, Smart 5000, and iO Series 3/4/5 (with included adaptor ring for iO). This universal compatibility across the entire Oral-B range is a significant advantage for Indian buyers.

**Oral-B iO Series (Series 6 and above):** Uses dedicated iO brush heads which do not fit older Oral-B handles. iO heads have a magnetic attachment mechanism and cost more (₹799 per head individually).

**Philips Sonicare:** Sonicare heads are compatible across the Sonicare range — 3100, 4100, 5100, 6100, 7100, DiamondClean 9000 — but NOT with Oral-B handles and NOT with Oclean or other brands. Sonicare uses a snap-on clip mechanism.

**Oclean:** Oclean replacement heads (P1S, P1C series) fit only Oclean handles. They are not compatible with Oral-B or Sonicare.

## FAQ

**Can I use Oral-B heads on a Philips Sonicare handle?**  
No. The two brands use entirely different attachment mechanisms and motor types. Oral-B heads are for oscillating motors; Sonicare heads are for sonic vibration motors. They are not interchangeable.

**Are third-party replacement heads safe?**  
They are not harmful, but quality varies significantly. Bristle hardness, tip rounding, and durability are often inferior to genuine heads. For daily oral care, genuine heads are worth the extra cost.

**Where can I buy Oral-B replacement heads in India offline?**  
Apollo Pharmacy, MedPlus, and large supermarkets like Reliance Smart and D-Mart stock Oral-B heads in 2-packs. Sonicare heads are harder to find offline.

**How many heads should I buy at once?**  
A 4-pack covers one person for a full year. For families sharing one handle, buy 4 heads at once (one per person) and replace all of them at the same time every three months.
