## CrossAction vs iO Magnetic Drive — Which Is Better?

This is the central question in the Oral-B lineup. Here is the honest answer:

**CrossAction technology** uses the classic Oral-B oscillating-rotating action — the brush head spins back and forth while also pulsating. Clinical studies show it removes up to 100% more plaque than a manual toothbrush. It has been the standard for over 30 years and is thoroughly proven.

**iO magnetic drive** is Oral-B's new motor technology. Instead of an oscillating mechanism, a magnetic ring drive delivers micro-vibrations through a redesigned brush head. The sensation is noticeably different — gentler, quieter, with less of the traditional "electric toothbrush buzz." The iO also uses a new round brush head with a softer, more flexible design. Clinical evidence shows iO removes up to 20% more plaque than CrossAction. The pressure sensor is also more sophisticated, with a colour-changing ring on the handle.

**Verdict:** For most practical purposes, a Pro 3 CrossAction brushes teeth exceptionally well. The iO difference is real but incremental — it justifies the price jump if you want the gentlest possible gum experience, a premium build, or wireless charging.
