## What to Check in a Top-Load Washing Machine

**Inverter motor:** Now standard at ₹15,000+. Quieter, more efficient, longer warranty (10 years). Never buy a non-inverter top-load in 2026.

**Auto Restart:** Essential for India. The machine should resume after a power cut automatically — not restart from zero. LG, Samsung, and Whirlpool all include this.

**Hard water compatibility:** Indian municipal water often has high TDS (300–600 ppm). Look for machines with hard water wash claims or use descaler sachets monthly.

**Spin speed:** 700–720 RPM is standard. 800 RPM+ clothes come out drier, reducing drying time — important during monsoon.
