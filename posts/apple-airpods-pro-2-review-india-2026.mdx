## The H2 Chip Advantage

The H2 chip processes 48,000 times per second — enabling ANC and Transparency mode computation far beyond what external chip-based competitors achieve.

**Practical result:** The ANC transition from isolating to transparent is seamless — no processing artifacts, no "switching" sensation. Just audio shifting between isolated and open. Competitors' transitions feel mechanical by comparison.

---

## Transparency Mode — Best Available

AirPods Pro 2's Transparency mode is the benchmark against which all other earbuds are measured. The H2 processes ambient sound so accurately that it sounds like the earbuds aren't there — a genuinely convincing pass-through that no other manufacturer has matched.

**India use:** Chai order at a stall, office conversation, metro announcement — Transparency mode handles all without removing earbuds. The quality of the pass-through is good enough that many AirPods Pro 2 users wear them in Transparency mode as default.

---

## Adaptive Audio — The Real Innovation

Adaptive Audio (iOS 17+) automatically blends ANC and Transparency based on surroundings:
- Walking in quiet street → shifts toward Transparency (hear surroundings safely)
- Mumbai Local train → shifts toward ANC (reduce noise automatically)
- Office meeting → shifts to conversational-aware Transparency

**India noise variability:** India's urban environments shift dramatically — quiet apartment corridor to loud market street in 30 seconds. Adaptive Audio handles this variability automatically without manual mode switching.

---

## Personalised Spatial Audio

The iPhone camera scans your ear geometry to create a personalised spatial audio HRTF (Head Related Transfer Function) — audio positioned in 3D space based on your unique ear shape. With head tracking, audio stays positioned in space as you move your head.

**Hindi content spatial audio:** Apple TV+ and Netflix India both support spatial audio on compatible content — the spatial experience on Indian content is identical to international content.
