## Colour Accuracy Guide for Designers

**Delta E:** Measures colour accuracy. Delta E &lt; 2 is professional standard. &lt; 1 is excellent. Most panels here are Delta E &lt; 2 out of the box.

**sRGB vs DCI-P3:** Web and UI design: 100% sRGB is sufficient. Video and app design: 95%+ DCI-P3 is better. Print design: Adobe RGB coverage matters most.

**Factory calibration:** Every professional monitor listed here ships calibrated. Don't trust uncalibrated monitors for colour-critical work.

**Resolution:** 4K at 27" provides ~163 PPI — every pixel is sharp, design work looks exactly as it will on retina screens. 1440p is adequate but shows pixelation on fine typography.

**Calibration hardware:** For the most accurate results, calibrate your monitor with a hardware colorimeter (X-Rite i1Display, ₹15,000–₹25,000) every 3–6 months.
