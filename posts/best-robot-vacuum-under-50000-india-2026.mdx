## Robot Vacuum for Indian Homes — What Matters

**LiDAR vs gyroscope:** LiDAR navigation creates a precise map and cleans in straight rows. Gyroscope navigation is random-pattern — takes longer to clean, may miss areas. Always choose LiDAR for Indian homes (₹20,000 and above).

**Suction on marble:** Indian marble, granite, and tile floors are smooth — 3000–4000 Pa is sufficient. Higher suction is more important for rugs.

**Mopping for Indian floors:** Indian households mop daily. A robot vacuum with sonic/vibrating mop handles daily maintenance. Still use a human mop for deep cleaning once a week.

**Obstacle avoidance:** Indian homes have sandals, bags, cables on floors. LiDAR + camera obstacle avoidance prevents the robot getting stuck.
