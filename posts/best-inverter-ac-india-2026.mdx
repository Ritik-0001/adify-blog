## Buying Guide: Inverter ACs in India 2026

**Capacity guide (room size → tonnage):**
- Up to 100 sq ft: 0.75 ton
- 100–130 sq ft: 1 ton
- 130–180 sq ft: 1.5 ton
- 180–240 sq ft: 2 ton

**Star ratings and electricity cost:**
- 3-star ISEER: ~₹8,000-9,000/year for 8 hours/day use
- 5-star ISEER: ~₹5,500-6,500/year — saves ₹2,500/year vs 3-star
- 5-star payback: ~2-3 years extra cost recovered in electricity savings

**Inverter vs Fixed-speed:** Always choose inverter in 2026 — fixed-speed is obsolete and uses 40-50% more electricity.

**Installation costs:** Average split AC installation in India: ₹2,000–₹3,500 for standard installation. Ask for it to be included when buying from dealer.
