## Straightener Safety Under ₹500

**Ceramic vs bare metal plates.** Budget straighteners with bare metal or low-quality plates cause more heat damage and uneven heating. All picks above use ceramic-coated plates — the minimum standard for safe straightening.

**Heat protectant is non-negotiable.** Any heat styling at 180°C+ damages hair cuticles without protection. Apply heat protectant spray before using any straightener — even under ₹500 options.

**Indian hair types.** Most Indian women have medium to coarse black hair. This hair type requires 180–210°C for effective straightening. Fine hair should use 150–170°C to avoid damage.
