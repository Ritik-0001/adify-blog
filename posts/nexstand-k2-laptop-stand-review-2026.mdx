## Ergonomics

Raising a laptop screen to eye level takes approximately 2–3 minutes to become habit. After that, the difference in neck and shoulder comfort during long work sessions is immediately apparent — head stays neutral rather than tilted 30–40 degrees downward.

The K2's highest position (approximately 27cm) suits most desk chairs and heights. The 6 locking positions let you fine-tune based on your chair height and preferred viewing angle.

Used with an external keyboard and mouse (required when the laptop is elevated), it creates a proper desktop ergonomics setup for under ₹6,000 total.

## Ventilation

Raised stands improve laptop ventilation significantly — the laptop's underside vents get airflow that's blocked when the laptop sits flat on a desk. In practice, CPU temperatures under load drop 5–8°C, which can reduce fan noise and thermal throttling on thin laptops.
