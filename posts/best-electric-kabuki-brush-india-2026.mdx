## Kabuki Brush for Indian Makeup

**Indian powder foundation:** Indian women frequently use compact powder or loose powder foundation for quick daily makeup — the kabuki brush's dense fibres pick up powder efficiently and apply without patchiness.

**Blush placement for Indian faces:** Indian cheek shapes require blush application from apple of cheek toward temple — kabuki circular motion distributes blush in this natural path automatically.

**Festival and wedding powder setting:** Indian festive makeup requires powder setting that lasts 8–12 hours in heat. Electric kabuki application presses setting powder into foundation more effectively than manual patting.
