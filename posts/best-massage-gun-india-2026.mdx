## What to Look For in a Massage Gun

**Percussion depth (amplitude):** 10mm is entry-level, 12–16mm is standard, 16mm is deep tissue. Higher amplitude reaches deeper muscle tissue for serious athletes.

**Stall force:** How hard you can press before the motor stalls. 20 lbs is entry, 40+ lbs for deep tissue muscle work. Low stall force guns feel weak under pressure — a critical frustration point.

**Noise level:** Key for home use — office environments, shared apartments, late-night recovery. Under 60 dB is quiet; under 45 dB is near-silent.

**Battery life:** Minimum 2–3 hours per charge for practical daily use. Look for USB-C charging (charge with phone charger) or standard USB.

**Attachment heads:** Ball (general), flat (large muscles), bullet (trigger points), fork (spine/neck), and wedge heads cover all use cases. Minimum 4 attachments recommended.
