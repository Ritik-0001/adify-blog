## Detailed Reviews

### Oral-B iO Series 7 — Full Breakdown

**Key Specs:**
- Motor: iO magnetic drive (oscillating-rotating + micro-vibrations)
- Cleaning modes: 5 — Daily Clean, Sensitive, Whitening, Gum Care, Intense Clean
- Pressure sensor: Colour LED ring (3 states: green, amber, red)
- AI brushing: Zone detection tracking 6 mouth zones via Oral-B app
- Handle display: Colour display showing mode, timer, battery, brushing coaching
- Battery: 14 days (twice-daily use)
- Charging: Magnetic USB charger (100–240V)
- Accessories: Travel case, Gum Care brush head included
- Warranty: 2 years (India)

**AI Zone Coaching — What It Actually Does:**
The headline feature of the Series 7 is position sensing that identifies which of the six zones of your mouth — upper left, upper right, upper front, lower left, lower right, lower front — the brush is currently cleaning. The Oral-B app displays a live mouth map: zones turn green when adequately cleaned, remain amber if you linger too long, and flag red if missed entirely.

In practice, this is genuinely useful. Most people habitually under-clean the same areas — typically the inner surfaces of lower front teeth and back molars on the non-dominant hand side. The AI coaching identifies these patterns and prompts you to correct them over time. After two weeks, the app builds a brushing score showing which zones improved. Accuracy is approximately 85–90%; the brush occasionally misidentifies inner versus outer surfaces on the same zone but gets the zone-level coaching right consistently.

**Colour Handle Display:**
Unlike the iO Series 4 and 6, the Series 7 shows real-time brushing information directly on the handle — no phone required. The display shows current cleaning mode, battery percentage, brushing timer counting up to 2 minutes, and pressure feedback. For users who brush without their phone nearby, this standalone feedback is a meaningful practical advantage.

**5 Cleaning Modes:**
Daily Clean, Sensitive, Whitening, Gum Care, and Intense Clean cover the full practical range. The Gum Care mode uses a motion pattern optimised for the gumline rather than tooth surfaces — clinically more effective for gingivitis management than simply using Sensitive mode as a substitute, which many users do on brushes without dedicated Gum Care.

**Pros:**

## FAQ

**Does the AI zone coaching on the iO Series 7 actually work?**
Yes, meaningfully. Zone-level coaching at approximately 85–90% accuracy helps users identify consistent blind spots in their brushing. The improvement in brushing coverage over 2–4 weeks of app-guided sessions is documented by many users.

**Does the Series 7 need to be connected to a phone to work?**
No. The brush operates fully standalone. The AI zone coaching requires Bluetooth connection to the Oral-B app, but all other functions — modes, timer, pressure ring, handle display — work independently.

**Are iO brush heads available in India for the Series 7?**
Yes. All iO-compatible heads (Ultimate Clean, Gentle Care, Sensitive Clean) are sold on Amazon India. The Gum Care head included in the Series 7 box is also available as a replacement.

**Is the iO Series 7 worth buying over a Philips Sonicare at the same price?**
The Philips Sonicare DiamondClean 9000 competes in this range. Oral-B's oscillating-rotating technology is more clinically studied for plaque removal; Sonicare's sonic technology is preferred by users with sensitive gums. The Series 7's AI zone coaching has no equivalent in Sonicare's India lineup at this price.

**What is the voltage compatibility for India?**
The USB magnetic charger supports 100–240V, fully compatible with Indian outlets. No adapter required.
