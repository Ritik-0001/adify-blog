**Smoky eye for India:** India's festive and bridal makeup tradition heavily features smoky eye looks — dark kajal, blended eyeshadow, and layered colour. Electric blending brushes achieve the seamless dark-to-light transition required for traditional Indian smoky eye faster than manual blending.

**Tutorial recreations:** Indian YouTube makeup tutorials have millions of followers — electric blending brushes help Indian viewers recreate professional results without professional technique.
