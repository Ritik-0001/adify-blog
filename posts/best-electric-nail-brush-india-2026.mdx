## Nail Care in India

**Cooking stains on Indian nails:** Indian cooking with turmeric, red chilli, and henna leaves stubborn nail staining. Electric nail cleaning brushes with rotating heads remove these stains faster than manual scrubbing.

**Acrylic nail growth:** Indian urban women increasingly use acrylic and gel nail extensions — regular electric nail file maintenance keeps extensions looking fresh between salon fills.

**Traditional mehndi/henna:** Indian henna application leaves residue under and around nails — electric nail cleaning brush removes mehndi staining efficiently post-occasion.
