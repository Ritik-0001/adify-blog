## What to Look For Under ₹5000

**Effective ANC vs gimmick ANC.** Many headphones under ₹5000 claim ANC but deliver minimal noise reduction (5–8dB). Sony WH-CH720N achieves genuine 20–30dB reduction. Always check real-world user reviews, not spec sheets, for ANC performance.

**Multipoint Bluetooth.** Sony WH-CH720N and Jabra Move Style support multipoint — connecting two devices simultaneously. Essential if you regularly switch between phone and laptop. JBL Tune 770NC lacks multipoint.

**Weight matters for comfort.** Over 250g becomes noticeable on long sessions. Sony WH-CH720N at 192g is exceptionally light. JBL Tune 770NC is 216g. Both are comfortable for 3+ hour sessions.

**App support.** Sony Headphones Connect app provides EQ, ANC adjustment, and auto-pause features. JBL Headphones app offers basic EQ. App support meaningfully improves the headphone experience for users who want to fine-tune sound.
