## Switch Types Guide for Indian Buyers

**Red switches (linear):** Smooth keystroke, no tactile bump, lightest actuation force (45g). Best for gaming — fast keystroke registration without resistance. Quiet. Popular choice: Cherry MX Red, Gateron Red.

**Brown switches (tactile):** Slight tactile bump at actuation point without audible click. Best for typing + gaming hybrid use — tactile feedback without the noise that disturbs shared office or home environments.

**Blue switches (clicky):** Audible click + tactile bump at actuation. Best typing feel — satisfying feedback for writers and coders. Loud — not suitable for open offices or shared living spaces common in Indian urban homes.

**Budget switch options in India:** Gateron switches (Chinese manufacture) cost less than Cherry MX but offer comparable quality. Outemu switches (budget) are functional but with less consistency. For most Indian buyers, Gateron is the sweet spot between price and quality.
