## Top Load vs Front Load Fully Automatic

**Top Load advantages:**
- Easier to load (no bending)
- Can add clothes mid-cycle
- Lower maintenance cost
- Better in areas with hard water (less scale buildup)
- Cheaper (₹13,000–₹25,000)

**Front Load advantages:**
- 40–50% less water use
- Better cleaning quality
- Gentler on fabrics
- Quieter operation
- Better energy efficiency

**Recommendation:** Top load for most Indian families in tier-2 and tier-3 cities. Front load for metro apartments with stable water supply.
