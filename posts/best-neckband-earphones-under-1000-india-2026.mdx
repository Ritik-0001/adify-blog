## What You Should Not Expect Under ₹1,000

Setting realistic expectations matters. Here is what you will not find in this price range:

- **No Active Noise Cancellation (ANC):** ANC requires dedicated hardware that adds cost. Under ₹1,000, passive noise isolation from the eartips is the best you will get.
- **No LDAC or aptX codec:** These high-resolution audio codecs are reserved for mid-range and premium earphones. You will get standard SBC here.
- **Limited water resistance:** Most models in this range have basic IPX4 splash resistance at best. Not suitable for heavy gym sessions or running in rain.
- **Microphone quality is basic:** Fine for calls but not for recording vocals or meetings.
