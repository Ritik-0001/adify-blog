## Buying Guide: LED Desk Lamps in 2026

**Why desk lighting matters for WFH:**
- Proper desk lighting reduces the contrast between your bright monitor and dark surroundings — the primary cause of digital eye strain
- Video call lighting: A lamp behind or to the side of your monitor illuminates your face — dramatically improves how you look on calls

**Colour temperature guide:**
- 2,700K–3,000K (warm white): Relaxing, evening use, reading
- 4,000K (neutral white): Balanced, all-day work
- 5,500K–6,500K (cool daylight): Most alert and focused — morning work

**Monitor light bars vs traditional lamps:**
- Monitor bars: No screen glare, elegant setup, illuminate desk directly
- Traditional lamps: More flexible placement, often brighter, better for paper/books

**What to look for:**
- Flicker-free LEDs (reduces headaches)
- Adjustable colour temperature (warm/cool for different times of day)
- USB charging port (convenient for phone charging)
- Stable base (lamps that tip over are a safety hazard)
