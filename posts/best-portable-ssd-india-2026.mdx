## What Makes a Portable SSD "Portable"

Standard external SSDs (like Samsung T7) are already compact. True portable SSDs go further — card-sized, ultra-lightweight (under 30g), and pocket-slippable without a case. The distinction matters for Indian commuters and travelers who need minimal carry.
