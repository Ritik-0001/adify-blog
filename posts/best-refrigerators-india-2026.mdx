## Buying Guide: Refrigerators in India 2026

**Capacity guide for Indian families:**
- 150–200L: 1–2 people (single door)
- 235–300L: 3–4 people (double door frost-free)
- 300–400L: 5–6 people (large double door)
- 500L+: 6+ people or side-by-side

**Single door vs double door:**
- Single door: ₹10,000–₹18,000, manual defrost required, freezer inside main compartment — fine for 1–2 people
- Double door frost-free: ₹18,000–₹45,000, no defrost needed, separate freezer — recommended for families

**Star ratings and electricity cost (annual estimate):**
- 2-star 250L: ~₹3,500/year electricity
- 3-star 250L: ~₹2,800/year electricity
- 5-star 200L: ~₹1,800/year electricity

**Inverter compressor:** Worth the ₹3,000–₹5,000 premium — variable speed compressor uses 30–40% less electricity and has 10-year warranty vs 1-year for fixed-speed.

**Frost-free vs direct cool:** Frost-free (double door) costs ₹5,000–₹8,000 more but eliminates manual defrosting every 2 weeks — strongly recommended for Indian households.
