**Best for:** Budget-conscious OLED buyers who want Samsung ecosystem.

## Who Should Buy a 55" OLED in India?

**Ideal room setup:** Viewing distance 2–3.5 metres. Works in 10x12 to 14x14 feet living rooms.

**Time of day viewing:** OLED's biggest advantage is evening/night viewing when room is dark. In full daylight with strong window light, a 65-inch QLED is often brighter and more visible.

**Gaming with PS5/Xbox:** The LG C4 is the clear winner — 144Hz VRR, HDMI 2.1, G-Sync Ultimate certification.
