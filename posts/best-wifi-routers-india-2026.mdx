## Buying Guide: WiFi Routers in India 2026

**WiFi standards explained:**
- WiFi 5 (802.11ac): Still adequate for most homes on plans under 200Mbps
- WiFi 6 (802.11ax): Faster, handles more devices simultaneously — recommended for new purchases
- WiFi 6E: Adds 6GHz band — future-proof for mesh systems

**Do you need a mesh system?**
Yes if: Your home is >2,500 sq ft, multi-floor, or has thick walls causing dead zones.
No if: A single apartment or you can run an Ethernet cable to a second access point.

**Speed tip:** For plans above 300Mbps, ensure your router has Gigabit (1,000Mbps) LAN/WAN ports — Fast Ethernet ports (100Mbps) will bottleneck your connection.
