ASUS and HP are the two most popular laptop brands in India after Lenovo. Both offer products from ₹30,000 to ₹1,50,000. The right choice depends on your priorities — here's the honest breakdown.

---

## ASUS vs HP — Brand Overview

**ASUS** is a Taiwanese manufacturer known for innovation, value at mid-high budgets, and pioneering features like OLED displays at low prices. Their ZenBook, VivoBook, and ROG lines cover every segment.

**HP** is an American manufacturer with one of the strongest service networks in India. Known for reliable business machines, good consumer laptops, and strong brand recognition among enterprises and government buyers.

## ASUS Strengths

- **Display technology:** ASUS leads with OLED panels at lowest prices in India
- **Innovation:** ROG gaming line is class-leading; ZenBook ultrabooks are premium
- **Value:** Better specs per rupee at ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 range
- **Build:** ZenBook and ROG lines have excellent aluminium builds

## HP Strengths

- **After-sales service:** HP has service centres in 900+ cities across India — the most extensive network
- **Business segment:** HP EliteBook and ProBook are enterprise-trusted
- **Reliability reputation:** HP laptops have strong long-term reliability track record
- **Government/PSU procurement:** HP is widely preferred in government contracts

## Bottom Line

ASUS wins on display quality and innovation at mid-to-high budgets. HP wins on after-sales service network and enterprise reliability. For most Indian buyers between ₹35,000–₹60,000, choose **HP if you're in a city with limited ASUS service** or **ASUS if display quality matters**.
