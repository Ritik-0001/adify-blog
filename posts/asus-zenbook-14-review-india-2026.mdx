## Display — The Main Selling Point

The 2.8K OLED display is exceptional for the price. 2880x1800 at 14" = 230 PPI, 100% DCI-P3, infinite contrast ratio, and VESA DisplayHDR True Black 600 certification. The 90Hz refresh rate makes scrolling noticeably smoother than 60Hz panels.

For Indian buyers: This OLED display quality was only available on ₹1,50,000+ laptops two years ago. ASUS has democratised OLED significantly.

**Note:** OLED displays have a potential burn-in risk with static content over years — less of a concern for general users, more relevant if you leave static UI elements on screen for hours daily.

---

## Performance

Ryzen 7 7745HX is an H-class processor — designed for sustained performance. Handles professional workloads: large Excel files, multi-layered Photoshop documents, video streaming + office apps simultaneously. Not a gaming chip (no discrete GPU), but for productivity it's class-leading at this price.

**Thermal management:** The ZenBook 14 runs warm under sustained load in the thin chassis — fans spin up noticeably during heavy tasks. Consider this if you work in a quiet environment.

---

## Battery Life

ASUS rates the ZenBook 14 at 15hr. Real-world Indian testing: **9–11hr** on mixed use (web, office, occasional video). Better than most 14" Windows laptops at this price. The 65W USB-C charger is compact enough to carry without extra weight.

---

## Build Quality

Ceraluminum (ceramic + aluminium alloy) chassis is visually premium and resistant to fingerprints. 1.39kg is light for a 14" laptop with this level of performance. Hinge feels solid — no wobble at any angle. The keyboard backlighting is even and the key travel is comfortable for extended typing.
