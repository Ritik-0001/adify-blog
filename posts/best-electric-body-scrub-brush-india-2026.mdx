## Why Electric Body Scrubbing for India

**Indian sun tanning:** Indian skin is highly susceptible to UV tanning — tan accumulates quickly from outdoor commuting, construction work, sports, and daily sun exposure. Regular mechanical exfoliation accelerates tan fade by removing dead tanned skin cells faster.

**Hard water mineral deposits:** Indian municipal water has high calcium and magnesium content — minerals deposit on skin after bathing, dulling skin tone. Electric scrubbing with a body wash removes these deposits that manual rubbing misses.

**Monsoon humidity + blocked pores:** India's monsoon season (June–September) increases sweat and sebum production — body pores block easily. Regular back and shoulder exfoliation prevents body acne.
