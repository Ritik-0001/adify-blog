OTG (Oven Toaster Griller) ovens are India's most popular home baking appliance — providing baking, grilling, and toasting without a full-size built-in oven. Priced from ₹1,500 to ₹6,000, OTGs are accessible for Indian home bakers making cakes, cookies, kebabs, and grilled dishes. Here are the best OTGs for Indian kitchens in 2026.

---

## OTG vs Microwave Convection for India

**OTG advantages:**
- Lower price (₹1,500–₹6,000 vs ₹14,000–₹20,000 for convection microwave)
- More precise baking — dedicated heating rods + rotisserie give authentic baked/grilled results
- Better for grilling and roasting (tandoori-style chicken, kebabs)

**Microwave convection advantages:**
- Also reheats and defrosts
- Faster cooking
- More versatile overall

**OTG is best for:** Dedicated bakers and grillers who want one appliance optimised for heat cooking at affordable price.
