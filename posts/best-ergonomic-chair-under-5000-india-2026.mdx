## What to Look For in an Ergonomic Chair Under ₹5,000

At this budget, you're making trade-offs. Here's what matters most:

**Lumbar support is non-negotiable.** Even a basic fixed lumbar curve is far better than a flat backrest. Avoid chairs that have no lumbar feature at all.

**Mesh beats fabric for Indian weather.** If you live in a region with warm, humid summers — which covers most of India — mesh backrests dramatically improve comfort during long sessions.

**Check weight capacity.** Budget chairs sometimes have low weight ratings. Make sure the chair you choose supports your body weight with at least 10–15 kg margin.

**Castors matter on Indian floors.** Hard-floor castors work better on marble and tile (common in Indian homes) and prevent floor scratches. Most budget chairs ship with universal castors.
