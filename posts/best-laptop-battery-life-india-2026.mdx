## Why Battery Life Claims Are Misleading in India

Manufacturers test under ideal conditions (low brightness, minimal apps, Wi-Fi off). Indian real-world use involves:
- High screen brightness (bright rooms, sunlight)
- Multiple apps simultaneously
- Constant Wi-Fi and Bluetooth
- Indian summer temperatures (25–40°C) reducing battery efficiency

**Rule of thumb:** Take the manufacturer's claimed hours and subtract 30–40% for Indian real-world conditions.

---

## Gaming Laptops and Battery Life

Gaming laptops (Lenovo Legion, ASUS ROG, HP OMEN) deliver 2–4hr on battery under gaming loads. Even for office work unplugged, gaming laptops average only 4–6hr. **Avoid gaming laptops if battery life is a priority.**
