## Buying Guide: Budget Smartwatches Under ₹3,000

**AMOLED vs LCD at this price:**
- AMOLED: Deep blacks, vibrant colours, better in dark rooms — now available from ₹1,500+
- LCD: Brighter in sunlight (some models), longer battery, looks less premium
At under ₹3,000, AMOLED is worth choosing for the visual experience.

**BLE calling reality:** BLE calling routes phone calls through your phone's Bluetooth — you speak into the watch microphone. Call quality is acceptable for quiet environments but poor in noisy streets. Think of it as a convenience feature, not a replacement for phone calls.

**Built-in GPS vs connected GPS:**
- Connected GPS: Uses phone's GPS while paired — watch GPS routes require carrying phone
- Built-in GPS: Amazfit and Realme models track independently — useful for runs without phone

**Battery life at this price:** Expect 7–10 days with health sensors active and 5–7 days with BLE calling enabled. Always-On Display cuts battery to 3–4 days.
