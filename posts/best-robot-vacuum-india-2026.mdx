## Why Robot Vacuums Work Well in India

**Hard floor dominance:** Most Indian homes have marble, granite, or ceramic tile floors — robot vacuums perform best on hard surfaces and work more consistently than on carpets. India's floor preferences are ideal for robot vacuum effectiveness.

**Dust levels:** India's outdoor dust pollution means indoor dust accumulation is significantly higher than in Western countries. Daily automated cleaning prevents the buildup that requires intensive manual cleaning sessions.

**Multi-zone homes:** Open-plan living areas and multiple rooms on a single floor are common in Indian apartments — LiDAR mapping allows efficient systematic coverage.

**Mop combo value:** Indian homes require both dry dust removal and wet mopping (for tile/marble). Vacuum-mop combo robots eliminate the need for two separate cleaning steps.
