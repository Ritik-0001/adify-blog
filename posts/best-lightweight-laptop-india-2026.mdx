## What to Check Before Buying

**Actual vs rated weight:** Manufacturers list "base weight" — the charger adds 200–400g. MacBook Air charger is 50W compact; some Windows chargers are large bricks.

**Battery life claims:** Halve the manufacturer's claim for real-world usage. LG Gram's 22hr rated = ~12–14hr real-world.

**Build quality:** Lightweight laptops in plastic feel flimsy. Pay for magnesium alloy or aluminium builds — they survive daily bag throws better.
