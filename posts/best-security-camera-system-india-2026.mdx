## How to Choose a CCTV System in India

A complete CCTV system requires: cameras, a DVR (digital video recorder) or NVR (network video recorder), a hard drive for storage, cables, power supplies, and a monitor or TV for local viewing. Wired systems use coaxial cable (for AHD/HDCVI/AHD cameras) or network cable (for IP cameras). The key decisions are resolution (2MP vs 4MP vs 8MP), storage capacity, and whether you want remote smartphone access. For most Indian homes and shops, a 4-channel system with 2MP–4MP cameras and 1–2TB storage is the right starting point.
