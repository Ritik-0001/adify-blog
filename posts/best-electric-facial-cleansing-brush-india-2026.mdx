## Why Electric Cleansing for India

**Pollution in Indian cities:** Delhi, Mumbai, and Bengaluru have AQI levels that deposit PM2.5 particles in pores. Standard manual cleansing removes surface-level particles; sonic cleansing vibration (300+ oscillations/second) dislodges embedded particles.

**Hard water residue:** Indian municipal water has high mineral content (calcium, magnesium) that leaves film on skin after washing. Sonic cleansing provides mechanical action that reduces mineral-deposit buildup.

**Sunscreen residue:** Indian climate requires SPF 30–50 daily application — water-based cleansers alone cannot fully remove modern chemical sunscreen formulas. Mechanical brush assistance significantly improves sunscreen removal.
