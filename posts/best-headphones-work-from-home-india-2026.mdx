## WFH Headphone Buying Guide

**ANC vs No ANC:** In a home with family, street noise, or shared spaces — ANC is not optional. It's a productivity tool. Prioritise it.

**Over-ear vs In-ear:** Over-ear (full cushions) is more comfortable for all-day wear. In-ear (earbuds) is better for calls-only users who don't want headphones on constantly.

**Call quality:** Built-in earcup mics (Sony, Bose) are good for most calls. Boom mic headsets (Jabra) are better for high-frequency calling roles.

**Battery:** 20+ hours is the minimum. You shouldn't be charging mid-workday.
