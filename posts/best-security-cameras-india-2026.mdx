## Buying Guide: Security Cameras for India

**Cloud vs local storage:**
- Cloud: Access footage from anywhere, footage safe if camera is stolen. Recurring cost.
- Local (SD card/NVR): No subscription, more private. Footage lost if camera stolen.
- Best approach: Cloud for critical cameras (entrance), local for indoor/general monitoring.

**Resolution guide:**
- 1080p (2MP): Adequate for general monitoring
- 2K (4MP): Identifies faces and licence plates clearly
- 4K (8MP): Zoomed-in detail recognition — for high-security areas

**Indian considerations:**
- Get IP65/IP67 rated cameras for outdoor use — India's monsoon is harsh on electronics
- Look for Hindi/regional language app support from Indian brands (Qubo, CP Plus)
- Solar-powered outdoor cameras are now viable in India — check Mi and Tapo solar versions
