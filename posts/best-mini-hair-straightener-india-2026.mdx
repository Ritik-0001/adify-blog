## Travel-Specific Considerations for India

**Voltage compatibility.** India uses 220V. Most hotel rooms in India, UK, Europe, and Middle East provide 220V — standard straighteners work fine. For travel to the USA (110V) or Japan (100V), only use straighteners with worldwide voltage input (100–240V) or you'll damage the heating element.

**Compact vs performance trade-off.** Mini straighteners sacrifice plate width (20–25mm vs 35mm in standard models) and maximum temperature range. For full styling sessions on long Indian hair, a full-size straightener is more efficient. Mini straighteners are best for touch-ups and fringe care rather than complete styles.
