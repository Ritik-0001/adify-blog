**Temperature control.** Basic temperature settings (low/medium/high or specific °C) become available, letting you match heat to your hair type. Under ₹500, most straighteners have single fixed temperature.

**Build durability.** ₹700–1000 straighteners use better hinge mechanisms and cord materials that last 2–3 years of daily use. Under ₹500 options typically last 1–1.5 years with daily use.
