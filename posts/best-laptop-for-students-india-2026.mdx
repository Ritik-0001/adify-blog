The right student laptop in India depends heavily on course type — an engineering student needs different specifications than an MBA student or a design student. Here are the best laptops by student profile in India 2026.

---

## By Course Type

**Engineering (CS, IT, ECE):** Programming, code compilation, virtual machines — 16GB RAM, fast SSD, and a capable processor are minimum requirements. Linux compatibility is a bonus.

**MBA/Commerce:** Excel, PowerPoint, video conferencing, case study research — 8GB RAM is sufficient, battery life and portability are higher priorities.

**Arts/Design:** Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro — colour-accurate display (sRGB 100%), dedicated GPU for rendering, 16GB+ RAM.

**Online classes/general:** Web browsing, video streaming, document editing — any modern ₹25,000–₹30,000 laptop with SSD suffices.
