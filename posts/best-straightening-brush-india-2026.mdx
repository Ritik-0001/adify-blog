## Straightening Brush vs Flat Iron

**Straightening brush advantages:**
- Faster — brushes through naturally rather than sectioning and clamping
- More natural result — slight movement rather than board-straight
- Less technique required — point and brush vs managing hot plates
- Lower damage risk — bristles space heat across hair rather than concentrated plate pressure

**Flat iron advantages:**
- More intense straightening for very resistant curly hair
- Defined creases and edges for sleek hairstyles
- Better for chemically treated Indian hair requiring precise heat control

**Best for:** Indian women with wavy to lightly curly hair who want everyday smooth results quickly. Women with very curly or coily Indian hair may need flat iron for complete straightening.
