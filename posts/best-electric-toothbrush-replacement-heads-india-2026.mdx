## When to Replace Brush Heads

**Replace every 3 months — or sooner if bristles splay.** This applies to all brands. Indicators that a head needs replacement:

- Bristles are visibly bent outward or flattened
- Blue indicator bristles (Oral-B CrossAction and some Philips heads) have faded to white
- Brushing feels less effective — you're working harder for the same result
- You've had a cold or illness — replace after recovery to avoid reinfection

**India-specific note:** Humidity in Indian bathrooms accelerates bristle degradation. In monsoon months or high-humidity cities (Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata), inspect heads monthly.

- Local pharmacies — Oral-B CrossAction widely available; Philips Sonicare heads rarer

**Counterfeit warning:** Generic "compatible" replacement heads are widely sold on Amazon by third-party sellers. These are not recommended:
- Bristle quality is uncontrolled — may be too hard and damage enamel
- No blue indicator bristles (no wear indicator)
- Motor stress from improperly balanced heads can damage your brush handle
- Some heads have reported allergen issues (nickel in metals)

Always buy Oral-B or Philips branded heads from authorised sellers (Fulfilled by Amazon or sold by brand directly).
