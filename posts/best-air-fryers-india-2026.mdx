## Buying Guide: Air Fryers in India 2026

**Capacity guide:**
- 2–3L: 1–2 people (small snacks, single servings)
- 4–5L: 3–4 people (standard family use)
- 6L+: 5+ people or batch cooking

**Temperature range matters:** Look for 80°C–200°C range — lower temperatures are needed for dehydrating and gentle reheating; 200°C is needed for crispy results.

**For Indian cooking specifically:**
- Round basket: Traditional shape, heats evenly
- Square basket: Fits more flat items (roti, bread slices, larger pieces)
- Mesh basket: Better for smaller items (peanuts, chana, popcorn)

**Oil usage:** Most air fryer recipes use 1–2 tsp oil vs 500ml+ for deep frying — healthy cooking with 90-95% oil reduction.
