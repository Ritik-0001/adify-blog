## Buying Guide: Induction Cooktops for India 2026

**Gas vs induction for Indian cooking:**
- Induction advantages: 50% faster boiling, 90% energy efficiency, safer (no open flame), precise temperature control
- Induction limitations: Requires induction-compatible cookware (magnetic base — check with a magnet)

**Cookware compatibility:** Only flat-bottomed magnetic cookware works — stainless steel, cast iron, and most modern non-stick. Old aluminium, copper, and clay cookware won't work.

**Wattage guide:**
- 1600W: Basic cooking, slow to boil for chai and large pots
- 1800–2100W: Standard — good for all Indian cooking
- 2200W+: Fast boiling — reduces cooking time significantly

**Voltage fluctuation:** Indian voltage fluctuations can damage induction cooktops — choose models with voltage protection (auto-shutoff below 180V / above 280V).
