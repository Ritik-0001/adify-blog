## Deep Dive: iO Magnetic Drive Technology

The iO Series 4 uses the same magnetic drive as the Series 3 and 6. This is worth understanding because it represents a genuine technology shift from all previous Oral-B models:

**Standard Oral-B (Pro 2, Pro 3, Pro 5000):** A small electric motor physically drives the brush head back and forth — oscillating-rotating action at up to 8,800 rotations per minute.

**iO Series (3, 4, 6, 7, 9):** A magnetic linear mechanism creates combined oscillating + micro-vibration movement. The result is smoother brush head movement, lower noise, and a fundamentally different cleaning sensation.

Clinical data: Oral-B's iO studies across 10,000 participants showed 6x more plaque removal than a manual toothbrush and measurable gum health improvement in 4 weeks. The magnetic drive specifically allows for more controlled pressure — which is why the LED pressure ring is more nuanced on iO brushes than on the Pro series.

## 5 Cleaning Modes — Full Breakdown

**Daily Clean:** 2-minute timer with 30-second quadrant alerts. The default for everyday use — what Indian dentists recommend as the standard routine.

**Sensitive:** Reduced motor intensity for patients with exposed root surfaces, post-scaling sensitivity, or orthodontic hardware. Useful for the first few weeks after dental work.

**Gum Care:** Extended gentle strokes focused on the gumline — most relevant for Indian adults given the high prevalence of early-stage gingivitis (data from Indian dental surveys shows 67–80% of Indian adults have some gum inflammation).

**Whitening:** Higher frequency motion for surface stain polishing. Particularly useful for chai drinkers — India's chai culture means tannin staining is a common cosmetic concern.

**Intense:** Maximum cleaning intensity for heavy plaque or post-holiday brushing recovery. Most users switch to Intense once a week.

## AI Brushing Feedback — How It Works on Series 4

Connect the iO Series 4 to the Oral-B app (free, iOS/Android) via Bluetooth Low Energy. The app:

- Maps your mouth into 16 zones
- Tracks dwell time in each zone per brushing session
- Highlights chronically missed zones (most people under-brush upper back molars)
- Records pressure incidents — how many times the LED turned red
- Shows weekly and monthly trends

**India-specific note:** The app works fully in offline mode — it syncs brushing data after reconnecting to Wi-Fi. Does not require constant internet connectivity during brushing, which matters for users in areas with inconsistent connectivity.

## LED Pressure Ring — Three-State Feedback

The iO Series 4 pressure ring has three states:

- **White (gentle):** Light pressure — acceptable contact
- **Green (ideal):** Correct pressure for optimal cleaning
- **Red (too much):** Reduce pressure immediately

The three-state system is more informative than the simple red/no-red indicator on the Pro 3 3000. Indian dentists report that patients with the iO's green-ring guidance reduce aggressive brushing significantly faster than those with binary indicators.
