## Why At-Home Pedicure for India

**Salon hygiene concerns:** Shared pedicure tubs and tools in Indian salons carry fungal infection risk — nail fungal infections are India's most common nail complaint. At-home electric pedicure tools eliminate shared-instrument hygiene risk.

**Cost savings:** Monthly salon pedicure = ₹3,600–₹7,200 per year. Quality electric pedicure set = ₹2,000–₹5,000 one-time. Break-even in 3–6 months.

**Monsoon foot care:** Indian monsoon season requires more frequent foot cleaning and nail care — moisture-trapped nails require regular cleaning to prevent fungal growth. Daily at-home nail cleaning is easier than monthly salon visits.
