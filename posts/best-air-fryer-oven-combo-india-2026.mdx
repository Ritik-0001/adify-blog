## Air Fryer Basket vs Air Fryer Oven: What Is the Difference?

Before deciding which to buy, understand the fundamental format difference:

**Basket Air Fryer** (like Havells Prolife Digi, Cosori Pro II):
- Pull-out drawer with a basket inside
- Single cooking layer
- Food cooks in a pile or single layer on the basket
- Easier to clean (basket goes in dishwasher)
- Compact footprint
- Best for: Quick snacks, fries, wings, small portions

**Air Fryer Oven** (like Agaro Regal 12L, Instant Vortex):
- Front-opening door like a traditional oven
- Multiple rack positions
- Can cook two different dishes simultaneously on separate racks
- Includes accessories: wire racks, baking trays, rotisserie spit, crumb tray
- Larger footprint
- Best for: Family meals, whole chicken, baking, dehydrating, multi-dish cooking

The air fryer oven is essentially a compact OTG (Oven Toaster Griller) with a powerful convection fan that circulates hot air rapidly — giving you OTG baking and roasting capabilities plus air fryer crisping speed.

## Who Should Choose an Air Fryer Oven Over a Basket Air Fryer?

**Choose an air fryer oven if:**
- You want to cook whole chicken, full fish, or large cuts of meat
- You bake occasionally (cakes, bread, cookies)
- You want to cook two different dishes simultaneously
- You want rotisserie capability for chicken or kebabs
- You want to dehydrate fruits or vegetables
- You are replacing or supplementing an OTG

**Stick to a basket air fryer if:**
- You primarily cook quick snacks and small portions
- Counter space is limited
- You prioritise easy cleanup (single basket is faster to clean than oven + racks + trays)
- You want Wi-Fi app control (basket models like Cosori Pro II have better app integration)
