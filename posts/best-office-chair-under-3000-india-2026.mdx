## What to Expect Under ₹3,000

Let's be honest about what a sub-₹3,000 office chair can and cannot do. At this budget, you should expect: basic height adjustment, a mesh or fabric back, a swivelling base, and usable armrests. You should not expect: long-term durability beyond 1–2 years of daily heavy use, premium ergonomic features, or adjustable lumbar support.

That said, for students, secondary workstations, light daily use (3–4 hours), or as a temporary solution, a well-chosen chair under ₹3,000 is perfectly serviceable. These are the best options available.

## Should You Buy a Chair Under ₹3,000?

A chair under ₹3,000 makes sense in these situations:
- You are a student who needs a study chair for 2–3 hours daily
- You need a temporary chair while saving for a better one
- You have a second workstation or a spare room setup
- Your use is very light (occasional emails, short tasks)

It does **not** make sense for:
- Full-time WFH use of 6–8 hours daily — invest in at least a ₹6,000–₹10,000 chair
- Anyone with existing back pain — a cheap chair will make it worse
- Long-term use — budget chairs rarely last more than 1–2 years under heavy use
