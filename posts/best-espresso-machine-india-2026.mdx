## Espresso Machine Types

**Manual/Semi-automatic:** You control extraction — grind, tamp, pull shot. Most control over espresso quality. Requires skill and practice. Best for coffee enthusiasts.

**Fully automatic:** Press a button — machine grinds, tamps, brews, cleans. Bean-to-cup convenience. Less control, consistent output. Best for daily users who want café quality without technique.

**Pod/Capsule:** Pre-portioned pods (Nespresso, Dolce Gusto). Zero skill required. Freshness compromise vs whole beans. Best for absolute convenience.

**Moka pot:** Stovetop espresso (not true espresso — approximately 1–2 bar, not 9). Produces strong concentrated coffee closer to espresso than drip. Cheapest entry.
