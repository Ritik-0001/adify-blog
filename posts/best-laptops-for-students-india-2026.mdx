## Buying Guide for Students

**How much RAM do you need?**
- 8GB: Fine for Office, browsing, video calls. Gets tight with 10+ Chrome tabs + apps simultaneously.
- 16GB: Recommended for CS/engineering students running IDEs, Docker, and multiple apps.

**SSD vs HDD:** Always choose SSD — the speed difference in boot time and app launch is transformative. All recommendations above use SSDs.

**Battery life priority:** Campus students need 8+ hours. The MacBook Air M3 is exceptional; the VivoBook 15 is good; the HP Pavilion is adequate.

**Warranty tip:** Always buy from Amazon (official seller) for clean warranty. Register the laptop with the brand website within 7 days of purchase.
