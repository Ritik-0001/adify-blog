## Buying Guide: Under ₹10,000 Tablets

**Realistic use-case checklist:**
- Online classes (Zoom/Meet/Classroom): All options above handle this
- YouTube/Netflix at 720p: All options above handle this
- HD video editing: None — upgrade budget to ₹15,000+
- Gaming (BGMI/Call of Duty): None at acceptable quality
- Photography: None — use a smartphone
- Heavy multitasking: None — single-app use recommended

**4G LTE vs Wi-Fi:** At this price range, LTE is worth the extra ₹500–₹1,000 if connectivity is uncertain. Many users in this segment rely on mobile data rather than broadband.

**Storage:** 16–32GB base storage fills quickly. Always use a microSD card (₹500–₹800 for 64GB) to expand storage for videos and offline content.
