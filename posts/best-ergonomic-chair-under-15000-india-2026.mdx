## Is ₹15,000 the Right Budget for an Ergonomic Chair?

The ₹10,000–₹15,000 range is arguably the sweet spot for Indian buyers who work from home. Here's why:

**You get real adjustability.** Lumbar height, depth, and armrest adjustments matter significantly for your posture. Below ₹8,000, these features are often absent or rudimentary.

**Better mesh longevity.** Chairs in this range use denser mesh that holds its tension for 3–5 years of daily use rather than sagging within 18 months.

**Tilt mechanisms improve.** Synchronized tilt with tension adjustment based on your body weight reduces fatigue during long sessions in ways that single-pivot reclines cannot.

**Still affordable.** You're not committing to ₹50,000 for a Herman Miller. A well-chosen ₹12,000–₹15,000 chair from a quality brand will serve you well for 5+ years.
