## Android TV vs Google TV vs Tizen vs webOS

**Android TV / Google TV (Sony, TCL, Hisense, OnePlus):** Full Google Play Store with all apps. Google Assistant. Chromecast built-in. Best app ecosystem.

**Tizen (Samsung):** Faster, more stable OS. Samsung-curated app store. No Google Play Store — apps from Samsung's store only.

**webOS (LG):** Fastest interface. LG Content Store. No Google Play Store. Better for streaming-focused users who don't need many apps.

**For Indian buyers:** Android TV provides the most comprehensive app ecosystem including regional Indian apps (JioTV, Zee5, SonyLIV, MX Player) that may not appear on Samsung or LG stores.
