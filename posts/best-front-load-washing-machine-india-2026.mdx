## Front Load Maintenance for India

**Drum cleaning:** Run a drum clean cycle monthly to prevent mold in the humid drum interior — critical in monsoon months.

**Detergent:** Use only HE (High Efficiency) detergent in front-load machines. Regular top-load detergent creates excess suds that damage the bearing.

**Hard water:** Use a water softener tablet if your TDS is above 500 ppm. IFB's Aqua Energie handles this internally.

**Door seal:** Clean the rubber door gasket weekly — mold accumulates there in high-humidity Indian environments.
