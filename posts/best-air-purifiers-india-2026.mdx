## Buying Guide: Air Purifiers for India in 2026

**What matters most:**
1. **True HEPA filter** — captures 99.97% of particles ≥0.3 microns (PM2.5, dust mites, pollen, bacteria)
2. **CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate)** — higher = cleans room faster. Target CADR ≥ room area (sq ft)/1.5
3. **Activated carbon** — essential for India: removes cooking smells, VOCs, NO₂, and SO₂
4. **Auto mode** — sensor-based auto adjustment so you don't have to manually change settings

**Room size guide:**
| Room Size | Required CADR | Recommended |
|---|---|---|
| Up to 150 sq ft (bedroom) | 100–150 | Philips AC1715 |
| 150–300 sq ft (large bedroom) | 150–200 | BlueAir 411i, Honeywell V2 |
| 300–500 sq ft (living room) | 250–400 | Xiaomi Purifier 4 |
| 500+ sq ft (open plan) | 400+ | Dyson TP07 |

**Indian-specific tip:** Replace filters every 6 months in high-pollution cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru) — the filter turns grey visibly fast.
