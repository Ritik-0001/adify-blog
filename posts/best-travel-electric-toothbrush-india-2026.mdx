## Travel Electric Toothbrush Buying Guide

**Voltage compatibility:** All electric toothbrushes listed above support 100–240V (universal voltage). You need only a plug adapter (no bulky voltage converter) for international travel. Verify voltage marking on the charger before travelling.

**International plug adapters for Indian travellers:**
- **Type A (USA, Japan):** Most common adapter need
- **Type G (UK, Singapore, Malaysia):** Available in India
- **Type C (Europe):** Required across Europe
- Universal travel adapters (₹500–₹1,200) cover all types in one

**Carry-on vs checked:** Electric toothbrushes are allowed in carry-on baggage on all airlines. Lithium batteries in the brush are well within airline limits (under 100Wh).

**Brush head travel hygiene:** Cover brush heads during travel — toothbrush caps or small zip-lock bags. Bristles collect bacteria in toiletry bags. The iO Series 4 travel case fully encloses the head.
