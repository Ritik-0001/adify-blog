Microwave ovens in India serve three primary use cases: reheating leftovers (most common), cooking dal/rice/vegetables, and baking/grilling (convection models). Choosing the right type — solo, grill, or convection — determines whether the microwave gets used daily or sits unused.

---

## Types of Microwave Ovens for India

**Solo microwave:** Heats only — reheating, defrosting, and basic cooking. Cannot grill or bake. Best for families who only want reheating convenience.

**Grill microwave:** Microwave + grill element (no baking). Good for tandoori-style grilling, chicken tikka, and browning without full oven investment.

**Convection microwave:** Microwave + grill + convection fan (can bake, roast, and grill). Replaces a separate oven. Best value for households who want full baking capability.
