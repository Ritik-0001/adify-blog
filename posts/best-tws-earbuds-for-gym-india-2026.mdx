## What Gym Earbuds Need

**Secure fit — ear hooks or sport tips:** Standard TWS earbuds often fall out during running and heavy lifting. Ear wings, ear hooks, or sport-specific ear tip shapes are essential for gym stability.

**IPX5 minimum sweat resistance:** IPX4 handles light sweat; IPX5 handles heavy gym sweating and running rain. IPX7 is optimal for Indian outdoor running in monsoon season.

**Bass emphasis:** Gym motivation is strongly correlated with bass-heavy audio — Bollywood workout playlists, hip-hop, EDM. Sport earbuds typically tune for elevated bass response.

**Low latency:** For gym workout videos and fitness apps — audio sync to video content matters during exercise form instruction.
