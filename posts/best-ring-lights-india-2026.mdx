## Buying Guide: Ring Lights in India 2026

**Size guide:**
- 10": Selfies, close-up calls — limited coverage, good for phones
- 14–16": Portrait photography, solo YouTube — good coverage
- 18–21": Full-body shots, dual presenters, professional setups — best coverage

**Colour temperature for Indian skin tones:**
Indian skin tones render best at 4500K–5000K (neutral white). Avoid going too warm (3200K — orange cast) or too cool (6500K — blue tint). Bi-colour ring lights let you fine-tune this.

**CRI (Colour Rendering Index):** Above Ra 90 for accurate skin tones. Consumer ring lights are typically Ra 85–90. Professional lights (Godox LR150) offer Ra 95+.

**Ring light for glasses:** Ring lights create a circular reflection in glasses — unavoidable. If you wear glasses on camera, use a panel light or angle the ring light off-axis.
