## Reading on Tablets — Eye Comfort Tips

**Reduce eye fatigue:**
1. Enable **Night Mode / Blue Light Filter** (all Android tablets have this — reduces blue light emission at night)
2. Use **dark mode** in reading apps — white text on black is easier on eyes in dim lighting
3. Set **auto-brightness** — tablet should match ambient light level
4. Follow 20-20-20 rule — every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds
5. Reading apps like **Kindle**, **Moon+ Reader**, and **ReadEra** allow custom fonts, line spacing, and margin size — increase these for comfort
