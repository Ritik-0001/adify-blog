## Buying Guide: Budget 5G Phones in 2026

**5G in India — is it worth it?**
Yes — 5G coverage in Indian cities has expanded significantly in 2025–26. Jio, Airtel, and BSNL 5G cover most Tier 1 and many Tier 2 cities. A 5G phone future-proofs your purchase for the next 3–4 years even if 5G isn't yet available in your area.

**Chipset guide for this segment:**
- Dimensity 6080 / Snapdragon 695: Entry-level 5G — smooth daily use, light gaming
- Snapdragon 7s Gen 2 / Dimensity 7200: Mid-tier — smooth heavy gaming and multitasking
- Snapdragon 8 Gen 2: Flagship (₹35,000+ phones only)

**Display: AMOLED vs LCD:**
Under ₹20,000, AMOLED is a genuine advantage — better contrast, outdoor visibility, and battery savings in dark mode. Prioritise AMOLED over LCD if given the choice.

**Camera: what to look for:**
- OIS (Optical Image Stabilisation): Makes a real difference in photos and video stability — rare under ₹20,000
- Main camera megapixels: 50MP is plenty; 108MP = larger file sizes, not necessarily better photos
