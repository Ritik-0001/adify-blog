## Vlogging Phone Checklist

**Front camera autofocus.** Fixed-focus front cameras produce soft faces during movement. Autofocus (AF) front camera is non-negotiable for vlogging — confirm it before purchasing.

**Stabilisation while walking.** OIS + EIS together (not EIS alone) produces smooth walking footage. Test: record yourself walking quickly and watch the result — good stabilisation looks like a gimbal, poor EIS looks like jello.

**Wind noise rejection.** Indian outdoor environments (traffic, markets, festivals) produce high wind noise. iPhone 15 and Samsung S24 both offer wind noise processing. Budget phones often don't — test outdoors before committing.
