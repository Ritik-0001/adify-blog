## What "360 Degree" Actually Means

True 360° cameras use a motorised base that rotates the camera horizontally (pan) and a tilt motor that adjusts the vertical angle. This is different from fisheye cameras that use a single wide-angle lens — motorised pan/tilt cameras give you a sharper image because you are using the full sensor resolution on a normal lens, not a distorted fisheye view. Most cameras labelled "360°" in India offer 355–360° horizontal pan and 90–114° vertical tilt, which covers an entire room from floor to ceiling.
