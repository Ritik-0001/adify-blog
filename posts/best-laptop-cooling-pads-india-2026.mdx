## Buying Guide: Laptop Cooling Pads in 2026

**Do cooling pads actually work?**
Yes — measured independently, a quality cooling pad drops CPU temperatures by 8–15°C on gaming laptops. This translates directly to:
- No thermal throttling = sustained performance during gaming and rendering
- Longer component lifespan
- Less loud laptop fans (laptop fan doesn't spin as hard)

**Fan size vs number:**
- 1× 200mm fan: Quieter, moves equivalent air to 3× 100mm fans
- Multiple small fans (80–120mm): Higher RPM = louder
- Rule of thumb: Fewer larger fans > more smaller fans

**Ergonomics bonus:**
Cooling pads with height adjustment (6 angles) also improve typing posture — reduces neck and wrist strain. This is as valuable as the cooling for WFH users.

**What won't work:**
Cooling pads don't help MacBook Air M-series, which throttles based on sustained load (not temperature). They're mainly beneficial for Intel-based gaming laptops and AMD Ryzen gaming laptops.
