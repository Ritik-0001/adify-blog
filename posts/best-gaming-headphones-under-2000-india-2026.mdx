## What Gaming Headphones Need Under ₹2000

**Boom mic is non-negotiable.** In-line mics on regular headphones are inadequate for gaming communication — they pick up too much keyboard noise and ambient sound. A directional boom mic, positioned close to the mouth, delivers significantly cleaner voice to teammates.

**50mm drivers for bass impact.** Gaming headphones use larger 50mm drivers (vs 40mm in regular headphones) to enhance the low-frequency rumble of explosions, engines, and bass-heavy game soundtracks. Under ₹2000, 50mm drivers are standard.

**Virtual surround vs stereo.** Virtual 7.1 surround sound (available via PC software with USB connection) provides directional audio cues. In competitive FPS games (BGMI, Valorant, Free Fire), hearing enemy footsteps from distinct directions is a genuine gameplay advantage over stereo audio.
