## Back Scrubbing for Indian Skin

**Back acne in India:** Sweat, heat (Indian summers reaching 40–45°C), and fabric contact from synthetic clothing create ideal conditions for back acne. Regular back scrubbing with an antibacterial body wash prevents clogging.

**Back tan:** Indian outdoor workers, cyclists, and sports enthusiasts accumulate back tan that is difficult to fade without exfoliation. Regular back scrubbing with a loofah brush accelerates tan removal.

**Shower flexibility:** Without a long-handle brush, back washing requires a spouse, family member, or complex loofah contortion. Electric long-handle scrubbers provide full back access solo.
