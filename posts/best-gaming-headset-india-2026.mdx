## What Indian Gamers Need in a Headset

**Surround sound for competitive play:** 7.1 virtual surround (software or hardware) provides directional audio cues — hearing footsteps from specific directions in BGMI, Valorant, and CS2 is a competitive advantage. Critical for Indian esports players.

**Mic quality for squad communication:** India's team-based gaming culture (BGMI squads, Valorant teams) requires clear mic pickup. Noise rejection from background noise (family environments, traffic) is relevant in Indian home gaming setups.

**Comfort for long sessions:** Indian gaming sessions average 3–5 hours — headset weight, ear cushion material (protein leather vs mesh), and clamp force directly affect session duration comfort.

**Platform compatibility:** India's gaming market is predominantly mobile (BGMI) + PC (Valorant, CS2, GTA). 3.5mm jack compatibility for mobile is required alongside USB for PC audio.
