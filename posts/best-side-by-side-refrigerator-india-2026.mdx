## Is a Side-by-Side Right for Indian Homes?

**Space requirements:** Side-by-side refrigerators are 90–100cm wide — wider than standard double-door models (65–70cm). Ensure your kitchen has sufficient depth and door swing clearance. Many Indian kitchens, especially older layouts, may not accommodate side-by-side fridges without renovation.

**Cost of running:** Larger capacity + extra features = higher electricity consumption. A side-by-side typically uses 400–600 units/year vs 250–350 for a standard double-door. At ₹8/unit, add ₹1,200–₹2,000/year to running costs.

**Water dispenser:** Available in some premium Samsung and LG side-by-sides but requires a plumbing connection. Check if your kitchen has a water supply line access behind the refrigerator position.
