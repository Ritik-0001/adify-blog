## Buying Guide: What to Look for Under ₹7,000

**Modes:** Minimum 3 modes (Clean, Whitening, Sensitive). Gum Care mode is worth having if you've had any gum issues.

**Battery:** 14 days minimum. 30 days (Oclean) is ideal if you travel frequently — India's frequent power fluctuations and travel between cities makes long battery a practical advantage.

**Replacement heads:** Check Amazon availability and price before buying. Oral-B and Philips replacement heads are widely stocked at ₹300–₹400 each. Oclean and Colgate heads are available but less common in offline pharmacies.

**Pressure sensor type:** Warning light (Oral-B iO) vs physical pause (Sonicare 5100) — the physical pause is more effective at breaking bad habits but both work if you pay attention.
