## What Makes a Good Gaming Tablet

**Essential gaming tablet features:**
- **High refresh rate display (90Hz minimum):** Higher refresh rates reduce input lag and make motion smoother — critical for BGMI and Call of Duty Mobile competitive play
- **Powerful processor:** Snapdragon 700+ series or Dimensity 8000+ for sustained 60fps gaming
- **Cooling:** Good thermal management prevents FPS throttling during 2-hour sessions
- **Battery:** 8,000mAh+ for 4–5 hours of gaming per charge
- **Large display:** 11+ inches for competitive games (more field of view, easier target acquisition)
