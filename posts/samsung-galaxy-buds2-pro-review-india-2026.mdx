The Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro are designed specifically for Samsung Galaxy phone owners — the 360 Audio, Auto Switch, and Galaxy Wearable app integration create a deeply connected Samsung ecosystem experience. At ₹14,999 for Samsung users or ₹12,999 on sale, here is the complete review.

## Samsung Galaxy Buds2 Pro — Key Specifications

**Driver:** 10mm woofer + 5.5mm tweeter (dual driver)
**ANC:** Active noise cancellation with Voice Detect
**Battery:** 5hr (buds) + 18hr (case) with ANC on
**IP rating:** IPX7 (water resistant — not dustproof)
**Codec:** Samsung SSC HiFi (up to 24-bit/96kHz with Galaxy phones), AAC, SBC
**Price India:** ₹12,999–₹14,999

---

## Samsung Ecosystem Integration — The Core Advantage

**360 Audio:** Dolby Atmos spatial audio that tracks head movement using the Buds2 Pro's gyroscope — audio field stays fixed in space as you turn your head, creating theatre-like immersion for compatible Samsung content.

**Auto Switch:** Seamlessly switches between Galaxy phone, Galaxy Tab, Galaxy Book laptop — without manually reconnecting. For Samsung multi-device users, this is the best multi-device switching implementation.

**Galaxy Wearable app:** Equaliser presets, touch control customisation, ANC level adjustment, fit test, and find my buds — all in Samsung's ecosystem app.

**360 Audio for Indian content:** Currently 360 Audio works best with Samsung Galaxy content and Dolby Atmos streams on Netflix/Prime — most Indian OTT content (Hotstar, Zee5, SonyLIV) is standard stereo. The spatial audio advantage is mostly for English language content consumption.

---

## ANC Quality Assessment

Samsung's ANC on the Buds2 Pro is second-tier to Sony WF-1000XM5 and Bose QC II but notably better than most earbuds at this price:

- **Low-frequency noise (AC units, traffic):** Effective — the dual-driver system handles rumble well
- **Mid-frequency noise (conversation):** Partial reduction — voices bleed through at about 60% reduction
- **High-frequency noise (typing, kitchen sounds):** Moderate reduction

**Voice Detect:** Automatically switches to Ambient mode when you start speaking — conversation mode without touching the buds. Useful for Indian commuters who frequently interact with auto drivers, shopkeepers, and colleagues.

---

## Call Quality

**3 microphones + Voice Pickup Unit:** Galaxy Buds2 Pro captures voice from bone conduction (jaw vibration), directional microphone, and ambient mic — filtering out traffic noise, wind, and background conversation.

**Wind noise:** The beamforming array handles Indian street conditions (auto traffic, construction, marketplace) better than most earbuds. Callers report clear voice even in noisy outdoor environments.

**Call codec:** Samsung VoIP over Samsung phones supports high-resolution call audio — significantly clearer than standard calls.

---

## Audio Quality for Music

The 10mm + 5.5mm dual-driver configuration delivers:

- **Bass:** Elevated but controlled — suitable for bass-heavy Bollywood and hip-hop
- **Mids:** Clear vocal presence — good for Indian classical and vocal-heavy content
- **Highs:** Detailed without harshness — instrumental separation is strong

**SSC HiFi codec with Galaxy phones:** Samsung's proprietary codec transmits up to 24-bit/96kHz audio — audibly better than AAC or SBC on Galaxy devices. For non-Samsung phones, the Buds2 Pro falls to AAC and loses this advantage.

## Comfort and Fit for India

**Silicone ear tips:** 3 sizes included — medium fits most Indian ear canal sizes. The ergonomic wing design holds the bud without in-ear pressure.

**IPX7 water resistance:** Survives sweat, rain, and splash but not full submersion. Sufficient for monsoon commuting and gym use.

**Wearing detection:** Auto-pause when removed, auto-resume when replaced — standard premium feature.
