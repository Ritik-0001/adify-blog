## What to Look For Under ₹3000

**ANC quality varies significantly.** Budget ANC (JBL Tune 670NC, Noise Evolve 2 Air) effectively reduces consistent noise like AC hum, train noise, and office ambiance. It is less effective against sudden or variable sounds. For flight travel and full noise isolation, you'll need to budget ₹8000+ for Sony WH-1000XM5-class ANC.

**Multipoint Bluetooth.** Sony WH-CH520 supports multipoint Bluetooth (connecting two devices simultaneously). JBL Tune 670NC does not. If you switch frequently between phone and laptop, prioritise this feature.

**USB-C charging.** JBL Tune 670NC and Sony WH-CH520 both use USB-C. boAt Rockerz 550 uses micro-USB. USB-C is preferable for the universal charging convenience.

**Over-ear vs on-ear.** On-ear headphones (JBL Tune 670NC) are lighter and more portable. Over-ear cups (Sony WH-CH520) provide better passive noise isolation and more comfort during extended sessions.
