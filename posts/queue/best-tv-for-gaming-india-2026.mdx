## Gaming TV Features Explained

**HDMI 2.1:** Required for 4K/120fps from PS5 and Xbox Series X. HDMI 2.0 limits to 4K/60fps. All TVs above ₹30000 now include at least one HDMI 2.1 port.

**VRR (Variable Refresh Rate):** Syncs TV refresh rate with GPU output to eliminate screen tearing and stuttering. Supported by PS5 (LG OLED C3, Samsung Q80C) and Xbox Series X.

**Response time:** OLED (0.1ms) is fastest. QLED (1–4ms) is excellent. Standard LED (8–16ms) creates ghosting in fast movement visible in competitive gaming.
