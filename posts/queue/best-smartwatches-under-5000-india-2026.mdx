## Buying Guide: Budget Smartwatches in 2026

**What ₹5,000 gets you in 2026:**
- AMOLED display with always-on option
- Built-in GPS (on some models)
- BLE Bluetooth calling
- Heart rate, SpO2, stress, sleep monitoring
- 7–14 day battery life
- IP67/IP68 swim-proof rating

**What you won't get under ₹5,000:**
- ECG/AFib detection (requires ₹15,000+)
- Wear OS or watchOS (requires ₹20,000+)
- Third-party apps (budget watches run proprietary OS)
- LTE/cellular connectivity

**GPS note:** Built-in GPS on budget watches is accurate enough for distance tracking but not precise enough for navigation. For serious runners, Amazfit Bip 5 has the best GPS in this segment.

**Calling quality:** BLE calling on budget watches routes calls through your phone — it's convenient for quick calls but not as clear as a phone held to your ear.
