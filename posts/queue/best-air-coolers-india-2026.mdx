## Buying Guide: Air Coolers for India 2026

**Cooler vs AC for India:**
- Air cooler: ₹8,000–₹18,000 upfront, ₹100–₹200/month electricity, works best in dry heat (North, West, Central India)
- AC: ₹35,000–₹60,000 upfront, ₹1,500–₹3,000/month electricity, works everywhere including humid areas

**Which type to choose:**
| Type | Tank Size | Coverage | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Personal | 12–20L | Up to 15 sqm | Bedroom, single room |
| Tower | 25–40L | Up to 25 sqm | Living room, urban apartment |
| Desert | 50–80L | 25–50 sqm | Large rooms, halls, North India |

**Dry vs humid climates:**
Air coolers work by evaporating water — in humid air (above 60% humidity), evaporation slows and cooling drops significantly. For Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata: choose inverter coolers with humidity mode. For Delhi, Rajasthan, UP, MP: standard coolers work excellently.

**Honeycomb vs wood wool pads:** Always choose honeycomb cellulose pads — wood wool (aspen) pads develop fungus in 1–2 seasons and produce musty smells.
