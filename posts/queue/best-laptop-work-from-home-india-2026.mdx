## WFH in India — Power Cut Considerations

Indian WFH users face 1–3 hour power cuts in many cities, especially in summer. A laptop with 10hr+ battery ensures you don't lose work. MacBook Air M3 (18hr rated, 14hr real) is the most resilient to power cuts. Pair with a UPS (₹3,000–₹5,000) for your router to maintain internet during cuts.
