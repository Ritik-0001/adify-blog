## Hub vs Dock: Which Do You Need?

**USB Hub:** Bus-powered, compact, no external power. Works for monitors + USB devices + charging. Port bandwidth is shared. Best for: laptop + 1 monitor + a few USB devices.

**Docking Station:** External power supply, more ports, dedicated bandwidth. Supports dual 4K displays. Best for: permanent desk setup with multiple monitors and peripherals.

**Thunderbolt Dock:** Requires Thunderbolt laptop. 40Gbps bandwidth. Only option for dual 4K/60Hz. Best for: MacBook Pro, ThinkPad X1, Dell XPS users with dual 4K monitors.
