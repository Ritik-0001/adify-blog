## Why 4K Matters at This Price

**Future-proof content.** Netflix 4K, Amazon Prime 4K, Disney+ Hotstar 4K, and YouTube 4K are widely available on Indian internet connections. A 4K TV with broadband/fibre connection delivers noticeably sharper content versus FHD.

**HDR support.** 4K TVs in this range support HDR10 and Dolby Vision. HDR (High Dynamic Range) improves contrast and colour accuracy more visibly than the jump from Full HD to 4K resolution alone — it's the more impactful upgrade for most content.
