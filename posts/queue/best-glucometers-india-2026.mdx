## Buying Guide: Glucometers in India 2026

**Accuracy standards:** Look for ISO 15197:2013 certification — requires 95% of readings within ±15mg/dL of laboratory reference. Accu-Chek, OneTouch, and Contour are ISO certified. Uncertified glucometers can be significantly inaccurate.

**Strip cost — the real long-term cost:**
At 2 tests/day = 730 tests/year. Strip cost per test:
- Budget brands (Dr. Morepen): ₹7–₹8 = ₹5,000–₹6,000/year
- Premium brands (Accu-Chek, OneTouch): ₹10–₹14 = ₹7,000–₹10,000/year

Strip availability in local pharmacies matters — choose brands whose strips are stocked in your city's chemist shops.

**Blood sample size:** Smaller sample requirement (0.5–0.6μL) is better for elderly patients with poor circulation who struggle to produce large blood drops.

**Memory and records:** Minimum 500-reading memory. App connectivity allows sharing records with doctors via email or printed PDF — valuable for consultations.

*Important: A glucometer is a monitoring tool, not a diagnostic device. Consult your doctor for diabetes diagnosis, treatment, and target glucose ranges.*
