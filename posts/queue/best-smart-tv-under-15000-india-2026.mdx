## What to Expect Under ₹15000

**HD Ready, not Full HD.** All TVs under ₹15000 are HD Ready (1366x768). Full HD (1920x1080) starts at ₹18,000+. For 32-inch TVs, the difference is less noticeable than on larger screens — at typical Indian TV viewing distances (2–3 metres), HD Ready is adequate for 32".

**Android TV vs other OS.** Android TV (Google ecosystem) is the most versatile — access to the Play Store with all major streaming apps (Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hotstar, YouTube). Budget TVs from lesser brands often run restricted Android variants that limit app installation.

**Speaker quality matters.** In small rooms, TV speakers are often the primary audio source. Prioritise 20W+ stereo speakers over lesser alternatives if you don't own a separate soundbar.
