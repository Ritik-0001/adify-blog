## Buying Guide

**Why pressure sensors matter for Indian buyers:** Clinical surveys show that over-brushing — pressing too hard — is the leading cause of gum recession and enamel abrasion in Indian adults, particularly those who grew up using firm-bristle manual brushes. A pressure sensor that slows the motor (Oral-B Pro 3) or flashes an alert (Oral-B Pro 2, Sonicare 3100) actively prevents this damage. At ₹2,000+, there is no reason to buy a brush without a pressure sensor.

**Three modes vs one mode:** The Oral-B Pro 3's Gum Care mode isn't a marketing gimmick — it uses a pulsing massage motion that stimulates the gingival tissue, shown in clinical studies to improve gum health over 4–8 weeks of regular use. If your dentist has mentioned "gum pockets" or "bleeding on probing," Gum Care mode is directly therapeutic.

**App connectivity trade-offs:** Both the Oclean X Pro and Colgate E1 offer app connectivity, but for different things. Oclean tracks brushing pressure and coverage over time. Colgate uses a phone camera for real-time plaque visualization. Neither is essential, but for data-driven health tracking, Oclean's approach is more directly useful for most users.

## FAQ

**What is the difference between the Oral-B Pro 2 and Pro 3?**
The Pro 3 adds a third cleaning mode (Gum Care) and upgrades the pressure sensor to a motor-slowdown system rather than just an indicator light. For approximately ₹300–₹500 more, the Pro 3's active pressure protection is worth the upgrade if you have any history of aggressive brushing.

**Is sonic or oscillating-rotating better for Indian gum conditions?**
Both are effective. Oscillating-rotating (Oral-B) is slightly more effective for total plaque removal in clinical comparisons. Sonic (Philips, Oclean) is gentler on gums and better suited for users with active gum sensitivity or recession. Your dentist's recommendation should be the deciding factor — many Indian periodontists prefer sonic for post-treatment maintenance.

**Are Oclean replacement heads available in India?**
Yes — Oclean heads are available on Amazon India with Prime delivery. However, they are not available in offline pharmacy or electronics stores as of mid-2026. Budget ₹350–₹450 per head and replace every 3 months.

**Can I use an electric toothbrush after dental scaling?**
Yes, and in fact most Indian periodontists recommend transitioning to electric immediately post-scaling, as the technique consistency helps maintain the clean tooth surfaces longer. Use Sensitive mode for the first 2–4 weeks post-scaling. Avoid pressing the brush directly against inflamed gum tissue.
