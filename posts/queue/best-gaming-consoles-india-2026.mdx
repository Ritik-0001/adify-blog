| Profile | Best Console |

| Budget gamer | Xbox Series S + Game Pass |

**Game Pass vs buying games:** At ₹599/month, Game Pass costs ₹7,188/year and gives 400+ games. Buying 5 AAA games costs ₹20,000–₹25,000. If you play 2+ games/month, Game Pass pays back immediately.

**India warranty:** PS5 and Xbox Series S/X have official India distribution (Sony India, Microsoft India) with 1-year warranty. Nintendo Switch is grey market import — no official India warranty.
