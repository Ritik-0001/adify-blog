## Buying Guide: What to Look for in a Kids' Smartwatch

**Safety and GPS:** For younger children (under 10), GPS tracking is the most important feature. Look for watches that support SIM card insertion or pair with a parent's phone for real-time location sharing.

**Durability:** Children are hard on devices. Prioritize IP67 or IP68 water resistance, shock-resistant cases, and silicone straps that can handle sweat and rough play.

**Battery Life:** A watch that needs daily charging is impractical for school-going kids. Aim for 5 days minimum battery life.

**Parental Controls:** Some watches in India allow parents to lock certain features or monitor usage. This is a valuable feature for peace of mind.

**Price Range in India:** Budget options start at ₹1,299 and offer solid basic features. The sweet spot for most families is between ₹1,500 and ₹2,500, where you get calling features, health tracking, and good durability.
