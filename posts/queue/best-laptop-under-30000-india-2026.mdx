## What to Expect Under ₹30,000

**Processor:** Intel Core i3 12th/13th gen or AMD Ryzen 3/5 5000 series — adequate for productivity, not gaming or video editing.

**RAM:** 8GB (minimum), 16GB available at higher end of bracket.

**Storage:** 256GB–512GB SSD — avoid laptops with HDD storage at any price.

**Display:** FHD 1080p IPS or TN — TN has poor viewing angles, IPS is significantly better for daily use.

**Battery:** 6–10 hours realistic use.
