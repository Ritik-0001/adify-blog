## What to Know About Sub-₹500 Earphones

**Expect plastic build.** Under ₹500, all earphones use basic plastic housings. Cable quality is the most important durability factor — look for strain relief at the 3.5mm plug and earpiece junctions.

**Driver size vs quality.** Larger drivers (10mm+) often produce more bass but aren't inherently superior. JBL's 8.6mm sounds better than many 10mm competitors because tuning matters more than raw driver size.

**Replacement ear tips.** Budget earphones usually include one size of silicone ear tips. If the included tips don't seal well, ₹50–100 aftermarket tips from Amazon significantly improve both comfort and sound isolation.

**No wireless alternative under ₹500.** Any wireless earphone under ₹500 will have poor audio quality and unreliable Bluetooth. Buy wired at this price.
