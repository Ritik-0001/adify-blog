## What Is Cognitive Processor XR?

Sony's Cognitive Processor XR claims to process images the way human eyes perceive them — analysing what you focus on in a scene rather than processing all areas equally. In practice, this means:

- **XR Contrast Booster** — improves local contrast around bright objects
- **XR Triiluminos Pro** — expands colour gamut and accuracy
- **XR Clarity** — upscaling algorithm for non-4K content
- **XR Motion Clarity** — motion handling for sports content
- **XR Sound** — matches audio direction to on-screen action (on OLED models with Acoustic Surface)

The Cognitive Processor XR is exclusive to Bravia XR models — Sony's non-XR TVs don't have it.

## Sony Bravia XR A95L — ₹2,49,990 (55")

**Best picture quality TV in India.** QD-OLED technology (Quantum Dot + OLED) delivers:

**Acoustic Surface Audio+:** Four drivers built into the screen panel — sound literally comes from the characters' mouths on screen. No need for external soundbar for immersive audio.

**Note:** At ₹2,49,990, it's significantly more expensive than LG C4 (₹1,29,990). The picture quality advantage is real but visible primarily in dark room, side-by-side comparison.

## Is Sony Bravia XR Worth It vs LG OLED?

Sony Bravia XR A80L (₹1,49,990) vs LG C4 (₹1,29,990):

**A80L advantages:** Sony's more natural colour science, Acoustic Surface Audio, Netflix Calibrated Mode.
