## Buying Guide: Printers in India 2026

**The most important decision: Ink Tank vs Cartridge**

| Type | Upfront Cost | Cost per Page (Black) | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ink Tank (EcoTank/MegaTank) | ₹8,000–₹25,000 | ₹0.10–₹0.20 | 50+ pages/month |
| Laser | ₹12,000–₹30,000 | ₹0.50–₹1.00 | Text-heavy offices |
| Cartridge Inkjet | ₹2,500–₹8,000 | ₹2.00–₹5.00 | <30 pages/month |

**If you print more than 50 pages/month:** Get an ink tank printer — you'll recoup the higher upfront cost within 2–3 months.

**Inkjet vs Laser:**
- Inkjet (ink tank): Better for colour, photos, and occasional printing; ink can dry if not used weekly
- Laser: Better for high-speed text; toner doesn't dry out; no colour photos

**Wi-Fi printing:** Essential in 2026 — print from your phone, laptop, or tablet without cables. All recommended printers above include Wi-Fi.
