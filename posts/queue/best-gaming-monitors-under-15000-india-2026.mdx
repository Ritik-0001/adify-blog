## Buying Guide: Gaming Monitors Under ₹15,000

**Refresh rate priority for games:**
- 75Hz: Minimum for gaming — smoother than 60Hz
- 144Hz: Standard competitive gaming — worth the upgrade
- 165Hz+: Marginal improvement over 144Hz — mainly for hardcore esports

**Panel technology:**
- TN: Fastest response, worst colours and viewing angles — avoid unless budget is extremely tight
- IPS: Best colours, good response, recommended for all game types
- VA: Best contrast (deepest blacks for dark games), moderate response — good for RPGs and single-player

**Resolution vs refresh rate tradeoff:** Your GPU must drive the resolution at high frame rates. With budget GPUs (GTX 1660, RTX 3050): stick to 1080p 144Hz. With mid-range (RTX 3060, RX 6600): consider 1440p 144Hz.

**For PS5/Xbox:** Look specifically for HDMI 2.0 (enables 120fps) — HDMI 1.4 caps at 60fps even if the monitor is 144Hz.
