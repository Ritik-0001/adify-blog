## Display Requirements for Video Editing

| Requirement | Minimum | Ideal |
|---|---|---|
| Resolution | 1920x1080 | 2560x1600 or 4K |
| Colour Coverage | sRGB 100% | DCI-P3 100% |
| Panel Type | IPS | OLED or Mini-LED |
| Brightness | 300 nits | 500+ nits |

**OLED is highly recommended** for video editing — the perfect blacks and accurate colours make colour grading significantly more reliable than IPS panels.

## Software Considerations

**DaVinci Resolve (Free/Studio):** Best with NVIDIA GPU (CUDA acceleration). The free version is powerful enough for most creators.

**Adobe Premiere Pro:** Works well with both NVIDIA and AMD GPUs. Requires Adobe subscription (₹1,675/month).

**Final Cut Pro:** macOS only. One-time purchase (₹29,900). Most efficient video editing on Apple Silicon.

**CapCut / DaVinci (free):** Best for budget setups — avoid paying Premiere subscription if you're just starting.
