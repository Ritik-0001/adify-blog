## What to Look For Under ₹15,000

**5G:** All top picks above support 5G. In 2026, 5G is available in most Indian metro cities and expanding. Choose 5G for future-proofing — 4G phones bought today will feel outdated sooner.

**AMOLED vs LCD:** AMOLED provides deeper blacks, more vivid colours, and saves battery on dark themes. Under ₹15,000 in 2026, AMOLED is available. Avoid IPS LCD at this price if possible.

**RAM:** 6GB minimum. 8GB is better for multitasking. Many phones offer RAM expansion (virtual RAM using storage).

**Camera:** 50MP vs 100MP matters less than camera software and sensor size. Redmi Note 13's camera is good but not exceptional for photography enthusiasts.
