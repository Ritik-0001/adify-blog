## Why Health Monitoring Matters for Indians

India has one of the world's highest burdens of lifestyle diseases — cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and hypertension affect hundreds of millions. A smartwatch with continuous heart rate monitoring, SpO2 tracking, and ECG capability can detect early warning signs like irregular heart rhythm (atrial fibrillation), low blood oxygen during sleep (sleep apnea indicator), and abnormal resting heart rate. For Indians managing chronic conditions or with family health history risks, these features are not luxury — they are preventive tools.
