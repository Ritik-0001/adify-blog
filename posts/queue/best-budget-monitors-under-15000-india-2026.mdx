## Buying Guide: Monitors Under ₹15,000

**Panel types:**
- **IPS:** Best colours and viewing angles — best for creative work and general productivity
- **VA:** Better contrast (deeper blacks) — better for movies and dark-themed gaming
- **TN:** Fastest response times but poor colours and viewing angles — mostly obsolete

**Refresh rate guide:**
- 60–75Hz: Standard — fine for office work, video, and casual gaming
- 100–144Hz: Noticeably smoother — significant for competitive gaming
- 165Hz+: Marginal improvement over 144Hz for most users

**Response time explained:**
- "1ms" quoted on monitors is usually MPRT (pixel response), not GtG — GTG response of 4–5ms is common on budget monitors but not visible at 75–100Hz

**Resolution:** 1920×1080 (Full HD) is the standard at this price — 1440p monitors start at ₹20,000+.
