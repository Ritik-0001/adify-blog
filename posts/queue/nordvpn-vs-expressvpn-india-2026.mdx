## Speed

Both VPNs use WireGuard-based proprietary protocols (NordLynx and Lightway respectively) that achieve near-native connection speeds. In independent speed tests on Indian broadband (Jio Fiber, Airtel Xstream), NordVPN consistently delivers slightly higher throughput on NordLynx vs ExpressVPN on Lightway. The difference is minimal for normal use but relevant for 4K streaming.

---

## Privacy

Both have independently audited no-logs policies and have passed real-world government data requests by producing no usable data. Both are headquartered in privacy-friendly jurisdictions (Panama and British Virgin Islands). Neither operates physical servers in India post-CERT-In requirements. Privacy credentials are equivalent.

---

## Price

NordVPN is significantly cheaper — ₹199/month vs ₹399/month on 2-year plans. The price difference over 2 years is approximately ₹4,800. ExpressVPN's higher price is partially justified by slightly simpler interface and marginally better router support.

---

## Which Should You Buy?

**Choose NordVPN if:** You want the best value, a larger server network, and strong privacy at half the price. Recommended for most Indian users.

**Choose ExpressVPN if:** You need the best router VPN support (sharing VPN across a home network), slightly simpler setup on complex devices, or you specifically need consistent access to streaming platforms that occasionally block NordVPN servers.
