## Laser Dust Detection — Game Changer or Gimmick?

The V15 Detect's built-in green laser in the Motorbar cleaner head illuminates dust particles invisible to the naked eye. The first time you run it on a "clean-looking" floor, the laser reveals a cloud of fine particles that you had no idea existed.

This is genuinely useful — not a marketing gimmick. The laser reveals:
- Soil and fine sand carried in by footwear (common in Indian homes near construction)
- Allergens and dust mites on marble and tile floors
- Pet dander and hair that's almost invisible on light-coloured floors

The piezoelectric sensor counts particles and automatically adjusts suction — louder, faster suction when heavy debris detected, quieter for fine particles. This extends battery life significantly.

## 60-Minute Battery — Is It Enough?

60 minutes in Eco mode (lowest suction) for a large Indian home of 2000–3000 sq ft. In Auto mode (sensor-adjusted suction), expect 30–45 minutes.

For a standard 2BHK or 3BHK apartment (1000–1500 sq ft), one full charge covers the entire home with battery to spare.

For large independent houses (3000+ sq ft), battery may require recharge mid-session.
