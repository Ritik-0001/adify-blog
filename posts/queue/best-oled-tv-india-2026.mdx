## OLED vs QLED vs LED for Indian Homes

**Indian living room light:** Most Indian living rooms have window light during day and overhead lighting at night. OLED's infinite contrast is less impactful in very bright rooms — a QLED may be brighter. OLED excels in evening/night viewing with lights dimmed.

**Burn-in risk:** OLED burn-in from static logos is a real risk if you watch news channels (NDTV, Aaj Tak) with static tickers for 8–10hr daily. For news-heavy households, QLED or NEO QLED is safer.

**Best size for India:** 55" for rooms up to 15 feet viewing distance. 65" for larger living rooms (15–20 feet).
