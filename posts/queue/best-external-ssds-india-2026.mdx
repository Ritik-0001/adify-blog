## External SSD Buying Guide

**Interface matters:** USB 3.2 Gen 2 (10Gbps) is standard on all drives here — delivers ~1,000 MB/s real-world. USB 3.1/3.0 cables will bottleneck speeds. Always use the supplied USB-C cable.

**NVMe vs SATA inside:** Most premium portable SSDs use NVMe internally. Budget options use SATA (~550 MB/s max). All recommendations here are NVMe.

**Encryption:** AES 256-bit hardware encryption is standard on all drives listed. Enable password protection for sensitive data.

**IP rating:** Only the Samsung T7 Shield has IP65 — if you work outdoors, the extra ₹1,500 is worth it.
