## Buying Guide: Gaming Monitors Under ₹30,000

**1440p vs 1080p for gaming:**
- 1440p looks sharper — small enemy models are more visible at range, text is cleaner
- 1440p requires more GPU power — an RTX 4060 comfortably runs 1440p 100fps; an RTX 3060 may need settings reduction

**IPS vs VA for gaming:**
- IPS: Better colours, wider viewing angles, no ghosting — best for fast-paced competitive games
- VA: Higher contrast, deeper blacks — best for atmospheric single-player games; can exhibit ghosting at high speeds

**Refresh rate guide for gaming:**
- 144–165Hz: Significant upgrade from 60Hz — recommended entry point for gaming monitors
- 240Hz: Reserved for competitive esports players — marginal improvement over 165Hz for casual gaming

**G-Sync vs FreeSync vs G-Sync Compatible:**
- G-Sync Compatible (most IPS monitors): Works on NVIDIA and AMD GPUs with adaptive sync
- FreeSync Premium: AMD-native but works on NVIDIA too — minimal real-world difference
