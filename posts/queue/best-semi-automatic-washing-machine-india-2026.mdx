## Why Semi-Automatic Still Makes Sense in India

**Water supply flexibility:** You control exactly how much water goes in. Intermittent municipal supply or low-pressure situations are easily managed.

**Durability:** Simpler mechanism means fewer failure points. A good semi-automatic lasts 10–15 years vs 8–10 for fully automatic.

**Electricity:** Semi-automatics use the motor only for wash agitation and spin — much less power than fully automatic machines running heating elements, sensors, and control boards.

**Cost of repair:** Service is simpler and cheaper. Parts are widely available even in small towns.

---

## Who Should Buy Semi-Automatic

- Families in areas with 2–4 hours water supply per day
- Budget buyers wanting long-lasting machine under ₹15,000
- Those in areas with frequent voltage fluctuations
- Joint families needing to wash large loads quickly
