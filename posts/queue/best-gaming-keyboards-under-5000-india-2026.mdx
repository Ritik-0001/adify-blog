## Buying Guide: Gaming Keyboards Under ₹5,000

**Switch types:**
- **Linear (Red):** Smooth keystroke, no tactile bump, quiet — preferred for gaming (fast actuation)
- **Tactile (Brown):** Bump at actuation point — good for typing + gaming hybrid
- **Clicky (Blue):** Audible click + tactile bump — satisfying but loud — not for shared spaces

**TKL vs Full-size for gaming:**
- TKL: No numpad — frees 25% more desk space for mouse movement — standard for competitive gaming
- Full-size: Has numpad — useful for work but cramped for gaming mouse tracking

**N-Key Rollover (NKRO):** Every key registers simultaneously even when pressing many keys at once — essential for complex gaming combos.

**Hot-swap worth it:** If you're unsure which switch type you prefer, hot-swap allows changing switches without soldering — strongly recommended for first-time mechanical keyboard buyers.
