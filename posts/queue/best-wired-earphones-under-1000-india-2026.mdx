## Wired vs Wireless Under ₹1000

At under ₹1000, wired earphones remain clearly superior to wireless options. Wireless earphones at this price use basic Bluetooth chips with poor codec support (often SBC only), resulting in compressed, flat audio. Wired earphones provide uncompressed audio directly from your phone's DAC — noticeably better sound for music listening.

The only exception: if you have a phone without a 3.5mm jack (newer iPhones, some Android flagships), wireless earphones or USB-C adapters become necessary. In that case, budget ₹1500–2000 for entry-level wireless earbuds.
