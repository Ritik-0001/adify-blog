## Buying Guide: Microwave Ovens in India 2026

**Which type to choose:**
| Type | What It Does | Best For | Price Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solo | Reheat, defrost, basic cooking | Students, small homes | ₹4,000–₹8,000 |
| Grill | Reheat + grill (kebabs, toast) | Snack lovers | ₹7,000–₹12,000 |
| Convection | Reheat + grill + bake + roast | Full kitchen use | ₹10,000–₹20,000 |

**Capacity guide:**
- 15–20L: 1–2 people
- 20–25L: 2–4 people
- 25–32L: 4+ people and batch cooking

**Inverter technology:** Worth the premium for cooking quality — prevents uneven heating that ruins eggs, fish, and delicate foods.

**Cavity material:**
- Stainless steel: Durable, good for grill mode (withstands high heat)
- Ceramic/enamel: Easiest to clean — recommended for heavy Indian cooking
