## Display — Compact but Limited

The 8.7-inch display is the Tab A9's defining characteristic — it's significantly smaller than competitors like the Redmi Pad SE (11 inches) and Realme Pad 2 (11.5 inches) at a similar price.

**Why choose 8.7 inches:** Portability. The Tab A9 fits in a jacket pocket and large handbags — true pocketable tablet. For users who travel frequently, use the tablet on public transport, or want one-handed holding, the compact size is a genuine advantage.

**Display quality:** The 1340×800 resolution at 8.7 inches produces 178 PPI — adequate but noticeably less sharp than the Redmi Pad SE at the same price. For reading text, the lower resolution is visible. For video, 800p is acceptable.

---

## Performance — Competent for Daily Tasks

The Helio G99 handles daily tasks comfortably:
- YouTube, Netflix, Prime Video — smooth
- WhatsApp, email, browsing — no issues
- Google Classroom, Zoom — works well
- Light gaming (Candy Crush, Clash of Clans) — fine
- Heavy gaming (BGMI, Call of Duty Mobile) — playable at medium settings, occasional stutters

**For the price:** The Helio G99 is a capable mid-range chip. However, at ₹13,999–₹16,999, the Redmi Pad SE offers Snapdragon 680 with a much larger screen — similarly capable for the same tasks with more screen real estate.

---

## Battery and Charging

5,100mAh is the Tab A9's weakest spec. At 60Hz and the smaller display, battery life is acceptable (7–8 hours screen-on time), but 15W charging is slow by 2026 standards — a full charge takes 2.5–3 hours.

**Comparison:** Redmi Pad SE has 8,000mAh (56% more battery) at a similar price. For students who need all-day battery, this matters significantly.

## Verdict

The Samsung Galaxy Tab A9 is a competent compact tablet held back by its display size, resolution, and battery capacity compared to similarly priced competition. Its main advantage is Samsung brand trust, service network, and the unique compact form factor. For most Indian users, the Tab A9 Plus (₹19,999) or the Redmi Pad SE (₹12,999) offer meaningfully better value. The Tab A9 is the right choice only if the compact 8.7-inch size is a non-negotiable requirement.
