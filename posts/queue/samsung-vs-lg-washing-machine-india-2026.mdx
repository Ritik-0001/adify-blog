## Technology Comparison

### Samsung's Key Technologies

**EcoBubble:** Pre-mixes detergent with air and water into micro-bubbles before entering the drum. Penetrates fabrics 40x faster than liquid detergent alone — better cleaning at lower temperatures (saves electricity).

**QuickDrive:** Moves drum and backwall independently in opposite directions — cuts wash time by 50% with equal cleaning quality. Available in higher-end Samsung front-loads.

**Digital Inverter Motor:** Variable speed control reduces energy consumption by 40% vs conventional motors. 10-year warranty is market standard.

### LG's Key Technologies

**TurboWash:** Powerful spray nozzle reduces wash time from 59 minutes to 39 minutes. Available on LG front-loads.

**Steam:** LG includes steam wash in more models — sanitises at 95°C, removes 99.9% allergens. Excellent for allergy-prone families.

**6 Motion Direct Drive:** Changes drum motion (rolling, tumbling, stepping, scrubbing, filtration, swinging) based on fabric type. Arguably the most advanced fabric care system available.

**Rat Away:** Motor winding coated to repel rodents — unique to LG in India and genuinely useful.

## Which Brand for Which Buyer

**Choose Samsung if:**
