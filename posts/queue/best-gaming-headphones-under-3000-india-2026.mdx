## What's Worth the Extra ₹1000 vs Sub-₹2000 Headsets

**Global brand vs Indian brand.** HyperX, SteelSeries, and Razer headsets undergo stricter quality control than Cosmic Byte and boAt. Driver consistency, headband durability, and mic quality are all noticeably better at the ₹2500–3000 range from international brands.

**Spatial audio quality.** DTS Headphone:X (HyperX) and Razer's 7.1 virtual surround algorithms are more accurate than the generic virtual surround software bundled with Indian gaming headsets. This translates to more precise directional audio in competitive FPS games.

**Comfort for long sessions.** Memory foam ear cushions (HyperX, Razer) compress less over long sessions than PU leather foam used in budget headsets. For 4–6 hour gaming sessions common in Indian gaming culture, comfort significantly affects performance.
