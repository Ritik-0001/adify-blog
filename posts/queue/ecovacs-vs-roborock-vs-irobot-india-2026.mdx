## Overview

Choosing a robot vacuum in India has never been harder — or more exciting. Three global giants now compete directly on Amazon.in: Ecovacs, Roborock, and iRobot. Each brand has a distinct philosophy and a flagship that represents the best of what it can do. Ecovacs leads with all-in-one stations and hot-water mopping. Roborock dominates on raw suction power and app sophistication. iRobot (now owned by SharkNinja) built its reputation on obstacle avoidance and reliability.

Indian homes present a unique set of challenges that expose the differences between these brands clearly. Most urban Indian apartments feature large expanses of marble, granite, or vitrified tile — surfaces that need to look clean and polished, not just vacuumed. Dust accumulation is heavy due to open windows, construction activity nearby, and seasonal pollution spikes. Pet hair from dogs and cats is increasingly common. And mopping matters here far more than in Western homes, where carpet is dominant. Any robot vacuum that cannot mop effectively will feel incomplete for Indian buyers.

In this comparison we evaluate all three brands across the dimensions that matter most to Indian consumers: price-to-performance ratio, cleaning on Indian tile and marble, mopping quality, app functionality in India, warranty support, and availability of physical service centres. ## Detailed Reviews

### 1. Ecovacs DEEBOT T20 Omni — Best Mopping System

**Price:** ₹44,999–₹49,999

The DEEBOT T20 Omni is Ecovacs' flagship for Indian buyers, and it makes a strong case based on one feature alone: hot water mop washing. While Roborock rinses its mop pads with ambient water, the T20 Omni heats water before washing the mop, making it genuinely hygienic. For Indian homes where floors are mopped daily as a matter of habit and hygiene, this is not a gimmick — it is a meaningful differentiator.

The All-in-One station handles auto-emptying (2.5-litre dustbag), mop washing, mop drying with warm air, and water refilling. You truly do not need to touch the robot between weekly maintenance sessions. Suction at 6,000 Pa handles fine dust on marble effortlessly, and the OZMO Turbo 2.0 rotating mop scrubs dried stains better than a flat oscillating pad.

**Pros:** Hot water mop wash, excellent all-in-one station, competitive price for features offered, AIVI 3D obstacle avoidance recognises shoes and cables
**Cons:** App not as polished as Roborock, fewer customisation options, brush can tangle with long hair

### 2. Roborock S8 Pro Ultra — Best Overall Performance

**Price:** ₹74,999–₹84,999

The Roborock S8 Pro Ultra is the most capable robot vacuum available in India right now, full stop. Its DuoRoller brush system uses two interlocked rubber rollers instead of the traditional bristle brush, which means it doesn't tangle with pet hair or long hair — a major benefit in Indian homes where long hair is nearly universal. Suction at 6,000 Pa is tied with the T20 Omni, but sonic mopping at 3,000 vibrations per minute lifts ground-in dirt on stone floors.

The self-empty, self-wash, self-refill station is genuinely set-and-forget. The Roborock app is the best in the business — multilingual support, granular room-by-room scheduling, and works perfectly in India without VPN. The S8 Pro Ultra also lifts its mop pad automatically when it transitions to carpet, a feature that prevents wet streaks on any rugs you have in bedrooms.

**Pros:** Best app experience, DuoRoller prevents hair tangles, mop lifts on carpet, official India support via Roborock India
**Cons:** Most expensive option, station is large, premium price may not be justified if mopping is your priority (T20 Omni mops better per rupee)

### 4. iRobot Roomba j7+ — Best Obstacle Avoidance

**Price:** ₹54,999

The Roomba j7+ is iRobot's most sensible choice for Indian buyers. Its PrecisionVision navigation camera identifies and avoids obstacles in real time — cords, shoes, socks, and yes, pet waste. The CleanBase Automatic Dirt Disposal empties the bin automatically into an allergen-lock bag. AeroForce cleaning system generates strong suction and works well on tile grout and marble.

Where iRobot falls short for India is mopping. The Roomba Combo j7+ adds a mop pad, but it does not scrub — it merely dampens the floor. For a country where mopping is a daily ritual, this is a significant limitation. Indian buyers who care about wet floor cleaning will find Roborock or Ecovacs more satisfying.

**Pros:** Best obstacle avoidance of the three brands, CleanBase is large and effective, US brand with now-local support

## Buying Guide / Comparison Analysis

**Price Range:** Roborock offers the widest spread (₹24,999 to ₹84,999), making it the most accessible brand at every budget. Ecovacs sits in the middle (₹44,999–₹54,999 for flagship), and iRobot is mid-to-premium (₹44,999–₹59,999) without offering an entry-level option.

**Cleaning on Indian Tile and Marble:** All three brands clean Indian hard floors well. Roborock's DuoRoller is best for homes with long hair. Ecovacs' rotating mop is best for dried stains. iRobot's AeroForce is reliable but less aggressive.

**Mopping Quality:** Ecovacs wins clearly with hot water mop washing and rotating mop pads. Roborock is second with sonic vibration mopping. iRobot is a distant third — its mopping is a checkbox feature, not a genuine capability.

**App Experience in India:** Roborock's app is the best — fast, responsive, works without VPN, and offers the most customisation. Ecovacs' app (Ecovacs Home) works in India but feels less refined. iRobot's app works but is clearly built for US users.

**Service Centres in India:** Roborock India has authorised service centres in major metros. Ecovacs India also has official support. iRobot's India support is managed post-SharkNinja acquisition but is less widespread — check availability in your city before buying.

**Warranty:** All three brands offer a 1-year warranty in India. Extended warranty via Amazon is available for all.

## FAQ

**Which brand is best for Indian homes with marble floors?**
All three handle marble well, but Ecovacs' rotating mop does the best job of cleaning dried stains on polished marble without leaving water marks.

**Does Roborock work in India without a VPN?**
Yes. The Roborock app works seamlessly in India. Roborock India is an official presence, so there are no connectivity or region-lock issues.

**Is iRobot officially sold in India?**
Yes, iRobot products are officially available on Amazon.in and some offline retailers. Warranty support exists but service centres are fewer than Roborock's.

**Which brand is best value for money in India in 2026?**
Roborock offers the best value at both the budget end (Q5 Pro at ₹24,999) and the premium end (S8 Pro Ultra). Ecovacs is better value if mopping is your primary concern.
