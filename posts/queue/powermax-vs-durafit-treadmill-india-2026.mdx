## Powermax Strengths

**Established track record:** Powermax has been in the Indian fitness equipment market since 2009 — 15 years of product refinement means fewer design flaws and more reliable motors. User reviews over 3–5 years of ownership are more available and consistently positive.

**Wider service network:** 300+ city service coverage is the best among Indian treadmill brands. For buyers in tier-2 cities, Powermax service accessibility is a meaningful advantage.

**Build quality:** Powermax frames tend to be heavier gauge steel — machines feel more solid during use, particularly at higher speeds. Less vibration at 12–14 km/h.

**Consistent motor performance:** Powermax motors are well-rated for sustained operation — 45–60 minute daily sessions at moderate speeds without significant overheating.

## Durafit Strengths

**Better value per spec:** Durafit consistently offers higher specifications (more HP, larger decks, auto-incline) at ₹2,000–₹5,000 lower price points than comparable Powermax models.

**Auto-incline at lower prices:** Durafit Eagle (₹29,990) offers motorised incline — a feature that typically appears in Powermax models at ₹35,000+.

**Belt size:** Durafit decks tend to be slightly larger at equivalent price points — 48x130cm vs Powermax's 46x120cm at similar prices. Better for taller users or those with longer strides.

**Growing service:** Durafit has expanded service to 200+ cities in just 6 years — improving rapidly.

## Who Should Buy Powermax

- Buyers in smaller cities where service matters
- Those who prioritise long-term reliability over specs
- Users who will run the treadmill daily for 45–60 minutes

## Who Should Buy Durafit

- Buyers in metro cities with good Durafit service
- Those who want more specifications per rupee
- Users who want auto-incline at lower price points
- Taller users (above 5'10") who benefit from larger decks

## Bottom Line

**Powermax for reliability and service.** **Durafit for value and specs.** Neither is a bad choice — both brands have delivered consistent products for Indian home gyms. If service accessibility is your primary concern, choose Powermax. If you want the most treadmill for your money, Durafit wins.
