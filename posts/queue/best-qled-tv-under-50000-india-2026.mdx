## QLED vs LED vs OLED

**QLED (Quantum LED):** Standard LED backlight + quantum dot colour filter. Brighter than OLED, excellent colour accuracy, no burn-in risk. Best for bright Indian living rooms.

**Standard LED:** Budget option, adequate colour gamut, lower peak brightness. All TVs under ₹25000 use standard LED.

**OLED:** Perfect blacks (pixels turn off), infinite contrast ratio, risk of burn-in with static elements (news tickers, cricket scores). LG OLED starts at ₹1,29,990 in India — premium segment.

For Indian homes with bright ambient light, QLED's higher brightness makes it preferable to OLED for daytime TV watching.
