## How to Read Indian Energy Star Ratings

India's BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) star ratings are recalibrated every 2–3 years — a 5-star from 2021 may be less efficient than a 3-star from 2024. Always check the **Annual Energy Consumption figure** on the sticker rather than star count alone.

**Better metric:** Annual kWh consumption. Under 200 units/year for a 250L refrigerator is excellent.

---

## Inverter Compressor vs Non-Inverter

**Non-inverter:** Motor runs at fixed speed, turns on and off repeatedly. Each start cycle draws peak current. Noisier and less efficient.

**Inverter:** Motor varies speed continuously — runs slower when less cooling needed. 20–40% more efficient. All major brands now include inverter compressors in models above ₹15,000.

**10-year motor warranty:** Offered by Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, and Godrej on inverter compressors — reflects manufacturer confidence.
