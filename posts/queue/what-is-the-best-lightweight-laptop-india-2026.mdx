## What "Lightweight" Actually Means for Indian Buyers

Carrying a laptop daily — metro commutes, college, travel — puts real strain on your back. The difference between a 1.2kg and a 2.0kg laptop is felt immediately over a full day's carry. Here's how laptop categories break down by weight:

| Category | Weight Range | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Ultralight (sub-1kg) | Under 1kg | LG Gram 14 (999g) |
| Light | 1.0–1.5kg | MacBook Air M3, ASUS Zenbook 14 |
| Standard | 1.5–2.0kg | HP Pavilion 15, Dell Inspiron 15 |
| Heavy | Above 2.0kg | Gaming laptops, workstations |

For daily carry, target under 1.5kg. Anything above 1.8kg becomes genuinely uncomfortable after 3–4 hours.
