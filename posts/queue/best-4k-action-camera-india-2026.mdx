## What to Look For in a 4K Action Camera

**4K frame rate.** 4K/30fps is standard. 4K/60fps provides smooth slow-motion. 4K/120fps (DJI Action 4, GoPro HERO12+) enables 4x slow-motion at full resolution — significantly more cinematic than digital slow-motion.

**Stabilisation at 4K.** Some budget cameras only apply EIS at 1080p. Confirm that stabilisation is active at 4K recording before purchasing — spec sheets sometimes obscure this limitation.

**Bitrate.** Higher bitrate (100 Mbps+) produces better colour grading latitude in post-production. GoPro (100–120 Mbps) and DJI (130 Mbps) both provide professional-grade bitrates. Budget cameras at 15–25 Mbps look flat and artefact-heavy when colour graded.
