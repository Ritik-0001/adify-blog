## Buying Guide: Soundbars in 2026

**Connection types — what you need:**
- **HDMI ARC/eARC:** Single cable for audio and control — best option if your TV has HDMI ARC
- **Optical:** Standard digital audio — works on all TVs
- **Bluetooth:** Wireless from phone/tablet — convenient but slight latency
- **USB:** Play music directly from drive

**Channel configurations:**
- 2.0 (stereo): Just the soundbar — improved over TV speakers
- 2.1: Soundbar + wireless subwoofer — best for movies and music
- 5.1/7.1: Soundbar + sub + rear speakers — genuine surround sound

**What makes a difference:**
Under ₹10,000, the biggest audio upgrade is getting a **subwoofer** — even a budget 2.1 system beats any 2.0 soundbar for movies.
