## Video Call Quality: The Biggest Differences

**Microphone vs camera.** Colleagues hear you through your microphone before they see you on video. A poor microphone that echoes or picks up background noise is more disruptive to professional calls than a 720p vs 1080p camera difference.

**Lighting matters more than webcam resolution.** A ₹1,695 Logitech C270 with a ₹500 ring light (or positioned near a window) looks better on video calls than a ₹10,000 webcam in poor lighting. Add lighting before upgrading your webcam.

**1080p requirement.** Most video call platforms compress video to 720p or lower by default on bandwidth-limited connections. Full 1080p is only fully utilised on high-bandwidth connections (100 Mbps+).
