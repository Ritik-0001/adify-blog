## Buying Guide: Coffee Machines in India 2026

**Style guide — choose your coffee type first:**
| Coffee Style | Best Machine Type | Recommended |
|---|---|---|
| Strong espresso shots | Pump espresso | De'Longhi Dedica Arte |
| Latte/cappuccino | Pump espresso + steamer | De'Longhi Dedica Arte |
| Quick, consistent | Capsule | Nespresso Vertuo Pop |
| South Indian filter kaapi | Drip/filter | Morphy Richards Europa |
| Fresh beans, automated | Bean-to-cup | Philips EP1224 |
| Budget, zero running cost | Stovetop | Bialetti Moka Express |

**Bar pressure explained:** 9–15 bar is the ideal range for espresso extraction. Under 7 bar produces weak, sour espresso. Budget machines claiming "20 bar" usually only reach 9 bar at the portafilter.

**Running costs to consider:**
- Capsules: ₹25–₹40/cup
- Ground coffee (espresso): ₹5–₹15/cup
- Whole beans (freshly ground): ₹8–₹20/cup
