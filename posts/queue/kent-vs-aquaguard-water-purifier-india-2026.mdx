## Brand Background — Kent and Aquaguard

**Kent RO Systems** was founded in 1999 by Mahesh Gupta in Noida. Kent popularised RO technology in Indian homes through aggressive marketing and competitive pricing. Today, Kent claims to be India's No. 1 RO water purifier brand by volume, with over 1,500 service centres across India.

**Aquaguard (Eureka Forbes)** has been in India since 1984 — far longer than Kent. The brand built India's water purifier market from scratch, starting with UV and gravity-based systems before adding RO. Eureka Forbes operates on a direct sales model with a large doorstep service team, particularly strong in metros and Tier 1 cities.

## Technology Comparison — Who Has the Better Purification?

Both brands offer RO+UV+UF at the mid-range. The differences lie in the proprietary technology layered on top:

**Kent's Technology:**
- Mineral ROTM — retains natural minerals post-RO using a TDS controller
- Zero Water Wastage concept (in premium models, reject water goes to storage tank)
- UV LED in some newer models (longer life than traditional UV lamps)

**Aquaguard's Technology:**
- e-boiling+ — UV exposure equivalent to 20 minutes of boiling
- IntelliSense — real-time monitoring of water quality and filter status
- Active Copper enrichment (in Copper models) for added health benefits
- UV+UF independently verified performance claims

On paper, Aquaguard's IntelliSense and e-boiling+ represent more sophisticated engineering. However, both brands deliver clean, safe water — the practical difference for most consumers is minimal.

## Service Network — The Most Critical Factor

This is where Kent and Aquaguard diverge most sharply, and it matters enormously in India.

**Kent Service Network:**
- 1,500+ service centres
- Available in most Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities, even some Tier 4 towns
- AMC widely available pan-India
- Replacement parts readily available in local markets
- Service response typically 24–48 hours in most cities

**Aquaguard Service Network:**
- Strong in metros: Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune
- Tier 1 cities well covered but Tier 2+ coverage is patchy
- Doorstep service model — technicians come to you
- Service response in metros: often same-day or next-day

If you live in a small city or semi-urban area, Kent's wider network makes it the safer choice. If you are in a major metro and prioritise service quality over service availability, Aquaguard edges ahead.

## Water Taste — Which Brand Produces Better Tasting Water?

Water taste after RO purification depends heavily on TDS controller settings. Kent's TDS controller gives users more manual control over output TDS, which many find produces better-tasting water. Aquaguard's IntelliSense does this automatically.

In blind taste tests conducted by various Indian consumer forums, neither brand consistently outperforms the other. The difference is negligible once TDS is adjusted correctly.

## Which Brand Should You Choose?

**Buy Kent if:**
- You live outside a metro or in a Tier 2/3 city
- You want lower filter replacement costs
- You want more control over TDS settings
- You want the widest service coverage pan-India

**Buy Aquaguard if:**
- You live in a major metro
- You prioritise service quality and doorstep support
- You want smart monitoring and IntelliSense auto-adjustment
- You are willing to pay a premium for Eureka Forbes' brand reputation

For the majority of Indian homes, **Kent Grand Plus** is the recommendation. It covers more of India, costs less to maintain, and delivers safe, clean water reliably. If you are in Mumbai, Bengaluru, or Delhi and will use Aquaguard's service infrastructure, **Aquaguard Marvel NXT** is an equally valid choice.
