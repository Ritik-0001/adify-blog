## Why Open-Ear Matters in India

**Traffic awareness:** Indian urban roads require constant awareness — honking, cyclists, two-wheelers on footpaths. In-ear noise isolation (especially ANC earbuds) significantly reduces awareness of surroundings. Open-ear options let you hear ambient sounds while listening to music.

**Heat and hygiene:** During Indian summers (35–45°C), silicone ear tips in sealed earbuds trap sweat and heat. Open-ear designs have no seal — more comfortable and hygienic for outdoor running.

**Long-run comfort:** Bone conduction and open-ear designs cause zero ear canal fatigue, making them better for runs exceeding one hour. In-ear options create pressure from ear tips that becomes uncomfortable over extended sessions.
