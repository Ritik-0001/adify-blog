## Running on a Treadmill Under ₹30,000 — Realistic Expectations

**Max speed of 14 km/h** covers:
- Brisk walking: 5–6 km/h
- Jogging: 7–9 km/h
- Easy running: 9–11 km/h
- Moderate running: 11–14 km/h

For recreational runners, 14 km/h is sufficient. If you run at 5-min/km pace or faster regularly, consider a ₹40,000+ machine with better motor durability.

**Belt size matters:** 45x130cm is the minimum for comfortable running — anything smaller forces shorter strides and increases injury risk.

---

## Apartment Safety Notes

Treadmills at 12–14 km/h create significant vibration. In Indian apartment buildings:
- Place a thick rubber mat underneath (₹2,000–₹4,000)
- Use the machine between 7 AM and 9 PM
- Avoid running sessions longer than 30 minutes continuously at full speed to manage motor heat
