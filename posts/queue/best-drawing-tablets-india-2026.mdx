## Buying Guide: Drawing Tablets in 2026

**Pen tablet vs pen display:**
- **Pen tablet (blind):** Draw on the tablet while looking at your monitor. Cheaper, lighter, no parallax. Takes 1–2 weeks to adjust hand-eye coordination.
- **Pen display (screen tablet):** Draw directly on the screen — natural and intuitive from day one. 3–5× more expensive.

**Pressure levels:**
- 4,096: Professional standard — more than adequate for most artists
- 8,192: Noticeable difference only in ultra-fine line weight work

**Active area sizing:**
- Small (6"×4"): Good for simple digital art, tight workspaces
- Medium (10"×6"): Best all-round size — natural arm movement, less wrist strain
- Large (12"×8"+): Professional standard for detailed illustration

**Free software to start with:**
- **Krita**: Free, professional-grade raster art — the best free Photoshop alternative
- **Clip Studio Paint**: ₹699 one-time — industry standard for illustration and manga
