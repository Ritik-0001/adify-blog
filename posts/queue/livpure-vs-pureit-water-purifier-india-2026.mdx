## Brand Overview — Livpure and HUL Pureit

**Livpure** was founded in 2012 by SAR Group (also the promoters of Liberty Shoes). The brand positioned itself as a technology-forward, value-priced alternative to Kent and Aquaguard. Livpure has since expanded its range significantly and improved its service network in larger cities.

**HUL Pureit** is the water purifier division of Hindustan Unilever Limited — one of India's largest FMCG companies. Pureit entered the market in 2004 with gravity-based purifiers and expanded to UV and RO. HUL's brand equity and distribution strength give Pureit an advantage in marketing and consumer trust, though its service infrastructure for RO systems is not as extensive as Kent's.

## Technology Comparison

**Livpure Technology:**
- RO (7-stage in GLO PRO) with mineralizer that adds back essential minerals
- UV + UF combination for comprehensive biological purification
- In-tank UV sterilisation in some models keeps stored water fresh
- Copper+ range with copper infusion post-purification

**Pureit Technology:**
- Advanced Alert System — alerts before filter exhaustion, not after
- e-boiling+ equivalent purification in newer models
- ECO mode in Pureit Eco reduces water waste significantly
- Germkill Battery technology in gravity models
- FiltraMax in premium models for multi-stage protection

Livpure's mineralizer is a genuine differentiator — many budget RO purifiers produce overly de-mineralised water. Pureit's Advanced Alert System and the Eco Water Saver's waste-water efficiency are its strongest technological claims.

### Water Waste: The Pureit Eco's Biggest Advantage

The HUL Pureit Eco Water Saver's standout feature is its exceptionally low waste-water ratio. Pureit claims it recovers 60% of inlet water — meaning only 40% is wasted — compared to the typical 25–30% recovery of standard RO purifiers. Over a year for a family of 4, this can mean saving 5,000–10,000 litres.

This is a meaningful advantage in water-scarce cities and in areas where municipalities charge volumetrically.

### Mineralizer: Livpure's Nutritional Edge

RO removes nearly all dissolved minerals including essential calcium and magnesium. Livpure's mineralizer adds a measured dose of these minerals back, resulting in water that is both safe and nutritionally better than plain RO output. Pureit's standard RO models do not have an equivalent feature — though this is less critical than marketers suggest, since minerals from food easily compensate.

## Service Network — Where Each Brand Wins

**Livpure Service:**
- Good coverage in metros and Tier 1 cities
- Improving in Tier 2 cities but still gaps in smaller towns
- Response time in metros: 24–48 hours typically
- Annual AMC available online and through service centres

**Pureit Service:**
- Leverages HUL's massive distribution network for parts availability
- Better for non-metro buyers where local shops stock Pureit filters
- Service for RO models is separate from the gravity-filter service model
- Response times can vary significantly by region

For basic gravity Pureit models, service is essentially non-existent (no moving parts to service). For RO models, both brands are comparable in service quality, with Livpure slightly stronger in city centres and Pureit having better reach through HUL's network for spare parts.

## Which Brand Should You Choose?

**Choose Livpure GLO PRO if:**
- You want the lowest filter replacement costs
- You live in a metro or Tier 1 city with good Livpure service
- You want a mineralizer to restore nutrients after RO
- You are a first-time RO buyer on a budget

**Choose HUL Pureit Eco Water Saver if:**
- Water conservation is a priority for you
- You want a larger 10-litre storage tank
- You trust HUL's brand quality and want their FMCG-backed product support
- Your municipal water quality is moderate and you want efficient purification

**Choose HUL Pureit Classic (gravity) if:**
- You only have municipal tap water with low TDS
- You want zero electricity consumption
- You are in an area with frequent power cuts
- You want the lowest total cost of ownership

For a straight RO comparison, **Livpure GLO PRO** wins on value. For buyers who care about water waste above all else, **HUL Pureit Eco** is the better choice despite the higher price.
