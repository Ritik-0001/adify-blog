## Lenovo ThinkPad — The Case For

**Best keyboard:** ThinkPad keyboards have been the gold standard for 30 years. The 1.5mm key travel with TrackPoint red nub is loved by users who type intensively. Difficult to go back to other keyboards once you've used a ThinkPad.

**Repairability:** ThinkPad E and T series are among the most repairable laptops — screw-based panels, replaceable RAM, upgradeable SSD. Long-term total cost of ownership is lower.

**Value:** ThinkPad E series starts around ₹55,000–₹70,000 with solid business specs — good entry point for SMEs.

**ThinkShield Security:** Built-in ThinkShutter privacy cover, fingerprint reader, TPM 2.0, and optional IR camera.

## Dell Latitude — The Case For

**ProSupport Network:** Dell's ProSupport service with next-business-day on-site support is available in virtually every Indian city. This is a significant operational advantage for enterprises — downtime is minimised.

**Management ecosystem:** Dell Command | Update, Dell ProManage (endpoint management), and Windows Autopilot deployment work seamlessly. IT departments already using Dell infrastructure prefer continuity.

**Display options:** Dell Latitude offers more display configurations — anti-glare, touch, and privacy screen options at various price points.

**Global presence:** For organisations with employees across offices in multiple countries, Dell's global service contracts are simpler to manage.

## Decision Framework for Indian Buyers

**Choose ThinkPad if:**
- Users type extensively (ThinkPad keyboard is that good)
- SME budget is ₹60,000–₹80,000 per machine
- Organisation values repairability and modularity

**Choose Dell Latitude if:**
- Enterprise IT team needs centrally managed fleet
- ProSupport on-site service is critical
- Organisation already standardised on Dell ecosystem
- Procurement via Dell's India enterprise sales team

---

## Bottom Line

Both are excellent business laptops — you won't regret either. **ThinkPad wins on user experience** (keyboard, build, repairability). **Dell Latitude wins on enterprise service** (ProSupport, IT management ecosystem). Choose based on your organisation's IT support capability.
