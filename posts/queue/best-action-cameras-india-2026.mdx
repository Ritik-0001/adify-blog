## Buying Guide: Action Cameras in India 2026

**Stabilisation comparison:**
- HyperSmooth 6.0 (GoPro): Best for ±45° horizon levelling
- RockSteady 4.0 (DJI): Best overall EIS + optional horizon levelling
- Budget EIS: Handles walking and cycling, struggles on motorcycles

**Resolution guide:**
- 4K 60fps: Standard for smooth video with some slow-motion capability
- 4K 120fps: Smooth 4x slow motion — motorcycling, skiing, water sports
- 5.3K/8K: Future-proof crop room — useful for cropping stabilised footage

**Sensor size matters:** Larger sensors perform better in low light (dawn/dusk trekking, underwater). DJI Osmo Action 4's 1/1.3" sensor is a clear advantage over smaller sensors.

**India use cases:**
- Motorcyclists: GoPro Hero 13 (stabilisation) or DJI Action 5 Pro (battery)
- Trekkers/hikers: DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro (battery + cold weather)
- Swimmers/divers: DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro (20m) or AKASO Brave 8 (budget)
- Content creators: Insta360 X4 (360° creative flexibility)
