## Streaming Webcam Setup Tips for India

**Lighting first.** A ₹800 ring light improves stream quality more than upgrading from C270 to C922. Position the light slightly above eye level, facing your face. Avoid backlight from windows — sit facing the window, not with the window behind you.

**Internet upload speed.** Streaming to YouTube/Twitch requires 3–6 Mbps upload for 720p at 30fps. Check your Jio/Airtel upload speed before investing in a premium webcam — a poor connection limits output regardless of webcam quality.
