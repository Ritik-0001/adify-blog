## What Should a ₹15,000 Water Purifier Offer?

At ₹15,000, you should expect:
- **RO + UV + UF** triple purification (not just RO alone)
- **TDS Controller or Mineralizer** — so purified water retains natural minerals
- **Auto flush / Pre-RO sediment filter** — extends membrane life
- **8–10 litre storage tank** — enough for a family of 4
- **50% or better water recovery** — anything below 40% is wasteful
- **Reputable brand service** in your city

If a purifier in this price range doesn't offer RO+UV+UF and some form of mineral retention, skip it.

## Buying Tips for ₹15,000 Budget

**Check your TDS first:** Before buying, use a TDS meter (₹200 on Amazon) to measure your tap water TDS:
- Under 500 TDS: UV purifier is sufficient (save money)
- 500–2,000 TDS: RO essential — any model above works
- Above 2,000 TDS: Need high-capacity RO like AO Smith Z9 or Kent Grand Plus

**Annual filter cost matters more than purchase price:** A ₹10,000 purifier with ₹6,000/year filter cost is more expensive over 5 years than a ₹15,000 purifier with ₹4,000/year filters.

**Ask about installation:** Most brands offer free installation. Confirm this before buying — third-party installation can cost ₹800–₹1,500.
