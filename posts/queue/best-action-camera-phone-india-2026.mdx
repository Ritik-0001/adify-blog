## What Makes a Good Action Camera Phone

**Stabilisation type.** OIS (optical) stabilises the physical lens element — good for general movement. EIS (electronic) crops the sensor and warps the frame — good for fast movement but reduces field of view. Action Mode (iPhone) + OIS + EIS combined = best result.

**IP rating for Indian conditions.** Indian outdoor activities involve rain, dust, and water. IP68 (Samsung, iPhone flagships) means 30 minutes at 1.5m depth. IP67 (OnePlus 12) is 30 minutes at 1m. For monsoon-season adventure content in India, IP67+ is essential.

**4K/60fps vs 4K/120fps.** 4K/60fps is fine for normal speed. 4K/120fps enables 4x slow-motion without resolution penalty — dramatically more cinematic for sports, waterfalls, and fast action sequences.
