## Electric Hair Comb Types for India

**Heated combs:** Ceramic-toothed combs that heat to 150–230°C — straighten and smooth curly or wavy Indian hair in sections. Original professional salon tool for tight curl straightening before flat irons became standard.

**Ionic combs:** Emit negative ions while combing — reduce static, tame frizz, and add shine. No heat. Best for daily Indian hair detangling and frizz control without heat damage.

**Vibrating combs:** Electric vibration assists through thick Indian hair — reduces manual force required, preventing breakage from detangling force.
