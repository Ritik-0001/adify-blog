## What Can You Get in This Budget?

The ₹7,000–₹10,000 range is where RO purification becomes genuinely accessible. Purifiers here typically offer:

- RO membrane to remove dissolved salts, heavy metals, and TDS reduction
- UV lamp for additional bacteria and virus kill
- UF membrane as a backup for when UV is off
- 7–10 litre storage tanks
- Wall-mount designs that save counter space
- TDS controllers to retain some beneficial minerals

This is a significant leap from the under ₹5,000 segment — you are no longer relying on gravity or simple UV, but getting multi-stage purification that handles most Indian water types.
