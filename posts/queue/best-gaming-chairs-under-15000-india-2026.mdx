## Buying Guide: Gaming Chairs in 2026

**Gaming chair vs office ergonomic chair:**
At the same price, office ergonomic chairs (like Green Soul's ergonomic line) provide better lower back support than gaming chairs. Gaming chairs are designed for racing car seat aesthetics first, ergonomics second.

**However, gaming chairs win on:**
- Recline (90°–180°) — for relaxation and couch-like gaming sessions
- High back with neck/head support
- Aesthetic appeal — important for streaming setups

**What to check before buying:**
1. **Foam density:** Cold-cure foam > hot-cure foam. Ask or read reviews about long-term compression.
2. **Armrest type:** 4D armrests are worth the premium over 1D or 2D
3. **Weight capacity:** Match to your weight + 20% buffer
4. **Height range:** Gas piston range should match your height (most support 165–195cm well)

**PU leather care in India:** India's heat and humidity degrades PU leather faster — keep gaming chairs away from direct sunlight and wipe down after sweaty sessions.
