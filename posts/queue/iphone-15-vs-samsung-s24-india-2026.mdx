## Performance: A16 Bionic vs Snapdragon 8 Gen 3

**iPhone 15 (A16 Bionic):** Single-core performance champion — faster in everyday tasks, app launches, and most iOS apps. Sustained performance stays consistent because iOS is optimised specifically for A16.

**Samsung Galaxy S24 (Snapdragon 8 Gen 3):** Better multi-core performance for heavy workloads. Android multitasking takes advantage of Snapdragon's parallel processing. Games with DLSS and Ray Tracing (not many on mobile yet) benefit from Snapdragon's GPU.

**Real-world verdict:** Both phones are fast enough for any task in 2026. A16 Bionic remains fast — Apple's chips are generationally ahead of Snapdragon in power efficiency.

---

## Camera Comparison

**iPhone 15 (48MP, f/1.6):** Natural skin tones, conservative HDR processing, accurate colours. 2x optical-equivalent telephoto from crop. 4K/60fps video. Best colour science for outdoor and portrait photography.

**Samsung Galaxy S24 (50MP, f/1.8):** Samsung's processing is more vivid and colour-boosted — photos look more impressive on social media but less accurate to real life. Galaxy AI photo editing (Generative Edit, Circle to Search) adds AI-powered editing tools. 4K/60fps video.

**Winner:** iPhone 15 for natural photo quality. Samsung S24 for AI editing features and social media-optimised output.

---

## Display: 60Hz vs 120Hz

Samsung Galaxy S24's 120Hz Dynamic AMOLED display is noticeably smoother than iPhone 15's 60Hz panel. This is the iPhone 15's biggest disadvantage against S24 — at ₹44,900, Apple's base iPhone still uses 60Hz while Samsung's base Galaxy uses 120Hz.

ProMotion 120Hz is available on iPhone 15 Pro (₹1,19,900) but not the base model. For scroll smoothness, gaming, and video consumption — Samsung S24's 120Hz wins clearly.

---

## Software: iOS 18 vs One UI 7

**iOS 18:** Clean, fast, consistent. All apps work well. Apple Intelligence features (Writing Tools, enhanced Siri). 6 years of iOS updates guaranteed. No customisation beyond Apple's boundaries.

**One UI 7 (Samsung):** More customisable. Galaxy AI features (Live Translate, Note Assist, Chat Assist). DeX mode for desktop computing via external monitor. Samsung Pay. 4 years Android OS + 5 years security updates.

**Choose iOS if:** You value simplicity, are already in Apple ecosystem, or want Apple Intelligence.

**Choose One UI if:** You want more customisation, Galaxy AI features, or Samsung's extended ecosystem (SmartThings, DeX).
