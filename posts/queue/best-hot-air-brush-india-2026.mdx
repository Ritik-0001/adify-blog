## Hot Air Brush vs Straightener

**Hot air brushes** create volume, waves, and natural movement simultaneously while drying — one step for a complete style. Better for achieving the blowout look.

**Straighteners** create sleek, stick-straight styles with no volume — ideal for pin-straight looks. Cannot add volume or natural movement.

Most Indian women use both tools — straightener for sleek days, hot air brush for voluminous blowout days. The Revlon One-Step is the priority purchase if you haven't yet integrated a hot air brush into your routine.
