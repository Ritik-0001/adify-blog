## Buying Guide: UPS in India 2026

**VA rating guide:**
| Setup | Required VA/Watts |
|---|---|
| Router + modem only | 300VA/180W |
| PC + monitor + router | 600VA/360W |
| Gaming PC (RTX 4060) | 1000–1100VA/600–660W |
| Workstation (RTX 4090) | 1500VA/900W |
| NAS + network gear | 700VA/420W |

**Offline vs Online vs Line-Interactive:**
- **Offline:** Switches to battery during power cut (~5ms delay) — adequate for most use
- **Line-Interactive (AVR):** Corrects voltage without battery use — best for India's fluctuating voltage
- **Online:** Always on battery, zero transfer time — for servers and NAS

**India-specific tips:**
- **Always choose AVR:** India's grid voltage fluctuates 150V–280V frequently — AVR protection saves hardware
- **Battery replacement:** UPS batteries last 2–3 years in India — factor in ₹1,500–₹3,000 replacement cost every 3 years
- **Pure sine wave:** Required for inverter ACs, modern gaming PCs, and laptops — modified sine wave can damage power adapters
