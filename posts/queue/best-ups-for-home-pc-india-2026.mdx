## 5. APC BR1000G-IN — Best Premium Pick

**Price:** ₹7,500–₹9,500

For a high-end home PC with a powerful PSU or a workstation, the APC BR1000G-IN steps up with pure sine wave output and a full 1000VA capacity. Pure sine wave is critical if your PC uses an active PFC power supply (which most modern 80 Plus certified PSUs do) — modified sine wave can cause buzzing, overheating, or even damage these PSUs. The BR1000G-IN also features PowerChute software for automatic, unattended shutdowns during extended outages — ideal for users who run overnight renders or downloads.

**Key specs:**
- Capacity: 1000 VA / 600 W
