## Why Go Without WiFi?

Many Indian locations — rural areas, industrial zones, older buildings, and commercial properties — either lack reliable WiFi or have connectivity that is too unstable for streaming cameras. Wired CCTV systems record directly to a local DVR hard drive without ever touching the internet. This also means your footage is not stored on third-party cloud servers, which is a significant privacy advantage for shops, banks, and businesses.
