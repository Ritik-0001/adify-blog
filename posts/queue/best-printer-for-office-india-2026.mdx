## What Makes an Office Printer Different From a Home Printer?

Office printing has different priorities from home printing. Here's what matters in an Indian office environment in 2026:

**Speed:** Offices print batches — 50 invoices, 100 reports, 200 letters. A printer at 20 ppm vs 10 ppm makes a tangible difference in productivity.

**Duty cycle:** Office printers need to handle 1,000–5,000 pages/month consistently without breaking down.

**Toner/ink longevity:** Office printers sometimes sit on weekends or during office closures. Toner (laser) never dries; inkjet heads can clog if unused for too long.

**Paper capacity:** Home printers have 60–100 sheet trays. Office printers should hold 150–250+ sheets to reduce constant refilling.

**Duplex (double-sided):** Automatic duplex printing halves paper costs for internal documents — essential for any medium office.

**Network connectivity:** Multiple users printing from different computers and phones requires WiFi or Ethernet — USB-only printers don't scale to teams.

**Scanner/ADF:** Office document management often requires scanning. An ADF (automatic document feeder) lets you scan multi-page documents without manual page-by-page placement.
