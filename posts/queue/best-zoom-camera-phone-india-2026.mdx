## Optical vs Digital Zoom: Why It Matters

**Optical zoom** uses a physical secondary lens with longer focal length — no image quality loss at the specified zoom level. 3x optical zoom on a 50MP sensor captures as much detail at 3x as the main camera captures at 1x.

**Digital zoom** crops and enlarges the main sensor's output — visible quality degradation beyond 2–3x. All phones can do unlimited digital zoom; only phones with secondary telephoto lenses offer true optical zoom.

For concerts, sports, and wildlife in India, optical zoom is not a luxury — it's the difference between usable and unusable shots at distance.
