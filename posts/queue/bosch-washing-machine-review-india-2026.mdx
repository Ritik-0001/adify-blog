## Bosch's Key Technologies

### EcoSilence Drive
Bosch's brushless motor runs at 55dB — the quietest commercially available washing machine motor in India. No brushes means no wear, less maintenance, and longer life. The 10-year motor warranty reflects this confidence.

### SpeedPerfect
Reduces standard wash cycle duration by 65% without compromising cleaning quality. A 60-minute cotton cycle becomes 21 minutes. Useful for working families who need quick turnaround on work clothes.

### AllergyPlus
60°C wash program certified by Allergy UK — kills dust mites, pet dander, and bacteria. Unlike LG's steam approach, Bosch uses sustained high temperature water wash rather than steam.

### ActiveWater Plus
Automatically measures load weight and adjusts water volume precisely — uses up to 11 litres less per wash than fixed-water-level competitors.

**Price:** ₹26,490 | **Capacity:** 7kg | **Speed:** 1200 RPM

**Build quality:** Exceptional. The drum, door, and control panel are clearly higher quality than IFB or Samsung at this price point. The stainless steel drum has a wave pattern that reduces fabric friction. Door seal is premium silicone.

**Noise performance:** Tested at 55dB during wash and 70dB at maximum spin — significantly quieter than IFB Senator (59dB/73dB) and Samsung (60dB/74dB). **After-sales service:** Bosch service in India is good but concentrated in major metros. In tier-2 cities and smaller towns, service wait times can be longer than IFB. Always check Bosch service availability in your specific city before buying.
