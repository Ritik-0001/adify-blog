## Hard Water Test

In areas with TDS above 400 ppm (most of North India, Rajasthan, Gujarat, parts of Karnataka):
- **IFB Aqua Energie** conditions the water before it enters the drum — reduces limescale buildup on drum and heating element significantly
- **Bosch** uses AquaStop to prevent flooding but doesn't condition hard water — you need to use descaler tablets separately

**For hard water areas: IFB wins clearly.**

## Noise Level Reality
