## Small Family Washing Tips

**Frequency over quantity:** Washing 2–3 times per week with full loads is more efficient than daily half-loads. A 7kg machine used at 80–90% capacity delivers the best energy and water efficiency.

**Cold water washing:** Modern detergents (Ariel Matic, Surf Excel Matic) work effectively in cold water — saves electricity versus warm water cycles for everyday clothing.

**Quick wash programs:** For small families who wear clothes again quickly (kurtas, gym wear, office shirts), quick wash programs (20–30 minutes) are adequate for lightly soiled items.
