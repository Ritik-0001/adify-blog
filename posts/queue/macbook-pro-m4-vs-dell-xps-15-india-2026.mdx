## Performance

**MacBook Pro M4** uses Apple Silicon — unified memory architecture means the M4 chip's CPU, GPU, and Neural Engine share the same 16GB memory pool. Single-threaded performance leads the industry. Multi-threaded performance beats Intel Core Ultra 7 by 25–30% in sustained workloads.

**Dell XPS 15 with Core Ultra 7** has the RTX 4060 as a discrete GPU — this is genuinely faster than M4's integrated GPU for CUDA tasks, 3D rendering in Blender, and DirectX gaming. However, battery drops to 2–3hr under GPU load.

**Winner for professional work:** MacBook Pro M4. **Winner for GPU-intensive tasks:** Dell XPS 15.

---

## Display

**MacBook Pro M4 (14.2" Liquid Retina XDR):** 3024x1964, 1600 nit peak, P3 wide colour, ProMotion 120Hz. This is the best laptop display available.

**Dell XPS 15 (15.6" OLED, 60Hz):** 2880x1800 OLED. Excellent contrast and deep blacks, but only 60Hz — noticeably less smooth than MacBook's 120Hz. 15.6" is significantly larger screen real estate.

**Winner for quality:** MacBook Pro M4. **Winner for screen size:** Dell XPS 15.

---

## Battery Life

**MacBook Pro M4:** 18–22hr real-world mixed use. Lasts full workday without charger. Charges via MagSafe 3 (140W included) or USB-C.

**Dell XPS 15:** 8–10hr in light use, 2–3hr under GPU load. Requires 130W power adapter. India's variable power supply doesn't affect either — both handle 100-240V automatically.

**Winner:** MacBook Pro M4 by a large margin. For Indian users dealing with power cuts or long travel days, this matters significantly.

---

## Software Ecosystem

**macOS:** Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, Xcode, Apple Intelligence. Native Unix terminal — no WSL needed. Large library of Mac-native apps. No Windows gaming.

**Windows 11 (XPS 15):** Full Microsoft Office native, Adobe Creative Cloud, AutoCAD/SolidWorks, Windows-only enterprise software. Full PC gaming with RTX 4060. Better compatibility with Indian banking and government portals.

**Choose macOS if:** You're a developer, creative professional, or in the Apple ecosystem.
**Choose Windows if:** You need specific Windows software, gaming, or enterprise IT compatibility.

## Who Should Buy What

**Buy MacBook Pro M4 if:**
- Professional developer, video editor, or creative director
- Already in Apple ecosystem (iPhone, iPad)
- Battery life is critical (travel, power cuts)
- You use Final Cut Pro or Logic Pro
- 3–4 year investment horizon (Apple supports 6+ years)

**Buy Dell XPS 15 if:**
- Need dedicated GPU (3D rendering, gaming, CUDA ML training)
- Need larger screen (15.6" vs 14")
- Windows-specific software requirements
- Budget is ₹1,50,000 (not ₹2,50,000)
- Enterprise environment standardised on Windows
