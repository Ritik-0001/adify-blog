## What Indian Buyers Need in an Electric Kettle

**Stainless steel interior:** Critical for India — plastic interiors leach plastic taste and BPA into hot water. Stainless steel 304 (food-grade) is the minimum standard. Avoid cheap kettles with plastic inner bodies.

**1.5L to 1.8L capacity:** Standard for Indian household chai making — 1.5L serves 4–6 people per boil for tea or instant coffee.

**Voltage fluctuation tolerance:** India's 220–240V supply has frequent spikes — good kettles have automatic cutoff and heat-resistant materials to handle this.

**Auto shut-off and boil-dry protection:** Essential safety features — India's kitchen multitasking means kettles are often left unattended.

**Wattage:** 1,500W minimum for acceptable boil time. 2,000W for fast boiling.
