## Android Compatibility: What to Look For

**Health Connect:** Android 14 and newer phones support Google's Health Connect platform, which allows health data from your smartwatch to sync with any health app. Watches that support Health Connect (Samsung, Amazfit) offer much better data portability.

**Google Assistant:** Only watches running Wear OS (Samsung Galaxy Watch series) support Google Assistant natively. Budget Indian watches use their own voice assistants or Alexa.

**Notification Management:** All watches on this list support Android notifications, but the quality of notification management (being able to reply, dismiss, or take actions) varies. Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 has the most comprehensive notification interaction capabilities.

**App Ecosystem:** Wear OS watches can install third-party apps from the Play Store. Budget watches run proprietary firmware with no third-party app support — what you see in the box is what you get.
