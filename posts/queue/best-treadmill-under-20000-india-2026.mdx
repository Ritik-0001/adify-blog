## What to Know Before Buying Under ₹20,000

**Motor HP reality:** "Peak HP" and "continuous HP" are different. A 2 HP peak motor might deliver only 1 HP continuously. For sustained jogging sessions, continuous HP matters more. At under ₹20,000, expect 0.8–1.2 HP continuous.

**Running belt size:** 40cm x 100cm is the minimum for comfortable walking. Running requires 46cm x 130cm minimum — no treadmill under ₹20,000 meets this standard. These machines are walkers, not runners.

**Apartment considerations:** Treadmill vibration transmits through Indian concrete floors. Add a treadmill mat (₹1,500–₹2,000) to reduce noise for downstairs neighbours.

**Weight limit:** Check max user weight carefully. Most cheap treadmills cap at 90kg — test after 6 months of heavy use often reveals motor strain.
