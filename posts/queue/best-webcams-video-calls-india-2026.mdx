## Webcam Buying Guide

**1080p vs 4K:** For video calls (Zoom, Teams), 1080p is the practical limit — platforms compress video heavily. 4K is only meaningful for local recording. Buy 1080p unless you record locally.

**Lighting matters more than camera:** A ₹6,500 C920 with a ₹1,500 ring light outperforms a ₹20,000 webcam in a dark room. Buy the light first.

**Autofocus:** Essential if you move while on calls. All Logitech webcams here have reliable autofocus.

**Built-in mic:** Adequate for occasional calls. For daily calls, add a USB microphone (Blue Snowball, ₹5,000) or headset mic for noticeably better voice quality.

**Mounting:** Universal clip mounts on most monitors and laptops. Tripod thread on premium models for flexible positioning.
