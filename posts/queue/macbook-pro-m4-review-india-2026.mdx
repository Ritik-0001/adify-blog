## M4 Performance vs M3

Apple M4 improves over M3 in several areas:

**CPU:** 15–20% faster on multi-core. Python script execution, Xcode builds, and file compression are noticeably faster.

**GPU:** 30–35% faster in Metal-accelerated tasks. 3D rendering in Blender and DaVinci Resolve effects are faster. Games using Metal API (Resident Evil Village, Cyberpunk 2077 for Mac) run at higher frame rates.

**Neural Engine:** 16-core vs M3's 16-core, but 60% more operations per second. Powers Apple Intelligence features — Writing Tools, Clean Up in Photos, enhanced Siri.

**Memory bandwidth:** 120 GB/s (vs M3's 100 GB/s) — faster unified memory access for GPU tasks.

---

## Apple Intelligence — What It Means for India

Apple Intelligence (AI features) became more capable on M4 than M3:

**Writing Tools:** Rewrite, proofread, and summarise text in any app. Useful for Indian professionals writing English reports, emails, and client documents where language polishing is needed.

**Enhanced Siri:** Understands context better, can read screen content and answer questions about it.

**Image Playground:** Create images from text descriptions — useful for designers and content creators.

**Notification Priority:** Prioritises notifications from contacts you interact with most — reduces interruption during focused work.

Note: Many Apple Intelligence features require US English in 2026. India-specific Siri improvements are present but less comprehensive than US market.

## Battery Life — 24hr Rating

Apple's 24hr video playback rating translates to approximately **18–22hr of real-world mixed use** — the longest battery of any professional laptop available. For Indian professionals dealing with power cuts, this is the most power-resilient laptop possible.

## Display — Best in Class

The Liquid Retina XDR display at 3024x1964 remains the standard against which all laptop displays are judged. 1600 nit peak, 1000 nit sustained, P3 wide colour, ProMotion 120Hz. Colour-accurate enough for professional colour grading. Text at 254 PPI is visually flawless.

## MacBook Pro M4 vs M3 — Is the Upgrade Worth It?

**Upgrade if:**
- You're buying new (always buy the current generation)
- You run GPU-heavy workflows (M4 GPU is 30% faster)
- You want Apple Intelligence features
- You rely on ML model inference locally

**Wait or buy M3 refurbished if:**
- Budget is ₹2,49,900 vs ₹1,99,900 — M3 refurbished or M3 Pro is better value
- Your workloads are CPU-bound (M4's 15–20% CPU gain rarely translates to real-world difference)

₹2,49,900 is a significant investment. For professionals earning ₹30–₹50 lakh annually for whom a better tool translates to faster delivery — it's justified. For students or early-career professionals — the **MacBook Air M3 at ₹1,14,900 delivers 70–80% of the benefit at half the price**.
