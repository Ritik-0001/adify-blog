## Why Professional Straighteners Justify Higher Prices

**Consistent temperature maintenance.** Consumer straighteners (under ₹2000) lose temperature during styling and recover unevenly — causing multiple passes at high heat. Professional models (GHD, Philips ProCare) maintain exact temperature, reducing passes needed and cumulative heat exposure.

**Build quality for daily professional use.** Professional straighteners are designed for 4–8 hours daily salon use — their hinges, plates, and cords outlast consumer models used even twice daily at home.
