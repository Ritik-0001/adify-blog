## What Makes a Great Travel Headphone

**ANC effectiveness:** For flights, ANC is the critical feature. Test on consistent drone-frequency noise (engine hum) rather than speech — the former is where premium ANC truly excels.

**Battery life:** For international flights (8–16 hours), you need 20+ hours ANC-on battery. Both Sony XM5 and Bose QC45 deliver this. Budget options under ₹10,000 often fall short.

**Foldability:** The Sony XM5 no longer folds into a compact ball — it lays flat in the included case. The Bose QC45 folds into a smaller case. Consider your carry-on space before deciding.

**Multipoint Bluetooth:** For Indian business travellers who carry both an iPhone and MacBook, multipoint Bluetooth (simultaneously connecting two devices) is essential — only available on Sony XM5 and select premium models.
