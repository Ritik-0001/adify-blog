## When to Choose Single Door vs Double Door

**Choose single door if:**
- Budget is under ₹17,000
- Household is 1–3 people
- Kitchen space is limited
- Power cuts are frequent (single door retains cold better when door is kept closed)
- Manual defrosting every 4–6 weeks is acceptable

**Upgrade to double door if:**
- Family of 3+ people
- Budget is ₹20,000+
- No-defrost convenience is important
- Large freezer storage needed (ice cream, frozen food, meat)
