## Buying Guide: Home Theatre Systems in India 2026

**Soundbar vs 5.1 system:**
- Soundbar: Clean installation, no wiring — good for apartments
- 5.1 system (separate speakers): Authentic directional surround — better for dedicated TV rooms
- With rear speakers (HT-S40R): Best of both — wireless convenience + real surround

**Understanding channels:**
- 2.1: Soundbar + subwoofer — most apartment setups
- 3.1: Left + centre + right + subwoofer — better dialogue and stereo
- 5.1: 3 front + 2 rear + subwoofer — full surround
- 5.1.2 or 7.1.2: Dolby Atmos overhead channels

**HDMI ARC vs eARC:** HDMI eARC supports full Dolby Atmos bandwidth. HDMI ARC supports older Dolby formats. If your TV and soundbar both have eARC — use it.

**Indian apartment considerations:** Concrete walls reflect sound differently than drywall. Most modern soundbars include room calibration — let the auto-setup run for the best results in your specific room.
