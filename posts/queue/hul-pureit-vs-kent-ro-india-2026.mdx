## Which Purifier Wastes Less Water?

This is where HUL Pureit Eco genuinely wins. Traditional RO purifiers waste 3 litres for every 1 litre purified (25% recovery). The Pureit Eco Water Saver uses advanced membrane technology to achieve **60% water recovery** — meaning for every 10 litres processed, you get 6 litres of clean water and waste only 4.

Kent Grand Plus offers about 50% recovery — better than budget RO purifiers, but behind Pureit Eco.

**If water conservation is a priority** (high water cost area, water-scarce city, environmental concern), go with Pureit Eco.

---

## Which Purifier Tastes Better?

Kent wins here, and it matters more than you think. Kent Grand Plus uses a **TDS Controller** — it bypasses a percentage of filtered water back through the UF membrane, retaining natural minerals (calcium, magnesium) in the output water. The result is slightly mineral-rich, "sweet" tasting water rather than completely de-mineralized water.

HUL Pureit Eco removes almost all dissolved solids without re-adding minerals (output TDS: 5–20 ppm). Some users find ultra-pure water tastes flat. If you care about natural mineral taste, Kent Grand Plus delivers better.
