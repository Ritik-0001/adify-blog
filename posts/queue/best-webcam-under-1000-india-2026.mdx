## What to Expect Under ₹1000

**720p is the ceiling.** No budget webcam under ₹1000 delivers credible 1080p — specifications may claim it, but sensor quality and optics limit real output to 720p performance. Genuine 1080p webcams start at ₹1,500–1,700 (Logitech C270).

**Microphone quality.** Budget webcam microphones are mono, with limited background noise rejection. In quiet rooms, they're adequate. In noisy Indian homes or offices, a separate headset microphone provides significantly clearer voice for calls.

**Light sensitivity.** Budget webcams struggle in low light — a desk lamp pointed at your face (not the screen) dramatically improves video quality without any hardware upgrade.
