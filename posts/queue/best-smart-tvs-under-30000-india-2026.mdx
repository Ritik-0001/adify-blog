## Buying Guide: Smart TVs in 2026

**Size guide for rooms:**
- 10×12 ft room: 43" — comfortable viewing from 6-8 feet
- 12×15 ft room: 50–55" — optimal viewing from 8-10 feet
- Living room 15×18 ft+: 55–65"

**Smart OS comparison:**
| OS | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| Google TV | Best app selection, personalised | Slightly more complex |
| Tizen (Samsung) | Fastest, most polished | No third-party apps |
| Fire TV | Best for Prime Video | Fewer apps than Google |
| MIUI TV (Xiaomi) | Good content discovery | More ads in UI |

**4K vs Full HD:** At 43" and above viewing from 6+ feet — 4K makes a visible difference. Under ₹30,000, almost all new TVs are 4K now.
