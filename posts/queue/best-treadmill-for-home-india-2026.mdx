## Indian Apartment Considerations

**Floor vibration:** Treadmill use in Indian apartment buildings creates significant floor vibration that transmits to downstairs neighbours. Use a 6–8mm thick rubber treadmill mat (₹2,000–₹4,000) and avoid running sessions between 9 PM and 8 AM.

**Space when folded:** Most home treadmills fold to 80–100cm depth when stored. Measure your storage space before buying. Some models also fold vertically.

**Humidity and monsoon:** Indian humidity (70–90% in monsoon) affects motor longevity. Keep the treadmill dry, use a cover when not in use, and lubricate the running belt every 3 months.

**Power requirements:** Home treadmills above 2 HP require a dedicated power socket — standard Indian 15A socket is adequate for up to 3 HP peak. Above 3 HP, ensure your circuit can handle the load.
