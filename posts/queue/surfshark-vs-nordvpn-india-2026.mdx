## Price Difference

Over a 2-year plan, Surfshark costs ₹3,576 vs NordVPN's ₹4,776 — a difference of ₹1,200. Surfshark's unlimited device connections mean this saving is even more meaningful for families or friend groups sharing a subscription.

---

## Speed

NordVPN's NordLynx protocol (custom WireGuard implementation) consistently delivers slightly faster speeds than Surfshark's WireGuard implementation in Indian connection tests. The difference matters for 4K streaming (where every Mbps counts) but is negligible for general browsing and HD video.

---

## Security Features

**NordVPN extras:** Double VPN (routes through two servers), Onion over VPN, Dark Web Monitor, Threat Protection Pro (ad and malware blocking). NordVPN's security infrastructure is more extensive.

**Surfshark extras:** CleanWeb (ad/tracker blocking), Alert (data breach notifications), Search (private search engine). Both offer Kill Switch and split tunnelling.

## Which to Buy

**Choose Surfshark if:** You want to connect unlimited devices (family or shared use), price is the primary concern, or you're a student splitting costs.

**Choose NordVPN if:** You want the fastest speeds, largest server network, most comprehensive security features, or you're a business professional who needs reliable VPN performance.

Both have 30-day money-back guarantees — try the one you choose and switch if unsatisfied.
