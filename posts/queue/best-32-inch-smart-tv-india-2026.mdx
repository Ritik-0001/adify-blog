## 32 Inch: When Is It the Right Choice?

**Bedroom TVs.** 32 inches is ideal for bedrooms with 6–10 feet viewing distance. At this distance, HD Ready resolution is adequate and Full HD provides a slight improvement in sharpness.

**Kitchen and small rooms.** 32-inch TVs work well in kitchens and compact spaces where a 43-inch would dominate the visual field. Lighter weight (under 5kg) also makes wall mounting straightforward.

**Secondary TV.** If you already have a 43–55 inch living room TV and need a secondary bedroom TV under ₹15000, the Samsung 32T4340AK or Redmi A32 are the correct choices.
