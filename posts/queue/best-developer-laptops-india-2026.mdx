## Developer Laptop Buying Guide

**RAM:** 16GB minimum. 32GB if you run Docker with multiple services, Android emulators, or Kubernetes locally. Don't buy 8GB for development in 2026.

**Storage:** 512GB SSD minimum. Docker images, node_modules, and build caches eat storage fast. 1TB is better for long-term comfort.

**Keyboard:** You type on this for 8 hours a day. ThinkPad and MacBook keyboards are the best. Budget keyboards become frustrating over time.

**Display:** 14–16" at 1440p or better. OLED is excellent for eye comfort during long sessions. Anti-glare coating for office environments.

**Linux compatibility:** If you run Linux: ThinkPad or Dell XPS are the safest choices. ASUS VivoBook generally works. Avoid HP for Linux if possible.
