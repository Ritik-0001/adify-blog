## Buying Guide: Washing Machines in India 2026

**Which type to choose:**
| Type | Price Range | Water Use | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Semi-automatic | ₹6,000–₹12,000 | Low | Areas with irregular water supply |
| Top load fully auto | ₹12,000–₹25,000 | Medium | Standard Indian households |
| Front load | ₹25,000–₹55,000 | Low | Families who want best fabric care |

**Capacity guide:**
- 6kg: 1–2 people
- 7–7.5kg: 3–4 people
- 8–9kg: 4–5 people
- 10kg+: Large families or bulk washing

**Front load vs top load for India:**
- Front load: 30–40% less water, gentler on fabrics, more expensive, needs bending to load
- Top load: Easy loading, more affordable, higher water consumption — good match for Indian daily washing habits

**Energy star ratings:** In washing machines, a 5-star rating saves ₹1,200–₹1,800 per year vs 3-star. Over 10 years, a 5-star machine saves ₹12,000–₹18,000 in electricity alone.
