## boAt Airdopes 141 — Best Budget Alternative

The boAt Airdopes 141 consistently sits around ₹1,200–₹1,500 on Amazon, making it one of the best-value TWS earphones in India. If the Samsung stretches your budget, the Airdopes 141 offers a strong package at roughly half the price.

**Sound quality:** boAt's 10mm drivers deliver punchy bass and clear mids. The Airdopes 141 sounds significantly better than the Airdopes 131 despite the modest price difference.

**Environmental Noise Cancellation (ENC):** The Airdopes 141 does not have ANC but includes ENC for calls — which removes background noise from microphone pickup during calls. This is different from ANC (which reduces what you hear) but useful for call quality.

**Battery life:** 6 hours from earbuds, up to 42 hours total with the case. The case battery is exceptional for the price.

**BEAST Mode:** Low-latency gaming mode that reduces audio delay for mobile gaming.

**Who should buy it:** Budget-conscious buyers who want the boAt experience with better performance than the Airdopes 131.

The Realme Buds Air 3 Neo sits in the sweet spot of the ₹1,500–₹2,000 range and delivers a well-rounded package. It is not the best at any one thing, but it performs admirably across sound, battery, and call quality.
