## Buying Guide: Neckband Earphones in 2026

**Why neckbands over TWS under ₹2,000:**
- 40–60 hour battery (vs 5-8 hours for TWS)
- Magnetic snap (earbuds stick together when not in use — no losing them)
- Better call quality (larger mic surface area vs tiny TWS mics)
- No case to carry — just hangs around neck

**Bluetooth codecs explained:**
- SBC: Universal baseline — all earphones support this
- AAC: Better quality, supported by Apple and most Android
- aptX/aptX HD: Better quality for Qualcomm Snapdragon phones
- LDAC: Sony's best codec — highest quality, requires Sony Android phones or compatible Realme/OnePlus

**IP/IPX ratings:**
- IPX4: Sweat-resistant — safe for light exercise
- IPX5: Water-resistant — safe for heavy workouts and light rain
- IP57/IP67: Dust and water resistant — outdoor and sports use
