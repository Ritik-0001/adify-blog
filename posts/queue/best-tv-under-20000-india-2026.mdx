## Buying Guide: Budget Smart TVs in India 2026

**Size vs resolution:**
- 32": HD Ready (720p) is fine — at 32" the pixel density is acceptable
- 40–43": Full HD minimum — 4K at this size is noticeable but not essential
- 50"+: 4K recommended — 1080p looks soft at large sizes

**Smart TV OS comparison:**
| OS | Speed | App Selection | Voice Control |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tizen (Samsung) | Fastest | Good | Bixby + Alexa |
| WebOS (LG) | Fast | Good | Google + Alexa |
| Android TV | Medium | Best (Play Store) | Google Assistant |
| VIDAA (Hisense) | Fast | Limited | Alexa |

**RAM matters for smart TVs:** Budget TVs with 1GB RAM lag during streaming app use. Prioritise 2GB RAM for smooth smart TV performance.

**Internet requirement:** Smart TV features require Wi-Fi (2.4GHz or 5GHz). Streaming quality depends on your broadband speed — 15Mbps+ for 4K streaming.
