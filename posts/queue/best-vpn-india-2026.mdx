## Buying Guide: VPNs for India in 2026

**Why Indians use VPNs:**
1. **Streaming:** Netflix India has a smaller library than US/UK — a VPN unlocks the full catalogue
2. **Privacy on public WiFi:** Airport, café, and hotel WiFi are prime targets for data interception
3. **Work from home:** Accessing company internal networks securely
4. **Bypassing ISP throttling:** Some ISPs throttle streaming speeds — a VPN can bypass this

**India-specific considerations:**
- India's CERT-In 2022 directives require VPN providers to store user data for 5 years — VPN companies that comply are less privacy-friendly; reputable providers (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, ProtonVPN) removed their India physical servers and now offer virtual India IPs to avoid this requirement
- Virtual India servers still provide Indian IP addresses and good performance

**Speed tips:**
- Use WireGuard or NordLynx/Lightway protocol (faster than OpenVPN)
- Connect to the nearest server — Singapore, Malaysia are fast from India
- Avoid free VPNs for streaming — bandwidth is too limited

**What to avoid:**
- VPNs that log your activity
- Free VPNs that sell your browsing data
- VPNs with no published privacy audit
