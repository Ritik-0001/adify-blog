## VPN Legality in India

VPNs are legal in India for privacy and security purposes. In 2022, India's CERT-In required VPN providers to log user data — most reputable VPN providers (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Surfshark) moved servers out of India rather than comply, meaning they operate with no-logs policies from offshore servers. Check the provider's current India server status before subscribing.

## What to Look For in a VPN for India

**Speed on Indian connections.** WireGuard-based protocols (NordLynx, Lightway) perform best on Indian broadband. Test with the free trial before committing to annual plans.

**India servers vs global servers.** India-based servers reduce latency for Indian content. Global servers are needed for geo-restricted content (Netflix US, BBC iPlayer).

**No-logs policy.** Third-party audited no-logs policies (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, Surfshark) provide verifiable privacy assurance. Unaudited claims should be treated with scepticism.
