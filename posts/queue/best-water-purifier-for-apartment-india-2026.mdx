## What Apartments Need vs What Houses Need

An independent house or villa has a large kitchen, counter space, and often higher TDS borewell water — so a big floor-standing RO with 15L tank makes sense.

Apartments are different:
- **Limited counter/wall space** — compact form factor essential
- **Municipal water** — usually TDS 150–500 ppm (lower TDS than borewell)
- **Smaller family size** — 2-4 people, 8–10 litres/day usage
- **No drain plumbing complications** — standard wall-mount installation works

This means you need a **compact wall-mount RO purifier, not a floor-standing model**, and you don't need to pay for heavy-duty borewell-spec hardware.

## Do Apartments with Municipal Water Need RO?

**Short answer:** Often yes, even for municipal water.

Municipal water in Indian metros is treated and has lower TDS (150–400 ppm typically), but it contains:
- **Chlorine** (disinfectant residue) — needs carbon filter
- **Chloramines** — carcinogenic over long-term consumption
- **Heavy metals** from old pipes (lead, copper leaching)
- **Bacterial contamination** during distribution (pipe leaks)

A UV purifier handles bacteria, but not dissolved chemicals and heavy metals. An RO handles both. For Indian apartments on municipal supply, an **RO + UV combo is still recommended** — the health protection is worth the ₹10,000–₹15,000 investment.

**Exception:** If you live in a building with stainless steel overhead tanks, modern PVC plumbing, and your TDS is consistently under 200 ppm, a UV purifier alone (₹4,000–₹6,000) may be sufficient. Test your TDS first.

## Installation Tips for Apartments

1. **Check your kitchen wall:** RO purifiers need a standard wall plug (16A socket) and nearby drain pipe connection. Most kitchens have both.
2. **Drain connection:** The reject water outlet connects to your kitchen drain pipe — no separate drainage needed.
3. **Installation time:** Standard RO installation takes 45–60 minutes. Most brands include this free of charge.
4. **Inlet connection:** Taps into your main kitchen tap water line — no plumber needed beyond the brand's technician.
