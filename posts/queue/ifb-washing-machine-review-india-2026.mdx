## IFB's Key Technologies Tested

### Aqua Energie — Does It Work?

Aqua Energie is IFB's hard water conditioning system — a flow-through cartridge that breaks down calcium and magnesium bicarbonates in water before it enters the drum. In areas with TDS above 400 ppm, this prevents:
- Limescale buildup on the heating element (extends heater life 2–3x)
- Scale deposits on drum bearings (extends bearing life)
- Detergent residue on fabrics from hard water interference

**Real-world verdict:** Users in Delhi, Jaipur, Ahmedabad, and Bengaluru (all hard water cities) consistently report better long-term drum and bearing condition in IFB machines vs competitors. The Aqua Energie cartridge needs replacement every 3 months (₹300–₹500). Worth it.

---

### Built-In Voltage Stabiliser

IFB machines operate on 180–270V without an external stabiliser — most competitors need 200–240V. In tier-2 cities where supply voltage dips to 185–190V frequently, this protects the machine and control board.

**Verdict:** A meaningful India-specific feature that Samsung and Bosch don't include. Important for non-metro buyers.

---

### Cradle Wash (Delicate Mode)

IFB's Cradle Wash rocks clothes in the drum rather than tumbling — reduces mechanical friction on delicate fabrics by 70%. Tested with silk sarees, chiffon dupattas, and cotton-silk blends — minimal shrinkage and colour fade vs standard delicate cycles on competitor machines.
