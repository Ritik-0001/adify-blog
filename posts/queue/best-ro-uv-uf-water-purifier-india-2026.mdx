## What Does RO+UV+UF Mean and Why Do You Need All Three?

Each purification technology targets different types of contamination. Here is why all three working together matters:

### RO (Reverse Osmosis)
RO uses a semi-permeable membrane (typically 0.0001 micron pores) to remove:
- Dissolved salts and TDS
- Heavy metals (lead, arsenic, fluoride, chromium)
- Pesticides and herbicides
- Some viruses and all bacteria
- Hardness-causing minerals (calcium, magnesium excess)

RO alone at the membrane stage kills most biological threats, but it cannot guarantee 100% virus removal in all conditions — hence the need for UV.

### UV (Ultraviolet)
UV light at 254 nm wavelength destroys the DNA/RNA of microorganisms, preventing them from reproducing:
- Kills bacteria (100% with correct exposure)
- Kills viruses (100% with correct exposure)
- Kills cysts like Giardia and Cryptosporidium (95–99%)

UV does NOT remove dead organism bodies from the water — they remain as harmless particles, but UF handles this.

### UF (Ultrafiltration)
UF operates at 0.01–0.1 micron, removing:
- Dead bacteria and virus bodies after UV treatment
- Remaining suspended particles
- Some cysts (but not dissolved minerals)
- Acts as a safety net if UV lamp weakens

UF works without electricity and continues to filter even during power outages, making it a vital backup stage.

### Why All Three Together?
With RO + UV + UF, you get:
1. RO removes dissolved chemical and mineral contamination
2. UV eliminates biological contamination
3. UF catches any remaining physical particles and provides safety redundancy

This combination covers all major threat categories in Indian drinking water.

## Do You Need RO+UV+UF or Just RO+UV?

If your water is municipal supply with moderate biological risk:
- **RO+UV** is sufficient for most purposes
- The UF stage adds redundancy and removes any residual turbidity

If your water is:
- From a borewell or tanker
- Has seasonal quality variations
- Has any history of contamination events in your area
- You have young children, elderly, or immunocompromised individuals

Then **RO+UV+UF** is strongly recommended. The UF stage costs very little to add (it is essentially built into most modern purifiers at this price point) and provides meaningful protection redundancy.
