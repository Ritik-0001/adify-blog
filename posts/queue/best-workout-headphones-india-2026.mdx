## Workout Headphone Buying Guide

**IP rating explained:** IPX4 = splash-resistant (light sweat). IPX5/6 = strong water jets (heavy sweat, rain). IP67/IP68 = temporary submersion. For most gym users, IPX5 is sufficient. For outdoor runners in Indian monsoon conditions, IP67+ provides peace of mind.

**TWS vs neckband for workouts:** TWS earbuds are lighter and more comfortable for most activities. Neckbands have less risk of losing individual earbuds and often have longer battery life. For heavy lifting, neckbands are more secure.

**Ear tip fit matters:** A proper seal improves both sound quality and passive noise isolation during workouts. Most TWS earbuds include small, medium, and large tips — experiment to find the seal that stays secure during head movement.
