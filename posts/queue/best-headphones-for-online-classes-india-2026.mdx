## What Matters for Online Class Headphones

**Microphone clarity over sound quality.** For online classes, your classmates and teachers hear you through the mic — mic quality matters as much as speaker quality. Test mic quality in app settings before a live class.

**Long-wear comfort.** After 3 hours, poorly designed headphones cause ear pressure and headband soreness. Lightweight (under 200g) and memory foam ear cushions are the two features that correlate most with all-day comfort.

**ANC for Indian homes.** Indian family homes rarely have quiet study rooms. ANC headphones create a personal quiet zone that meaningfully improves concentration. The JBL Tune 670NC and Sony WH-CH720N provide the best ANC-to-price ratio for students.
