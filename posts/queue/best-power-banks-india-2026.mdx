## Buying Guide: Power Banks in India 2026

**Capacity guide:**
- 10,000mAh: 2 full phone charges — daily carry, shirt/jeans pocket
- 20,000mAh: 4–5 charges — travel, weekend trips
- 25,000mAh+: Laptop-level — business travel and remote work

**Airline rules:** Power banks above 27,000mAh (100Wh) are not allowed on flights. All models above are airline-approved.

**Wattage matters more than capacity:**
- 10W (5V/2A): Old standard — charges phones slowly (3–4 hours per charge)
- 18–22.5W: Fast charging — charges most phones in 1–1.5 hours
- 45–65W: Laptop-capable — single bank for phone + laptop
- 100W+: Full laptop power — even gaming laptops

**PD (Power Delivery) vs proprietary fast charging:**
USB-C Power Delivery works with all devices (phones, tablets, laptops). Proprietary protocols (Realme DART, OnePlus WARP, Samsung Adaptive Fast Charge) only work fast with matching brand devices. If you use multiple brands, choose PD.

**BIS certification:** Always verify BIS mark on Indian power banks — uncertified banks have caused fires. All brands listed above are BIS certified.
