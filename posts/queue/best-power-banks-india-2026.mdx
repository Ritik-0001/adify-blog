## Buying Guide: Power Banks in 2026

**Capacity guide:**
- 5,000–10,000mAh → 1–2 full phone charges; slim and pocketable
- 20,000mAh → 4–5 phone charges; best for multi-day travel
- 24,000–27,000mAh → laptop charging territory; heavier

**Fast charging explained:**
- 18W–33W: Fast charges most phones; standard for this segment
- 45W–65W: Fast charges laptops (older/thin models) and tablets
- 100W+: Full laptop charging support for MacBooks and Windows ultrabooks

**What to look for:**
- USB-C PD (Power Delivery) — universal fast charging standard
- Input wattage: higher means faster recharging of the power bank itself
- Number of ports: 2 USB-A + 1 USB-C covers most users
- Airline restrictions: 100Wh max for carry-on (~27,000mAh at 3.7V)
