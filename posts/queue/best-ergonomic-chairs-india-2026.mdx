## Buying Guide: Ergonomic Chairs in 2026

**What actually matters for back health:**
1. **Adjustable lumbar support** — the single most important feature; a chair without it will cause lower back pain
2. **Seat height adjustment** — your feet should rest flat on the floor with thighs parallel to the ground
3. **Armrests** — adjustable armrests prevent shoulder and neck strain from hovering your arms

**Mesh vs foam/leather:**
- Mesh: Breathable, better for India's climate, conforms to your back shape
- Foam/leather: More cushioned initially but compresses over time; hot in summer

**Gaming chairs vs office chairs:**
Gaming chairs look dramatic but most lack proper lumbar support compared to office ergonomic chairs at the same price. For pure ergonomics, office chairs like Green Soul Monster Ultimate beat gaming chairs under ₹20,000.

**Assembly note:** Most chairs in India arrive in parts — assembly takes 30–60 minutes. Keep the Allen key from the box.
