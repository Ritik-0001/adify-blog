## Buying Guide: Wireless Chargers in India 2026

**Wireless charging standards:**
| Standard | Max Speed | Compatibility | Magnetic? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Qi | 10–15W | Universal | No |
| Qi2 | 15W | iPhone 12+, some Android | Yes |
| MagSafe | 15W | iPhone 12+ only | Yes |
| Samsung Fast Wireless | 15W | Galaxy S/Note only | No |
| OnePlus AIRVOOC | 50W | OnePlus only | No |

**Does your phone support fast wireless?**
Check your phone's spec sheet for wireless charging wattage. Most phones charge at 5–10W standard Qi. Fast wireless (15W+) requires both phone and charger to support the same protocol.

**Wireless charging efficiency:** Wireless charging is 75–85% efficient vs wired 95%+. A 15W wireless charger uses ~18W from the wall. Slightly higher electricity consumption than wired — negligible for daily use.

**Best practices:** Remove thick phone cases (above 3mm) for optimal wireless charging contact. Metal cases prevent wireless charging entirely.
