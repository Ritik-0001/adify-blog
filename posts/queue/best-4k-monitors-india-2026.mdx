## Buying Guide: 4K Monitors in India 2026

**Resolution: Do you need 4K?**
At 27" and above, 4K is a significant upgrade — text is razor-sharp, UI elements are 2× crisper than 1080p. For design work, 4K is now the standard. For gaming, you need a powerful GPU (RTX 4070+) to drive 4K at playable frame rates.

**USB-C PD wattage guide:**
- 45W: Charges 13" MacBook Air, thin ultrabooks
- 60W: Charges most 14" laptops and some 15" models
- 90W+: Required for 15"–16" MacBook Pro and Dell XPS 15 at full speed

**Colour accuracy for creative work:**
- sRGB 99–100%: Required for web/digital design, photo editing
- DCI-P3 95%+: Required for video editing (HDR and cinema colour space)
- Delta E&lt;2: Factory calibration for accurate colour reproduction

**IPS vs OLED at this price range:**
- IPS Black (Dell, LG): Good contrast, no burn-in risk, 3000:1 contrast
- Standard IPS: 1000:1 contrast — adequate for most work
- OLED 4K monitors: Start at ₹80,000+ in India — out of this comparison range
