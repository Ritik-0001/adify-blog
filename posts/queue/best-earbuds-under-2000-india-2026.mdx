## Under ₹2000: What Separates Good from Great

**Call quality improvement.** The ₹1500–2000 range is where earbuds start including ENC (Environmental Noise Cancellation) for calls — using microphone algorithms to filter background noise from your voice. This makes a meaningful difference for office and outdoor calls. The boAt Airdopes 181 and Realme Buds Air 5 both include effective ENC.

**Low-latency modes.** Gaming modes (45–65ms) significantly reduce audio-video sync lag in videos and mobile games. Without it, you'll notice a slight delay between on-screen action and audio. BEAST Mode on boAt and Game Mode on Realme are worth using for media consumption.

**Codec support.** Most earbuds in this range support SBC and AAC. The Realme Buds Air 5 additionally supports LDAC for hi-res audio — notable at this price, though you need an LDAC-compatible source device to benefit.
