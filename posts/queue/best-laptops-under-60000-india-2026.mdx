## Buying Guide: Laptops Under ₹60,000 in India 2026

**AMD Ryzen 7 vs Intel Core i7 at this price:**
- Ryzen 7 7730U: Better multi-core performance, better integrated graphics, higher battery life
- Intel Core i7-1355U: Better single-core performance, Thunderbolt 4, better Windows compatibility
Both are capable — choose based on use case and ecosystem preferences.

**Display priority:**
- OLED (ASUS VivoBook, Lenovo Slim 5, Acer Swift Go): Best colours, deepest blacks — for content and media
- IPS 120–144Hz (HP Pavilion, Dell): Better for fast content and Windows fluidity
- IPS 60Hz: Adequate, avoid if alternatives available at same price

**RAM and storage at ₹55K:** Minimum 16GB RAM and 512GB SSD — don't compromise on either at this price. Avoid 8GB RAM laptops unless they have an accessible upgrade slot.

**For coding:** Core i7 + 16GB RAM + SSD + good keyboard — all models above qualify. Developer experience is similar across all picks; keyboard feel becomes the differentiator.
