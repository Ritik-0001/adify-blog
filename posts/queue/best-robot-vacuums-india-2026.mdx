## Buying Guide: Robot Vacuums for India

**LiDAR vs Random Navigation:**
- LiDAR: Maps your home precisely, cleans in efficient rows, learns furniture placement. Worth the premium.
- Random bounce: Covers area eventually but re-cleans the same spots and misses corners consistently.

**Indian home specifics:**
- **Long hair:** Standard brush rolls tangle — choose dual rubber rollers (Roborock S8, Q5 Pro)
- **Marble/tile floors:** Most robot vacuums handle this well — suction 2,000 Pa+ is enough
- **Doorway thresholds:** Indian homes often have 1-2cm raised door sills — choose models with good climb capacity (15-20mm)
- **Area rugs:** Boosted carpet mode (Roborock) prevents the vacuum from dragging the rug

**Suction guide (Pa):**
- 2,000 Pa: Tiles, marble, light dust — adequate for basic use
- 4,000 Pa: Handles hair, pet dander, and door threshold climbing
- 5,500+ Pa: Deep cleaning, rugs, and pet homes
