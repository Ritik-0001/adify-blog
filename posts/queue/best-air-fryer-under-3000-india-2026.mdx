## What Do You Actually Get for Under ₹3,000?

Air fryers under ₹3,000 are a real category in India — and for the right user, they are genuinely worth buying. Here is an honest picture of what this price tier offers.

**What you get:**
- 3–3.5L basket capacity (enough for 2 people, or snacks for 3)
- 1200–1400W heating element (adequate for most air frying tasks)
- Mechanical dial controls (timer + temperature knob)
- Basic preset temperatures (typically 80°C to 200°C range)
- Round basket design

**What you do not get:**
- Digital display or touchscreen controls
- Preset cooking programs (you set time and temperature manually)
- Preheat function
- Shake reminders
- App connectivity
- Large capacity for family cooking

These are honest trade-offs, not dealbreakers — a ₹2,799 air fryer fries samosas and chips just as effectively as a ₹5,000 one. The premium models add convenience features, not fundamental cooking capability.
