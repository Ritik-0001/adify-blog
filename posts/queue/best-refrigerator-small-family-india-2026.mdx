## Indian Family-Specific Features to Prioritise

**Curd Maestro / Fermentation zone:** Indian families regularly make dahi (curd), buttermilk, and fermented foods at home — a dedicated fermentation compartment maintains 35–40°C ideal for setting curd.

**Vegetable crisper humidity:** Indian cooking uses large quantities of fresh vegetables daily. High-humidity crispers keep coriander, mint, ladies finger, and raw tomatoes fresh for 7–10 days vs 3–5 days in standard crispers.

**Power-cut retention:** Essential for Indian conditions. Samsung's Cool Pack (12hr) is the best-in-class. For areas with longer power cuts, consider a smaller UPS for the refrigerator (₹3,000–₹5,000).
