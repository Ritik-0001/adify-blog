## Mounting for Bike Vlogging

**Helmet mount (front):** Most common — captures the rider's POV including the road and handlebars. Requires checking local regulations (some states don't allow front-mounted helmet cameras on road legal vehicles).

**Handlebar mount:** Steady footage but captures only road ahead, not rider reaction. Combined with handlebar dampener (₹500) for smooth footage on Indian roads.

**Chest mount:** Captures rider body position and handlebars in frame — preferred for motorcycle review content.
