## 8Z Pellicle Mesh — Why It Matters for Indian Climate

The Aeron's 8Z Pellicle mesh is a breathable suspension material covering the seat and back. Unlike foam seats, mesh allows airflow — preventing the sweaty, uncomfortable seat experience that plagues Indian professionals in 30–40°C temperatures.

In Indian summers without AC, mesh chairs are significantly more comfortable than foam-padded alternatives. The Aeron's mesh is engineered in 8 zones of varying firmness — firmer in areas needing support, softer in pressure-sensitive zones.

---

## PostureFit SL Lumbar Support

PostureFit SL provides dual-zone support — sacrum (lower) and lumbar (upper). This supports the natural S-curve of the spine, unlike most office chairs that only support the lumbar curve while letting the sacrum tilt backward into a slouch.

For Indian professionals spending 8–12hr at desks, this spinal support distinction matters significantly for long-term back health. Many Aeron users report elimination of chronic lower back pain that conventional office chairs didn't resolve.

---

## 4D Armrests

Adjustable in 4 directions: height, width, depth, and pivot angle. The armrests at typing height support forearms during extended typing — reducing shoulder and neck strain. Critical for Indian software engineers and data entry professionals.
