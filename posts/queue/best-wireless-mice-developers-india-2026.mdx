## Choosing the Right Mouse

**Grip Style:** Palm grip (large mice like MX Master) reduces fatigue in long sessions. Claw grip (smaller, lighter mice) allows more precision. Fingertip grip (very light mice) maximises speed.

**DPI:** For development work, 800–1600 DPI is ideal. Higher DPI is for gaming/precision design work. Most productivity mice let you adjust.

**Scroll Wheel:** If you read long documents, scroll through large codebases, or work in spreadsheets: MagSpeed (Logitech) is worth the premium. Standard scroll wheels are fine for light use.

**Wrist Health:** Vertical mice prevent RSI. If you have any wrist discomfort, try the MX Vertical before symptoms worsen.
