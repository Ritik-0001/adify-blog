## Buying Tips for Under ₹50,000

**Prioritise 16GB RAM** — at ₹50,000 there's no excuse for 8GB. More RAM means smoother multitasking, especially with Chrome, Office, and Zoom open simultaneously. **H-series vs U-series processors:** H-series (7535HS, i5-12500H) are faster but drain battery faster. U-series (7530U) are better for battery life. Choose based on whether you're plugged in most of the day.
