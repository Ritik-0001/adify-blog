## Buying Guide: ANC Earbuds in India 2026

**ANC quality spectrum in India:**
1. Sony WF-1000XM5 — best overall
2. Bose QuietComfort Ultra — best silence
3. Apple AirPods Pro 2 — best for iOS
4. Nothing Ear (2) — best under ₹10K
5. OnePlus Buds Pro 2 — best under ₹7K

**ANC dB ratings — reality check:** Claimed dB figures are measured in ideal lab conditions. Real-world ANC performance in Indian environments (metro noise, construction, traffic) is typically 50–70% of claimed figures. Sony and Bose consistently deliver the highest real-world performance.

**In-ear seal matters more than ANC spec:** ANC + passive isolation together beats ANC alone. If earbuds fit poorly (common with smaller ear canals), no ANC will compensate. Check if the brand offers multiple ear tip sizes.

**Codec quality:** LDAC (Sony) > LHDC (Nothing, OnePlus) > AAC (Apple) > SBC (default). For Android + audiophile: LDAC. For iPhone: AAC is the best available.
