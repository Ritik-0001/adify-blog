## Buying Guide: Bluetooth Speakers Under ₹3,000

**Wattage isn't everything:** A 20W boAt and a 12W Sony don't sound the same — driver quality, enclosure design, and tuning matter more than raw wattage.

**IP Ratings:**
- IPX5: Splash and rain resistant — handles most outdoor use
- IPX7/IP67: Fully submersible to 1m for 30 minutes — pool/beach safe

**Battery tips:**
- 5–8 hours: Casual home use
- 12–16 hours: Day trips and travel
- 20–24 hours: Camping and multi-day use

**What to skip under ₹3,000:**
- True 360° room-filling sound — needs ₹5,000+ speakers
- High-fidelity audiophile quality — get JBL Charge 5 or UE Boom 3 at ₹7,000+
