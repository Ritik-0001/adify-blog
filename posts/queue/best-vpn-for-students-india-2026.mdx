## College WiFi and VPN

Many Indian college and university networks block or throttle certain traffic types. VPNs can bypass these restrictions by encrypting traffic that the network cannot inspect. However, use VPNs for legitimate privacy purposes — bypassing academic institution content policies may violate terms of service. Check your institution's IT policies before use.
