## Bedroom TV Buying Guide

**Size selection.** 32 inches is ideal for bedrooms with 5–10 feet viewing distance. 40 inches works in larger master bedrooms (12+ feet depth). Avoid 43+ inches in standard Indian bedrooms — the screen dominates the room visually and is uncomfortably large at close distances.

**Blue light filter.** Streaming content before sleep is common in Indian bedrooms. Samsung, LG, and Redmi TVs all include Eye Care or blue light reduction modes — enable these for late-night use to reduce sleep disruption.

**Sound without soundbar.** In bedrooms, wall-mounted TVs often benefit from elevated speaker output. Check the watt rating — 20W+ is recommended for adequate bedroom audio without a separate speaker. The LG 32LM565BPTC and Samsung 32T4340AK both deliver good bedroom audio.
