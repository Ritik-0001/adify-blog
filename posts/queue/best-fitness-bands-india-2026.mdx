## Buying Guide: Fitness Bands in 2026

**Fitness band vs smartwatch:**
- Fitness band: Longer battery (10-16 days), lighter, more comfortable 24/7 wear, cheaper
- Smartwatch: Apps, Bluetooth calling, bigger screen, more features — but 1-2 day battery

**Health metrics explained:**
- Heart rate 24/7: Resting heart rate trends (lower = better fitness)
- SpO2: Blood oxygen — important for high-altitude travel and detecting breathing issues
- HRV: Heart rate variability — best predictor of recovery and stress (on premium bands)
- Skin temperature: Detects illness early — temperature deviation from baseline = possible fever

**GPS types:**
- GPS-connected (via phone): Most bands — tracks route using phone's GPS
- Built-in GPS: Mi Band 8 Pro — tracks accurately without phone
