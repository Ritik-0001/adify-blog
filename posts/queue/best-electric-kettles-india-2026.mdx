## Buying Guide: Electric Kettles in India 2026

**Plastic vs stainless steel interior:**
Cheap kettles have plastic interiors — plastic-taste water is real and measurable. Always choose stainless steel or glass interiors. Even budget models (Prestige PKGSS) offer this.

**Wattage and speed:**
- 1500W: Boils 1L in ~4–5 minutes
- 2200–2400W: Boils 1L in ~2.5–3 minutes — meaningful time savings for daily use

**Capacity guide:**
- 1–1.5L: 1–2 people
- 1.7–2L: 3–5 people or when you fill it fully

**Variable temperature — why it matters for chai:**
Indian masala chai brewed at 100°C extracts the most flavour from spices. However, green tea and delicate teas become bitter at high temperatures. If you drink both, variable temperature is worth the ₹1,500 premium.

**Limescale in Indian hard water:** Hard water (North India particularly) builds limescale fast. Descale monthly with diluted white vinegar (10 minutes of soaking, rinse well). Glass kettles show buildup earliest — helpful reminder.
