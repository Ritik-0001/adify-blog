## What Unlocks at the ₹60,000 Price Point?

Crossing from ₹50,000 to ₹60,000 brings real, tangible upgrades that you will notice daily:

- **Better displays:** 2.8K OLED or 2K IPS panels become available
- **Higher-wattage CPUs:** Ryzen 7 7730U or Ryzen 5 7535HS (H-series)
- **16GB RAM** as a more common configuration
- **Fingerprint readers and IR cameras** for Windows Hello
- **Better thermal systems** — dual fans and vapour chambers on some models
- **More metal in the build** — aluminium lids and top decks

If you spend significant hours at your laptop daily, this jump in budget pays dividends in both productivity and comfort.
