## Switch Guide for Developers

**Linear (Red/Yellow):** Smooth, no bump, quiet. Best for fast typists who bottom out. Reduces finger fatigue in long sessions.

**Tactile (Brown/Banana):** Small bump at actuation point. Typing feedback without loud click. Most popular for programmers — you feel each keypress without noise complaints.

**Clicky (Blue/Green):** Loud click + tactile bump. Satisfying but disruptive in shared spaces. Only viable for home offices.

**Recommendation for most developers:** Start with Gateron Brown or Keychron K Pro Brown — tactile feedback helps touch-typing accuracy without being loud.
