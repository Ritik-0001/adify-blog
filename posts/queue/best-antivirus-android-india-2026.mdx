## Do Android Phones Need Antivirus?

**Google Play Protect** (built into all Android phones with Google Play Store) provides real-time scanning of installed apps and downloads. For users who: (1) only install apps from Google Play Store, (2) don't sideload APK files, (3) are careful about permissions — Play Protect is adequate.

**Install a paid antivirus if:** You regularly sideload APK files from WhatsApp or third-party sites, use your phone for mobile banking on public WiFi, or share your phone with family members who have less digital security awareness.

**India-specific risk:** Fake bank apps, UPI fraud apps, and WhatsApp-spread malicious APKs are India's primary mobile threat vectors. A paid antivirus with real-time protection catches these before they install.
