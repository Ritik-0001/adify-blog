## Plate Quality

**Philips HP8316 plates** are infused with keratin particles embedded in the ceramic — not just surface coating. This produces smoother hair glide, consistent heat distribution, and longer plate life before coating wear. The keratin infusion adds a conditioning effect during straightening.

**Vega plates** use standard ceramic-coated aluminium. Adequate for straight hair results but lacks the conditioning properties of Philips. Coating lifespan with daily use is slightly shorter than Philips.

---

## Heat Distribution

**Philips's SilkProtect** monitors temperature 30 times per second and adjusts to prevent hot spots — eliminating the localised overheating that causes visible damage over time. **Vega** uses fixed temperature settings without active monitoring. On fine or already-damaged Indian hair, Philips's active monitoring meaningfully reduces cumulative damage.
