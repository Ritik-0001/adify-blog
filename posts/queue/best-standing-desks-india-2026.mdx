## Buying Guide: Standing Desks in 2026

**Electric vs manual crank:**
- Electric: One-button height change — you'll actually use it daily
- Manual crank: Cheaper but tedious — most users stop alternating sit/stand after a week

**Single motor vs dual motor:**
- Single motor: Adequate for most setups under 25kg
- Dual motor: More stable under load, higher weight capacity — worth it for multi-monitor setups

**Desk size guide:**
- Single monitor + laptop: 120×60cm minimum
- Dual monitor setup: 150×70cm or larger
- Triple monitor / recording studio: 180×80cm+

**Standing tips for health benefit:**
- Don't stand all day — alternate 30-45 min standing with 1-2 hours sitting
- Get an anti-fatigue mat (₹1,500-3,000) — standing on hard floors causes leg fatigue
- Eye level: Monitor at eye level standing, then lower when sitting — built-in presets make this automatic
