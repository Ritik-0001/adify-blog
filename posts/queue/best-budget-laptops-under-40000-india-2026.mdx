## What You Should Expect Under ₹40,000 in 2026

**Must-have features (available in this price):**
- SSD storage (512GB minimum) — never buy an HDD laptop in 2026
- 8GB RAM — minimum viable; 16GB is better but rare under ₹40,000
- AMD Ryzen 5 or Intel Core i5 — both handle everyday tasks well
- Wi-Fi 6 — increasingly standard at this price

**What you won't get:**
- Dedicated GPU (discrete graphics for gaming)
- 4K or OLED display
- Thunderbolt 4
- All-metal build (mostly plastic at this tier)
- More than 8 hours battery

**Upgrade priority:** If you can stretch to ₹45,000–₹50,000, the ASUS VivoBook 15 OLED or Lenovo ThinkPad E14 Gen 5 significantly upgrade the experience.
