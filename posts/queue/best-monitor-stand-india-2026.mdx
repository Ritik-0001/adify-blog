Monitor stands for India serve two purposes: ergonomic positioning (eye-level screen reduces neck strain — India's leading WFH health complaint) and desk space optimisation (Indian home offices are typically small, requiring under-monitor space for keyboard and accessories). Here are the best monitor stands for Indian setups in 2026.

---

## Monitor Stand Types for India

**Riser stands:** Fixed-height platforms that elevate monitor 10–15cm. Cheapest option (₹800–₹2,000). No adjustment, but creates under-monitor storage.

**Monitor arms (single/dual):** Articulating arms clamped to desk — full height, tilt, and swivel adjustment. Best ergonomics. Requires VESA mount on monitor (100×100mm standard — check monitor spec before buying).

**Height-adjustable stands:** Replacement for monitor's included stand — adjusts height without requiring desk clamp. Middle ground between riser and arm.
