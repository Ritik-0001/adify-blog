## Curl Types for Indian Hair

**19mm barrel (Philips BHB862, Vega VHCH-01):** Tight curls — suitable for short and medium length hair. Creates defined ringlets.

**25mm barrel:** Medium curls — the most flattering curl size for shoulder-length Indian hair. Creates beachy waves.

**32mm barrel:** Loose waves — best for long Indian hair. Creates soft, natural-looking waves rather than tight curls.

Indian hair typically holds curls for 4–8 hours without products. Using a light-hold hairspray after curling extends durability to 12+ hours.
