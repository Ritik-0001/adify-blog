## Buying Guide: Treadmills for India 2026

**Motor power — what you actually need:**
- Walking (4–6 km/h): 1.5–2HP peak is sufficient
- Jogging (6–10 km/h): 2.5–3HP peak minimum
- Running (10–14 km/h): 3HP+ peak or 2HP+ continuous

**Continuous vs Peak HP:** Always compare continuous HP when evaluating treadmills. A "4HP peak" machine may only sustain 1.5HP — the motor that actually matters for your workout.

**Weight capacity:** Choose a treadmill rated 20kg above your body weight — running creates 2–3× your body weight in impact force.

**India-specific considerations:**
- Voltage fluctuations can damage treadmill motors — consider a voltage stabiliser (₹2,000–₹3,000)
- Service availability: PowerMax > Cockatoo > Durafit for pan-India service
- Monsoon storage: Store in a dry room — belt mould is a common issue in India's humid climate
