## What to Expect Under ₹5000

**Image quality vs GoPro.** Action cameras under ₹5000 produce footage that looks good on YouTube at 1080p and adequate at 4K. Side-by-side comparison with GoPro HERO13 (₹33,000) reveals the difference — but for solo content viewed on mobile screens, the budget camera is sufficient.

**Stabilisation.** Budget EIS (Electronic Image Stabilisation) reduces rolling shutter but doesn't match GoPro's HyperSmooth or DJI RockSteady. For bike-mounted shooting on Indian roads, expect some vibration shake in the footage. A handlebar dampening mount (₹300–500) reduces this significantly.

**Accessories compatibility.** SJCAM and Akaso cameras use the GoPro-compatible accessory mounting system — third-party mounts from Amazon work with these cameras, allowing access to India's large budget GoPro-accessory market.
