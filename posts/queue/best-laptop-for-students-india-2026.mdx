## What Every Student Should Know Before Buying

**RAM is not upgradeable** on most modern laptops (soldered on). Always buy at least 8GB; 16GB if budget allows.

**SSD is mandatory.** A laptop with HDD storage in 2026 will feel painfully slow within 6 months.

**Battery life matters more than specs** — a fast laptop that dies in 4 hours is useless in a long class.

**Avoid gaming laptops as student laptops** unless you actually game — they're heavy, loud, and run hot.
