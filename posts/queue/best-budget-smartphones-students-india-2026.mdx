## Student Smartphone Buying Guide

**5G vs 4G:** 5G rollout in India accelerated significantly. All picks here are 5G — the premium over 4G is minimal, and it future-proofs for the next 3–4 years.

**Storage tip:** Go for 128GB minimum; 256GB if available in budget. Cloud storage has limits and paying for Google One adds up. Internal storage saves money long-term.

**AMOLED vs LCD:** AMOLED is noticeably better for outdoor visibility, battery life on dark themes, and display quality. Worth prioritising if your budget allows.

**Charging speed:** 45W+ charging means 50% in under 30 minutes. For students rushing between classes: fast charging reduces the "plug in for 10 minutes" scramble.
