## Buying Guide: Electric Scooters in India 2026

**Real-world vs ARAI range:** ARAI certified range is tested under ideal conditions. Expect 70–80% of claimed range in Indian city traffic with AC roads and 2 riders. Always use real-world range for planning.

**Total cost of ownership (vs petrol):**
- Petrol scooter fuel: ~₹3,000–₹4,000/month at ₹100+/litre
- Electric scooter charging: ₹200–₹400/month at home (₹8–10/kWh)
- Annual savings: ₹30,000–₹45,000 — recovers EV premium in 2–3 years

**Charging infrastructure check:** Before buying, verify charging options at home (dedicated 15A socket or charging point) and Ather Grid/Ola Hypercharger/Hero charging stations near your workplace.

**Service network matters more than brand:** For Tier 2–3 cities, prioritise TVS iQube or Hero Vida — widest physical service networks. Ather and Ola service is concentrated in metro cities.
