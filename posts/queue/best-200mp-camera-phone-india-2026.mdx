## When Does 200MP Actually Matter?

**Large print photography.** 200MP captures sufficient resolution to print 40-inch wide prints at full quality — beyond what any other phone resolution achieves. For professional photography or large format printing, 200MP provides a genuine advantage.

**Aggressive cropping.** Shooting at 200MP and cropping to 50MP equivalent allows telephoto-like framing from a single wide-angle shot — useful when you can't physically move closer to a subject.

**Standard use:** For sharing on Instagram, WhatsApp, and social media, 12–50MP is sufficient. 200MP provides no visible advantage on social platforms — the files are larger with no display benefit. Shoot in pixel-binning mode for standard use and switch to full 200MP only when needed.
