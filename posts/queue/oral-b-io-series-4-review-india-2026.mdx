### iO Series 4 vs iO Series 3 — Is the ₹1,000 Upgrade Worth It?

The Series 3 offers three cleaning modes (Daily Clean, Sensitive, Whitening) versus the Series 4's five. The practical upside of Series 4: Gum Care mode and Intense Clean are available when you need them. Both models use the same iO magnetic drive motor, identical 14-day battery, and identical build quality. If you use only Daily Clean mode and healthy gums are not a concern, the Series 3 saves ₹1,000 without sacrificing core performance. The LED pressure ring on the Series 4 (versus a simpler warning on Series 3) is the clearest functional improvement.

### iO Series 4 vs iO Series 6 — What Do You Get for ₹3,000 More?

The Series 6 adds a built-in colour display on the handle showing brushing mode, timer, and real-time zone coaching without needing your phone open. It also includes a premium travel case and additional brush heads. The cleaning performance and motor are identical. For users who brush without a phone nearby or travel frequently, the Series 6 display is a genuine quality-of-life upgrade. For home-use buyers with a phone accessible, the Series 4 delivers the same cleaning result for ₹3,000 less.

## FAQ

**Does the Oral-B iO Series 4 work without the app?**
Yes. The brush functions fully as a standalone device. The 2-minute timer, quadrant alerts, and LED pressure ring all operate independently. The Oral-B app adds AI zone coaching and brushing history but is entirely optional.

**Are iO Series 4 brush heads available in India?**
Yes. iO-compatible heads (Ultimate Clean, Sensitive Clean, Gentle Care) are sold on Amazon India year-round, typically priced at ₹799–₹1,199 for a pack of two.

**What voltage does the charger support?**
The magnetic charging stand supports 100–240V, making it fully compatible with Indian power outlets. No adapter is required.

**How long does the battery actually last?**
Oral-B's 14-day rating is based on two 2-minute sessions daily. Most Indian users report 10–14 days in practice, with heavier mode usage (Intense Clean) shortening that slightly.

**Is the 2-year warranty valid in India?**
Yes. Oral-B India honours the warranty through authorised service centres. Purchase from an authorised Amazon India seller and retain your invoice.

**Can I use older Oral-B brush heads on the iO Series 4?**
No. The iO Series uses a dedicated magnetic connection incompatible with older Oral-B round heads. You must use iO-specific replacement heads.
