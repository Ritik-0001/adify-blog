## What Makes the Under ₹2,000 Segment Special?

The sub-₹2,000 TWS range in India has exploded in quality over the past two years. At this price in 2024, you got basic Bluetooth and 4-hour battery. In 2026, you are getting:

- 6–8 hours of earbud battery life
- 30–42 hours total with charging case
- Environmental Noise Cancellation (ENC) for calls
- Low-latency gaming modes
- Companion app support with equaliser
- IPX4–IPX5 water resistance

The two features that define this segment are **battery life** and **call quality improvement** — both of which have advanced significantly at this price point.

## Realme Buds Air 3 Neo — Best All-Rounder Under ₹2,000

The Realme Buds Air 3 Neo is the product to beat in the sub-₹2,000 TWS category. It was already impressive at launch and has only become a better value pick as prices have settled.

**Sound quality:** The 10mm dynamic drivers offer a balanced, slightly warm sound signature. Bass is present without dominating — a good sign that the tuning team exercised restraint. This makes the Buds Air 3 Neo versatile across genres: pop, classical, Bollywood, and rock all sound appropriately rendered.

**Battery life:** 7 hours per charge from the earbuds themselves is among the best in this price range. The case extends this to 38 hours total. For most users, this means charging every four to five days.

**Noise Cancellation:** The Buds Air 3 Neo includes active noise cancellation that works best against steady-state sounds like air conditioning, engine noise, and fans. It is less effective against sharp, sudden noises. At this price, that is expected and still genuinely useful.

**Call quality:** Dual-microphone ENC keeps your voice clear during calls. Background noise suppression is noticeably better than single-microphone alternatives. WFH users will find this adequate for most calls.

**Realme Link App:** Equaliser, noise cancellation controls, low-latency mode toggle, and a find-my-earbuds feature. The app is well-designed and updated regularly.

**Fit:** Stem-style design with silicone eartips. Comfortable for extended use. The case is compact and pocket-friendly.

**Who should buy it:** All-rounder buyers who want the best balance of battery, sound, and features under ₹2,000.

## What Should You Prioritise — Battery, ANC, or Call Quality?

This is the most important question to answer before buying. Here is how each model performs:

**If Battery Life Is Your Priority:**
Choose the **boAt Airdopes 141**. 42 total hours from the case is class-leading in this price bracket. Charge once a week and forget about it.

**If ANC/Noise Cancellation Is Your Priority:**
Choose the **Realme Buds Air 3 Neo**. It has the better noise cancellation implementation between these two, though neither matches the Samsung Galaxy Buds FE at ₹3,000.

**If Call Quality Is Your Priority:**
The **Realme Buds Air 3 Neo** edges ahead due to its superior microphone tuning. The **Noise Buds Connect** is also worth considering specifically for call-heavy use — its ENC performance is strong.

**If Sound Quality for Music Is Your Priority:**
Both are good, but in different ways. The **boAt Airdopes 141** wins for bass-heavy genres. The **Realme Buds Air 3 Neo** wins for balanced, genre-versatile listening.
