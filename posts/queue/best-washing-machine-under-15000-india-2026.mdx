## Semi-Automatic vs Fully Automatic Under ₹15,000

**Choose semi-automatic if:**
- Water supply is intermittent (common in many Indian cities and towns)
- Power fluctuations are frequent
- You want lower water consumption
- Budget is strict — semi-automatics last longer with lower maintenance

**Choose fully automatic if:**
- Continuous water supply is available
- Convenience of one-button operation matters
- You live in a metro with stable power and water
