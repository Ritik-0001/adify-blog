## Buying Guide: Electric Toothbrushes in India 2026

**Rotary (Oral-B) vs Sonic (Philips):**
- Rotary: Physically scrubs teeth in circular motion — excellent plaque removal from surfaces
- Sonic: Vibration creates fluid dynamics — reaches between teeth without direct contact. Better for interdental cleaning

**Clinical evidence:** Both technologies are proven to outperform manual brushing. Oral-B has more clinical studies; Philips Sonicare has stronger gum health studies. Both are valid choices.

**Replacement heads:** Build this cost into your budget — replace every 3 months. Oral-B replacement heads: ₹300–₹500 each. Sonicare: ₹500–₹800 each. Oclean: ₹250–₹350 each.

**Timer matters:** The dentist-recommended 2 minutes is hard to judge manually — all models above include a 2-minute SmartTimer. Look for 30-second quadrant reminders (Oral-B calls it QuadPacer) to ensure each mouth section gets 30 seconds.
