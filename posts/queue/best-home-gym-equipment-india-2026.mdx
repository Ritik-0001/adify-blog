## Space Planning for Indian Homes

**2BHK spare room:** Can fit treadmill + bench + dumbbell rack. Typical 10x10 foot room accommodates a basic home gym.

**Apartment balcony/terrace:** Bodyweight equipment, resistance bands, exercise cycle. No noise issues.

**Garage/basement:** Full equipment setup possible. Rubber floor mats recommended for barbell drops.

**Studio apartment:** Foldable equipment only (foldable treadmill, folding bench, resistance bands). Space is critical.

---

## Multi-Gym vs Free Weights

**Multi-gym (all-in-one machine, ₹15,000–₹40,000):** Single piece handles 10–15 exercises. Space-efficient. Good for beginners.

**Free weights (barbell + dumbbells + bench):** More exercises, better functional strength, more scalable. Requires more space.

For most Indian home gym buyers, **free weights are the better long-term investment** — more versatile, better results, and no cable/pulley maintenance.
