## What Does a Home Printer Actually Need to Do?

Home printing in India in 2026 covers a surprisingly wide range:

- **Kids' school work** — assignments, projects, worksheets, colouring pages
- **Adults' documents** — forms, applications, office work from home
- **Family photos** — special occasions, travel memories, birthday printouts
- **Utility documents** — bills, tickets, Aadhaar copies, insurance papers
- **Religious texts and calendars** — a genuinely common print category in Indian homes
- **Creative projects** — labels, cards, invitations

Home use is different from office use in one important way: **the printing is irregular.** A printer sits idle for a week, then suddenly everyone needs to print something. A good home printer needs to handle this burst-and-idle pattern without drying up or jamming.

## Home Use vs Office Use — How They Differ

| Factor | Home Use | Office Use |
|---|---|---|
| Print volume | 50–500 pages/month | 500–5,000 pages/month |
| Primary content | Mixed (docs + photos + kids' work) | Documents, mono text |
| Speed priority | Low-medium | High |
| Connectivity | WiFi helpful | WiFi/network essential |
| Scanner need | Occasional | Regular |
| Ink priorities | Low cost, good colour | Low cost, high mono volume |

A home printer doesn't need to be fast — 8–10 ipm is perfectly adequate when you're printing one assignment or a few photos. What matters more at home is **low running cost, colour quality, and reliability between uses.**

## The Ink Tank Advantage for Home Printing

The biggest challenge with home printers in India has historically been ink cost and cartridge reliability. Here's what ink tank printers solve:

**Ink drying problem:** Cartridge inkjet printers that sit unused for 2–3 weeks often develop clogged print heads. Ink tank printers are less prone to this because the tank system maintains ink flow more consistently.

**Cost predictability:** With a cartridge printer, ink cost is unpredictable — you might need a new cartridge right before a big printing day. With a G2020 ink tank, one ₹600 refill set covers thousands of pages.
