Rechargeable electric toothbrushes eliminate the ongoing battery replacement cost of AA/AAA battery models and provide more consistent motor power throughout the charge cycle. Here are the best rechargeable options in India 2026, covering every charging type and budget.

---

## Charging Types Explained

**Induction charging (Oral-B, Philips traditional):** The brush sits on a charging cradle that wirelessly transfers power. No connectors. Simple — place and charge. Slower charging (several hours for full charge).

**USB charging (Oclean, Mi, Beurer):** Standard USB or USB-C cable charges the brush directly. Works with any phone charger, power bank, or USB hub. Faster and more travel-flexible.

**USB magnetic charging (Oclean):** A USB cable attaches magnetically to the handle end — snap connection is convenient without a physical port exposed to water.

**Glass charging (Philips DiamondClean):** Wireless charging by placing the brush in a glass dock. Premium, aesthetic, no connector management.
