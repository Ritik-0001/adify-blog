## Buying Guide: Budget TWS Earbuds in India 2026

**What you can and can't get under ₹2,000:**
- ✓ ANC (from ₹1,200+)
- ✓ 30+ hour total battery
- ✓ Good call quality with ENC mics
- ✓ Gaming low-latency mode
- ✗ Hi-Res Audio certification
- ✗ Multipoint pairing (most budget models)
- ✗ Wear detection (auto-pause on removal)

**Driver size:** 10–13mm drivers at this price — bigger is generally better for bass. Dual drivers (two per earbud) are rare but available in Realme Buds T300.

**ANC vs ENC:** ANC (Active Noise Cancellation) blocks ambient environment sound for you. ENC (Environmental Noise Cancellation) blocks noise on your calls for the other person. Both matter — look for both in your earbuds.

**Codec support:** Budget earbuds mostly support SBC codec. Some support AAC (better for iPhones). Very few in this range support aptX or LDAC. SBC is fine for casual listening.
