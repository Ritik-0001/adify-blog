## GoPro HERO13 — ₹33,000

**Best traditional action camera.** HyperSmooth 6.0 stabilisation is the smoothest in the industry for standard footage. 5.3K/60fps delivers cinema-quality video for Indian YouTube content. GP2 chip enables fast processing and 10-bit video for colour grading. The largest accessory ecosystem — every third-party mount works with GoPro. For Indian bike vloggers, travel creators, and sports content, GoPro is the industry standard.

## Insta360 X4 — ₹44,990

**Best for content versatility.** 360-degree capture records everything around the camera simultaneously — in post-editing (Insta360 Studio app), you choose where the camera "looks" after filming. This means you never miss a shot because you weren't pointing the camera correctly. For Indian travel content where unexpected moments happen, 360-degree capture is a safety net.

Reframe tool allows creating standard (non-360) clips from 360 footage — one video file produces multiple perspectives for Instagram Reels, YouTube, and social content. Invisible selfie stick mode hides the monopod from 360 shots.

## Which for Indian Content Creators?

**Choose GoPro if:** You shoot bike vlogs, travel documentation, or sports — standard footage with the best stabilisation available.

**Choose Insta360 X4 if:** You want creative flexibility for social media content, travel reels, and the ability to reframe shots after filming. Higher price, but the versatility enables content types that standard action cameras cannot produce.
