## Buying Guide: Antivirus in 2026

**Do you still need antivirus in 2026?**
Yes — particularly for:
- Phishing protection (fake bank/payment sites) — growing problem in India
- Ransomware (encrypts your files and demands payment)
- Public WiFi protection
- Android devices (Google Play Protect has gaps)

**Is Windows Defender enough?**
For most careful users on Windows 11 who don't install pirated software and keep Windows updated — yes. For families with children, online banking, and less tech-savvy users — a paid suite adds meaningful protection.

**macOS antivirus:** macOS is not immune to malware (particularly adware, spyware, and phishing). Malwarebytes free tier or Bitdefender for Mac provides a meaningful safety net.

**Android antivirus:** Google Play Protect covers basics. Bitdefender Mobile Security (₹299/year) adds anti-phishing and anti-theft features worth having.
