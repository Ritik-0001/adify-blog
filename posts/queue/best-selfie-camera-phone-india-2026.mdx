## What to Look For in a Selfie Camera

**Autofocus vs fixed focus.** Selfie cameras with autofocus (Samsung A55, Vivo V29e) produce sharper selfies because they focus at your exact distance. Fixed focus selfie cameras rely on a fixed focal plane — if you're closer or farther than expected, the shot is slightly soft.

**Wide selfie mode.** A wide selfie angle (90°+) fits groups without a selfie stick. Check that the wide mode is available without significant quality degradation — some phones crop the full sensor for wide selfies, reducing resolution.

**South Asian skin tone calibration.** Google (Real Tone), Samsung (Expert RAW calibration), and Apple (Photonic Engine) have all invested in accurate representation of darker skin tones. Cheaper phones often apply overly bright, desaturated processing that makes darker complexions look washed out.
