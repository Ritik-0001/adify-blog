## Is ₹1,000 Enough for Good True Wireless Earbuds?

Honest answer: yes, but with caveats. The sub-₹1,000 TWS market in India has matured significantly. You can now get:

- Bluetooth 5.0 with stable pairing
- 4–5 hours of earbud battery plus a charging case for 2–3 additional charges
- Usable call quality with a basic microphone
- Water resistance at IPX4 or IPX5

What you will not get: active noise cancellation, premium drivers, LDAC codec, multi-device pairing, or reliable ear detection (auto-pause when you remove one earbud). These are genuinely premium features that cost more to implement.

For commuting, gym use, and casual listening, the sub-₹1,000 segment now serves most buyers well. For serious music listening or WFH call quality, you should consider the ₹2,000–₹3,000 range instead.
