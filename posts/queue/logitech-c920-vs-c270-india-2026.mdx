## Resolution Difference

The C920s captures 1080p (1920x1080) vs C270's 720p (1280x720) — 2.25x more pixels. In video calls on fast internet, this difference is visible: C920s images are sharper, faces show more detail, and background elements are clearer. On slow internet (below 10 Mbps), both webcams are compressed to similar quality by the platform.

For YouTube streaming and recording, the C920s difference is clearly visible in final output. For video calls, it depends on connection speed and platform settings.

---

## Microphone Difference

**This is the more important difference for calls.** C920s's dual stereo microphones with noise cancellation capture voice more naturally while filtering out room noise. C270's mono microphone is adequate for quiet rooms but picks up keyboard, AC, and ambient noise in typical Indian homes.

For Indian professionals attending 8+ calls daily, the C920s microphone quality is a professional necessity. For students in quieter environments, C270's microphone is sufficient.

---

## Who Should Buy Each

**Buy Logitech C270 (₹1,695) if:** You attend 1–3 video calls per week, use it primarily for online classes, are a student, or have a quieter home setup.

**Buy Logitech C920s (₹7,999) if:** Video quality is a professional requirement, you stream content to YouTube, you attend daily client or management calls, or your employer has video quality expectations.
