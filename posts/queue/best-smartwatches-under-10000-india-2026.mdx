## Buying Guide: Smartwatches ₹5,000–₹10,000 in 2026

**Proprietary OS vs Wear OS at this price:**
- Proprietary OS (Amazfit, Noise, Fire-Boltt): Better battery (7–14 days), no third-party apps
- Wear OS (Samsung, OnePlus): Real apps (Maps, Pay, Spotify), 1–4 day battery

**GPS accuracy tiers:**
- Single-band GPS: Accurate in open areas, struggles near buildings
- Dual-band GPS (Amazfit GTR 4): Near-professional accuracy in urban environments

**BLE calling:** Routes calls through your phone — convenient but audio quality depends on your phone and earpiece placement.

**Health sensor accuracy:** Amazfit's Zepp Health and Samsung BioActive sensors are validated against medical devices. Indian budget brands (Noise, Fire-Boltt) have lower sensor accuracy — adequate for trends, not medical-grade readings.
