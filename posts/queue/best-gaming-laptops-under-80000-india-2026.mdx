## What to Check Before Buying

**TGP (Total Graphics Power):** RTX 4060 laptops vary from 60W to 115W depending on chassis size. Higher TGP = better gaming performance. Aim for 85W+ for this budget.

**RAM:** 16GB is standard and sufficient. 32GB future-proofs for 3+ years. Both channels should be filled for dual-channel performance (check spec sheet).

**Display:** 144Hz is the minimum. 165Hz is a nice bonus. 1080p FHD at 15.6" is fine for gaming — 1440p is better but rare under ₹80,000.

**Thermals:** Gaming laptops at this budget run hot under load (90°C+ CPU under sustained gaming). Normal — it's physics in a thin chassis.
