## Orthodontic Cleaning Technique with Electric Toothbrush

**The correct method for braces:**
1. Apply a small amount of fluoride toothpaste
2. Position brush head at 45° to the gumline (same as without braces)
3. Clean each bracket individually — don't slide along the wire
4. Clean above the wire (gumline side) and below the wire (chewing surface side) separately
5. Use interdental brushes (floss threaders or water flosser) for under-wire cleaning after brushing

**Interdental cleaning is mandatory for braces:** No toothbrush cleans under orthodontic wires. Use:
- **Interdental brushes (TePe):** ₹150–₹250 for a pack — most effective for bracket spaces
- **Waterpik water flosser:** ₹6,999–₹7,999 — easiest under-wire cleaning, particularly good for teens who resist flossing
