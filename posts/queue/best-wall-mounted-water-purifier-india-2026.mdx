## Why Choose a Wall-Mounted Water Purifier?

Indian kitchens — particularly in apartments and flats — are significantly smaller than Western kitchens. A counter-top purifier with a separate tank takes up meaningful counter space that could otherwise be used for cooking. Wall-mounted purifiers solve this by:

- Freeing up counter space entirely
- Keeping water purification out of the way of cooking activity
- Looking cleaner and more modern in the kitchen
- Making filter access easier (usually from the front face)

Modern wall-mounted RO purifiers offer the same purification performance as counter-top models — the form factor is the only difference.

## Under-Counter RO Purifiers — A Space-Saving Alternative

For maximum kitchen space savings, under-counter RO purifiers are installed below the kitchen counter and connected to a separate tap. They are popular in modular kitchens but require plumbing modifications.
