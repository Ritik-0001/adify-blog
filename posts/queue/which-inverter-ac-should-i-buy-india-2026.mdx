## Why Inverter AC Over Fixed Speed in 2026?

Fixed-speed ACs are still sold in India, but they make very little financial sense anymore. An inverter AC costs roughly ₹3,000–₹5,000 more upfront but saves ₹4,000–₹7,000 per year on electricity bills for a household running the AC 8 hours a day. The break-even point is under 12 months.

The core difference: a fixed-speed compressor runs at full power or shuts off completely. An inverter compressor modulates its speed — running slower when the room is nearly cool, faster when it's hot. This saves power and also reduces wear on the compressor significantly.
