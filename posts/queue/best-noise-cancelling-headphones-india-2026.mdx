Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headphones are one of the highest-value tech purchases for Indian commuters — metro rail, bus, auto-rickshaw, and open-plan office environments generate the exact low-frequency noise (engine rumble, AC hum, crowd noise) that ANC is most effective at eliminating.

---

## Why ANC Headphones Are Particularly Valuable in India

**Indian commute noise profile:** Delhi Metro, Mumbai Local, Bengaluru Namma Metro generate 75–85 dB of consistent low-frequency noise — exactly the frequency range where ANC technology delivers 20–30 dB of reduction, effectively silencing the commute.

**Open-plan offices:** India's IT sector and startup culture have widely adopted open-plan offices. ANC headphones are a productivity tool, not just a comfort accessory.

**Air travel:** India has one of the fastest-growing domestic aviation markets — ANC headphones for IndiGo and Air India cabin noise are a direct quality-of-life improvement.
