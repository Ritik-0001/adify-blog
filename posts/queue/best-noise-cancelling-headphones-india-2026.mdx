## How ANC Technology Works

ANC headphones use microphones to sample ambient noise, then generate a counter-wave that cancels it before it reaches your ears. The quality of the microphones, the processor speed, and the physical seal of the earcups all determine how effective the ANC is.

**What ANC cancels best:** Low-frequency constant noise (airplane engines, air conditioning, traffic rumble). These are the easiest waves to sample and cancel.

**What ANC cancels poorly:** High-frequency, unpredictable sounds (conversations, sudden noises). Better ANC headphones handle this better but no headphone eliminates it entirely.

**Passive isolation:** The physical seal of over-ear headphones blocks high-frequency noise independently of ANC electronics. This is why over-ear still outperforms earbuds in total noise isolation.
