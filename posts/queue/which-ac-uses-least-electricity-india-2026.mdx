## Understanding ISEER — What the Star Rating Actually Means

**ISEER** (Indian Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio) is the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) standard rating for AC efficiency in India. It accounts for Indian seasonal temperature patterns, unlike the old EER ratings which measured at fixed temperatures.

Higher ISEER = more cooling per unit of electricity consumed.

| Star Rating | ISEER Range | Annual Bill (8 hrs/day, ₹8/unit) |
|---|---|---|
| 2-Star | 3.30–3.49 | ₹10,500–₹11,500 |
| 3-Star | 3.50–3.99 | ₹8,000–₹9,500 |
| 4-Star | 4.00–4.49 | ₹7,000–₹8,000 |
| 5-Star | 4.50+ | ₹5,500–₹6,500 |

Note: The BEE revises ISEER rating thresholds periodically. Models that were 5-star in 2023 may now be 3-star under stricter 2026 norms. Always check the current BEE label on the model you're buying.

## Simple Tips to Reduce AC Electricity Bill (Beyond the Star Rating)

1. **Set temperature to 24°C:** Every degree below 24°C increases consumption by 6%. 24°C feels comfortable with a fan.
2. **Use sleep mode at night:** Most ACs raise temperature by 1°C every 30 minutes in sleep mode — saves 10–15% on overnight bills.
3. **Clean filters monthly:** Clogged filters force the compressor to work harder. Monthly cleaning maintains efficiency.
4. **Use timer/schedule:** Don't cool an empty room. A 30-minute pre-cool before you arrive is better than all-day cooling.
5. **Ensure no heat sources near indoor unit:** Avoid placing the AC near kitchen heat, direct sunlight on the indoor unit reduces efficiency.
