## Who Actually Needs 20,000mAh vs 10,000mAh?

**Get a 20K power bank if:**
- You travel for 2+ days without reliable power access
- You are a remote worker or frequently work from cafes/trains
- You want to charge a laptop from the power bank
- You carry multiple devices (phone + tablet + earbuds)
- You go on long treks, road trips, or camping

**Stick with a 10K power bank if:**
- You charge once a day and have access to power at home/office
- You only need backup for one phone
- Weight and portability are important to you
- Your budget is under ₹1,500

The 20K adds roughly 200–250g of weight compared to a 10K unit. That matters if you are running or at the gym, and it does not matter at all if you are in a backpack.
