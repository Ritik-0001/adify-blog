## Business Laptop vs Consumer Laptop — Key Differences

| Feature | Business Laptop | Consumer Laptop |
|---|---|---|
| Warranty | 3-year standard | 1-year standard |
| Repairability | Modular, parts available | Often proprietary |
| Build standard | MIL-STD-810H | Varies |
| Security features | TPM, IR camera, privacy shutter | Basic |
| Keyboard | Premium travel, durable | Variable |
| Weight | Prioritised for portability | Mixed |
| Support | On-site available | Carry-in only |
