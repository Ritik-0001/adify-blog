## What Adults Need in an Electric Toothbrush

**Pressure sensor:** Adults who have brushed manually for years typically over-press — a major cause of gum recession that worsens with age. A pressure sensor is essential for adults transitioning from manual brushing.

**2-minute timer with quadrant alerts:** Most adults brush for under 60 seconds. The 30-second quadrant timer is the single feature that most improves brushing quality for new electric toothbrush users.

**Multiple modes:** Adults benefit from at least Daily Clean + Sensitive — some gum days require lighter cleaning. Whitening mode is a genuine benefit for chai and coffee drinkers.

**Replacement head availability:** Adults will use the brush for 3–5+ years — brush head availability in India matters. Oral-B and Philips have the widest replacement head availability.

## India-Specific Adult Oral Health Context

**Cavity risk:** India's diet is high in refined carbohydrates — white rice, maida products, mithai, packaged biscuits. Electric toothbrush + 1,450 ppm fluoride toothpaste is the most accessible cavity-prevention system.

**Gum disease:** India has a high prevalence of periodontal disease — estimated 65–90% of adults have some form of gum disease. Electric toothbrushes with pressure sensors and gum care modes address this directly.

**Surface staining:** Chai twice daily is standard for most Indian adults. Coffee, tobacco (cigarettes, pan) create heavy surface staining. Electric toothbrush whitening modes visibly lighten these stains within 2–4 weeks.
