**BLDC payback calculation:** BLDC premium over standard fan = ~₹1,500. Annual saving = ₹2,100+. Payback: Under 1 year. After that, pure savings.

**Fan size guide:**
- Room under 100 sq ft: 900mm sweep
- 100–150 sq ft: 1050–1200mm sweep
- 150–200 sq ft: 1200mm sweep
- 200+ sq ft: 1200mm × 2 fans or 1400–1500mm sweep

**BEE Star Rating for fans:**
- 4-5 star BLDC: Most efficient — choose these always
- 3-star standard: Basic compliance
- Pre-BEE rating fans: Replace immediately for savings
