## How to Choose the Right 1.5 Ton AC

Before diving into model recommendations, there are four key parameters to consider:

**Star Rating (ISEER):** A 5-star AC costs ₹6,000–₹10,000 more than a 3-star but saves ₹4,000–₹6,000 annually on electricity if used 8+ hours per day. For heavily used ACs, 5-star is the right choice. For guest rooms used 3–4 hours daily, 3-star is more cost-effective upfront.

**Inverter vs Fixed Speed:** Always choose inverter in 2026. Fixed-speed ACs are being phased out for good reason — they use 30–40% more electricity.

**Refrigerant:** R-32 is the current standard. It is more efficient and environment-friendly than R-410A. All reputable brands now use R-32.

**Brand Service Network:** Match your choice to service availability in your city (discussed per model below).
