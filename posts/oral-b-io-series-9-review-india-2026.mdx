## Deep Dive: 7 Cleaning Modes

The iO Series 9 adds two exclusive modes over the Series 6's 5:

**1. Daily Clean** — standard 2-minute brushing with 30-second quadrant alerts
**2. Sensitive** — reduced intensity for gum sensitivity, post-procedure care, dental implants
**3. Gum Care** — extended gumline focus with gentler strokes; particularly relevant for Indian adults with high gingivitis prevalence
**4. Whitening** — high-frequency polishing for surface stain removal
**5. Intense** — maximum cleaning power for heavy plaque
**6. Tongue Cleaner** — specialized mode for tongue surface cleaning with adapted brush head motion
**7. AI Whitening** — the Series 9 exclusive: combines AI-guided brushing with polishing motion, tracking whitening progress in the app over time with before/after zone mapping

The AI Whitening mode is the flagship's killer feature — it personalizes the whitening process based on your actual brushing behavior rather than applying the same blanket polishing to all users.

## iO Sense Charging Station — The Premium Accessory

The iO Sense is the Series 9's unique charging station — a premium desk accessory that does more than charge:

- **LED ring on base:** Glows to indicate charging status and syncs brushing feedback
- **Data sync:** Automatically syncs the previous brushing session to the Oral-B app when you dock the brush — no phone required during brushing
- **Aesthetic design:** Matte finish base designed to sit on bathroom counters without looking like a charging gadget

For Indian buyers with dedicated bathroom counters and an interest in oral health tracking, the iO Sense is a genuine quality-of-life upgrade. For buyers who will travel most of the time and leave the base at home, its value is limited.
