## What to Look For in a Camera Phone Under ₹15000

**OIS over megapixels.** Optical Image Stabilisation (available on Samsung A25 5G) improves real-world photo quality more than higher megapixel count without OIS. A stable 50MP shot beats an unstable 100MP shot in practical use.

**AMOLED display for photo review.** Shooting on a phone with AMOLED display shows truer colours when reviewing photos — important if you share photos directly from the phone. LCD displays misrepresent colours.

**Ultrawide quality.** Check the ultrawide lens megapixels (not just that it exists) — 8MP+ ultrawides produce usable shots; 2MP ultrawides are essentially decorative.
