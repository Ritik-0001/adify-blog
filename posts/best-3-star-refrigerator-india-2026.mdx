## Annual Cost Reality — 3-star vs 5-star

For a 250L double-door refrigerator:
- 3-star: ~250 units/year → ₹2,000/year @₹8/unit
- 5-star: ~175 units/year → ₹1,400/year @₹8/unit
- **Annual difference: ₹600–₹800**
- **Price premium for 5-star: ₹2,000–₹4,000**
- Break-even: 3–5 years

For buyers who may upgrade within 5–7 years anyway, or where the upfront price matters more than long-term savings, 3-star is a perfectly rational choice.

## Should You Buy 3-Star or 5-Star?

**Choose 3-star if:**
- Budget is tight and upfront savings matter more
- You plan to upgrade the refrigerator in 5–7 years
- Your electricity tariff is under ₹6/unit
- Multiple family members share the running costs (electricity difference feels small)

**Upgrade to 5-star if:**
- You plan to keep the refrigerator for 10+ years
- Your electricity tariff is above ₹8/unit
- You're environmentally conscious
- Annual savings of ₹600–₹1,200 matter at your household scale
