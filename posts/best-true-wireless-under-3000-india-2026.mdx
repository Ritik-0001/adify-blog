## Buying Guide: TWS Under ₹3,000 in India 2026

**What to expect at this price:**
- ANC: Available (25–42dB) — meaningful for commutes
- Battery: 30–45 hours combined (case + buds)
- IP rating: IPX4–IPX5 — sweat and rain resistant
- Codecs: SBC + AAC standard; LDAC on premium models
- Call quality: 2–4 mic arrays with ENC

**ANC quality at this price:** Budget ANC doesn't match Sony XM5 or AirPods Pro — but it does reduce ambient noise by 20–40dB, which is meaningful on public transport.

**TWS vs neckband under ₹3,000:**
- TWS: More modern, no cable, compact
- Neckband: Better battery, better call mics, magnetic snap so you don't lose earbuds
- For calls primarily: Neckband wins. For casual listening: TWS wins.
