## Buying Guide: Android Tablets in 2026

**Should you buy a tablet or laptop?**
- Tablets are better for: media consumption, note-taking with a stylus, reading, portability
- Laptops are better for: full productivity apps, file management, software development, typing-heavy work

**Chipset guide:**
- Helio G99 / Snapdragon 695: Light tasks — browsing, streaming, social media
- Snapdragon 870 / Exynos 1380: Heavy tasks — gaming, video editing, multitasking
- Snapdragon 8 Gen 2: Flagship (₹80,000+ tablets only)

**Display technology:**
- IPS LCD: Good colours, wide viewing angles — adequate for most use
- AMOLED/OLED: Better blacks, more vibrant colours, better for dark mode use

**Stylus note:** Only Samsung Galaxy Tab S series and Lenovo Tab P series come with proper low-latency styluses for drawing and handwriting.
