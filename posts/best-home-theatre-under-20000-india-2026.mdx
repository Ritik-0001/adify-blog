## Setting Up a 5.1 System in an Indian Apartment

This is where many buyers hesitate, and rightly so. Indian apartments — especially 2BHK flats in cities — often have furniture-heavy living rooms where running cables to rear positions is genuinely challenging.

**Practical solutions:**
1. **Cable raceways:** Flat adhesive cable channels (available for under ₹500 on Amazon) can route speaker wires along skirting boards or the ceiling edge cleanly
2. **Under rug routing:** If you have a large centre rug, speaker cables can run underneath it
3. **DIY ceiling mount:** For a permanent setup, drilling a small cable channel through the false ceiling is the cleanest solution

If none of these options work for your space, the boAt Aavante Bar 1700 or a step-up soundbar like the Samsung HW-C450 (₹17,999) is the more practical choice — and still a major upgrade over TV speakers.
