## Display — The Best Under ₹40,000

The 11.61-inch 144Hz display is the OnePlus Pad's headline feature — and it delivers. At 2800×2000 (7:5 aspect ratio), the Pad uses a squarer format than typical widescreen tablets, which is better for productivity and reading.

**144Hz at ₹38,000:** The Xiaomi Pad 6 also offers 144Hz but at ₹26,999 — the OnePlus Pad's display advantage needs to come from something else. It does: the 7:5 aspect ratio is significantly better for document work, web browsing, and split-screen productivity than the 16:10 Xiaomi Pad 6.

**Display accuracy:** The OnePlus Pad's display is factory calibrated with accurate colour reproduction (Delta E below 1 — accurate enough for photo editing reference use). Most tablets in this range are not factory calibrated.

## Performance — Flagship Tier

The Dimensity 9000 is one of the fastest chips ever used in an Indian tablet at this price:

**Benchmark context:** The Dimensity 9000 outperforms Snapdragon 870 (Xiaomi Pad 6) and Exynos 1380 (Samsung Tab S9 FE) in multi-core tasks by 30–40%. For gaming, video editing, and intensive multitasking, this is the fastest Android tablet under ₹45,000.

**Gaming:** BGMI, Genshin Impact, and Call of Duty Mobile run at 60fps maximum settings without throttling. The Dimensity 9000's efficient architecture keeps temperature manageable during extended gaming sessions.

**Multitasking:** 8GB RAM (LPDDR5) with OxygenOS' memory management allows 8–10 apps in memory simultaneously without app reloading. Heavy split-screen use (two full apps side-by-side) is smooth.

## 67W SUPERVOOC — The Fastest Tablet Charging in India

67W charging is the fastest available in any tablet under ₹50,000:
- 0 to 100% in approximately 80 minutes
- 0 to 50% in approximately 35 minutes

For context, Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 FE charges at 45W, iPad at 20W (with USB-C), and most mid-range tablets at 18W. The OnePlus Pad charges faster than any competitor — relevant for users who need a quick top-up before leaving home.

**9,510mAh battery life:** 12–14 hours of mixed use. With 67W charging, even a 30-minute charge provides 4–5 hours of use — the most practical charge cycle in the tablet category.

## OxygenOS for Pad — Best Android Tablet OS

OnePlus' OxygenOS is generally considered the cleanest Android experience after stock Android — less aggressive with ads, bloatware, and MIUI-style restrictions than Xiaomi. For Pad, OnePlus has added:

- **Multi-window floating apps:** Open apps as floating windows over any other app
- **Smart Sidebar:** Quick access app launcher from the side of the screen
- **Shelf:** Customisable widgets on the right side
- **PC Connect:** Connect OnePlus phone to Pad wirelessly for file sharing and mirroring

**OnePlus ecosystem integration:** OnePlus Buds, OnePlus Watch, and OnePlus phones integrate more seamlessly with the OnePlus Pad than with Samsung or Xiaomi tablets. For OnePlus phone users, this is a meaningful advantage.

## OnePlus Stylo — Stylus Support

The OnePlus Stylo stylus (sold separately, ₹4,999) provides:
- 4,096 levels of pressure sensitivity
- Tilt support
- Magnetic charging (attaches to Pad side)
- Low latency (9ms input lag)

**Vs Samsung S Pen:** The S Pen is included with the Tab S9 FE; the Stylo costs extra. If stylus is important, the Samsung Tab S9 FE (S Pen included at ₹24,999) is significantly better value. The OnePlus Pad + Stylo totals ₹42,998–₹46,998.
