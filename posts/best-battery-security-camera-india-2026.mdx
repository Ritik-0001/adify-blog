## Why Battery Cameras Make Sense for India

Battery-powered cameras are ideal for locations where running power cables is impractical — farmhouse gates, shed entrances, rental properties, temporary construction sites, and society parking areas. In India, irregular power supply in semi-urban areas also makes battery cameras an attractive option for locations where mains power is unreliable. The latest models last 3–6 months on a single charge when using motion-triggered recording rather than continuous recording.
