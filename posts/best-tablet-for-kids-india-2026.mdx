## Kids Tablet Buying Guide

**By age:**
- Ages 3–5: Amazon Fire HD 8 Kids / Lenovo Tab M7 with parent supervision
- Ages 6–9: Amazon Fire HD 8 Kids / Samsung Tab A7 Lite Kids mode
- Ages 10–12: Redmi Pad SE with parental controls / Samsung Tab A7 Lite (exit kids mode)
- Ages 12+: Standard tablets — treat as adult device with digital wellbeing settings

**Essential accessories:**
- **Protective case:** ₹300–₹800 — non-negotiable for children
- **Screen protector:** ₹200–₹400 — tempered glass preferred
- **Headphones:** Wired recommended for children — no Bluetooth radiation concerns, no charging requirement

**Screen time guidelines (AAP — American Academy of Pediatrics):**
- Ages 2–5: 1 hour per day (with parent co-viewing)
- Ages 6+: Consistent limits set by parents
- Use built-in screen time tools to enforce — all tablets above support this
