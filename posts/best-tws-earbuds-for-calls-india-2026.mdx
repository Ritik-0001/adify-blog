For Indian WFH professionals and commuters, call clarity is the most important TWS feature — background noise from family, traffic, AC units, and open offices can make calls unintelligible. Here are the best TWS earbuds for calls in India 2026.

---

## What Makes Good Call Quality in TWS Earbuds

**Microphone count and placement:** More mics = better noise isolation. 4–6 mic arrays with beamforming technology isolate voice from ambient noise more effectively than 2-mic systems.

**AI noise suppression:** On-device AI processing identifies and suppresses non-voice sounds — traffic, keyboard clicks, AC hum, family background noise — all common in Indian call environments.

**Outward-facing mics:** Mics positioned to capture both voice (inward) and ambient noise (outward) for better noise cancellation processing.

**Call microphone rating:** Different from audio quality. Some earbuds (Sony XM5, Bose QC45) have outstanding audio quality but only average call microphone performance.
