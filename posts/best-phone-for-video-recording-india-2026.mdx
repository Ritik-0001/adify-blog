## Video Recording Specs That Matter

**Stabilisation method.** OIS + EIS combined (iPhone, Samsung flagship) provides smoother footage than EIS alone. Action Mode (iPhone) applies aggressive EIS cropping for the smoothest possible result when moving.

**Bitrate.** Standard H.265 video at 4K/60fps is typically 60–100 Mbps. ProRes (iPhone) is 1,000+ Mbps — dramatically more editing latitude. If you edit your videos, ProRes or Log recording matters significantly.

**Microphone quality.** The best video phone is useless with poor audio. iPhone 15 Pro's three-microphone system with Wind Noise Reduction is the best smartphone audio recording system for outdoor Indian conditions (traffic, wind, crowds).
