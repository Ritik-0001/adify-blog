## Buying Guide: Smart Home in India 2026

**Which voice assistant to choose:**
- Alexa (Amazon): Best India support — understands Indian accents, has most India-compatible devices
- Google Home: Good for Android/Google users — integrates with Google Calendar, Maps
- Apple HomeKit: Best for iPhone users — Siri control, most secure
- Most devices support all three — not an exclusive choice

**Wi-Fi smart home requirements:** All smart home devices need a stable 2.4GHz Wi-Fi network. 5GHz Wi-Fi is NOT compatible with most smart home devices (too short range through walls).

**Hub vs hubless:** Hub-required systems (Philips Hue) work locally even without internet and have more sophisticated automations. Hubless systems (Wipro, Mi) are simpler and cheaper but depend on cloud connectivity.

**Starting your smart home:** Buy a smart speaker first (Echo Pop or Echo Show) → add smart bulbs → add smart plug. Start simple, expand gradually.
