## Why ₹20,000 Is a Smart Chair Budget in India

The ₹15,000–₹20,000 range represents the best value-per-feature point for Indian professional buyers. Here's the case:

**You avoid diminishing returns above ₹30,000.** The jump from ₹20,000 to ₹50,000 (Herman Miller territory) offers meaningful but incremental improvements in materials and refinement. The jump from ₹10,000 to ₹20,000 offers dramatic improvements in adjustability and comfort.

**5+ year durability is achievable.** Chairs in this range use materials rated for 8-hour commercial use. With proper maintenance, a ₹18,000 chair from a quality brand will last 5–7 years — working out to roughly ₹7–10 per working day.

**Your back health is worth it.** Back pain treatment in India typically costs ₹3,000–₹15,000 per episode in physiotherapy costs alone. A proper ergonomic chair is preventive medicine.
