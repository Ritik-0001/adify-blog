## Under ₹5000: Premium Features at Mid-Range Price

**49dB ANC is competitive with premium earbuds.** Sony WF-1000XM5 (₹19,990) delivers the benchmark ANC experience. OnePlus Buds 3 at ₹3,999 gets remarkably close in practical use. The gap is noticeable but significantly narrower than the 5x price difference suggests.

**Dual driver advantage.** Both OnePlus Buds 3 and Nothing Ear (2) use separate drivers for bass and treble. This produces more accurate sound separation than single-driver earbuds — you can hear instruments and vocals occupying distinct space in a mix.

**Multipoint Bluetooth.** OnePlus Buds 3 supports multipoint (connecting phone + laptop simultaneously). This feature, previously reserved for ₹10,000+ earbuds, is now available at ₹3,999 — genuinely useful for work-from-home scenarios.
