## Why Spin Scrubber for Indian Bathrooms

**Mold in Indian humidity:** India's monsoon season (June–September) creates ideal mold growth conditions in bathrooms. Manual scrubbing can't match the 300+ RPM motorised action for effective mold removal from tile grout.

**Hard water stains:** Indian municipal water's mineral content leaves calcium deposits (white stains) on tiles, faucets, and tubs. Spin scrubbers with stiff brush heads remove mineral deposits faster than manual scrubbing.

**Indian bathroom frequency:** Indian bathroom floors are typically wet-cleaned daily — the combination of manual cleaning frequency and Indian water mineral content accelerates stain buildup that requires periodic intensive cleaning.
