## Why Electric Toothbrushes Make Excellent Gifts

**The "I wouldn't buy it for myself" factor:** Electric toothbrushes sit in a price range that feels indulgent to self-purchase but perfectly appropriate to receive as a gift. Most Indians still use manual toothbrushes — an electric brush is a genuine upgrade that the recipient will notice every single morning.

**Every day use:** Unlike candles, books, or decor, a toothbrush is used twice daily for 2–3 years. The gifter's generosity is experienced twice every day.

**Premium unboxing experience:** Oral-B and Philips Sonicare packaging is genuinely premium — clean, well-structured boxes with magnetic closures (on premium models) that feel appropriate for gift-giving without additional wrapping.

**Universal appeal:** There is an electric toothbrush for every age, dental need, and budget. The challenge is knowing which one to choose.
