## What Makes a Great Fitness Band in India?

The best fitness band for Indian users needs to balance accurate step counting, reliable heart rate and SpO2 monitoring, long battery life, and durability in India's varied climate conditions — from Rajasthan's dry heat to Mumbai's humidity. Price-to-feature ratio matters enormously in this category, where the best options range from ₹1,299 to ₹14,999.

## 1. Mi Smart Band 8 — Best Overall Fitness Band

**Price:** ₹2,499

The Mi Smart Band 8 continues Xiaomi's tradition of delivering exceptional value in the fitness band category and remains the benchmark for budget fitness tracking in India. The 1.62-inch AMOLED display is strikingly large for a fitness band and offers 60Hz smooth scrolling — a meaningful upgrade over the choppy interfaces on competing devices. Step counting accuracy on the Mi Band 8 has improved with a refined 6-axis accelerometer that reduces false steps during driving and gestures. Battery life is one of the Mi Band 8's strongest suits — up to 16 days on standard use with notifications enabled. With the always-on display activated, this drops to around 10 days. Both are class-leading figures. The watch is compatible with both Android and iOS via the Xiaomi Health app.

## Fitness Band vs Smartwatch: Which Should You Buy?

**Choose a fitness band if:**
- Battery life of 7+ days is a priority
- You want a slimmer, lighter wearable for sleep tracking
- Your budget is under ₹3,000
- You primarily care about health data and not smart notifications
- You want something discreet for work environments

**Choose a smartwatch if:**
- You want Bluetooth calling from your wrist
- App ecosystem and GPS matter to you
- You want a larger display for reading notifications
- Budget extends to ₹2,500 or beyond
