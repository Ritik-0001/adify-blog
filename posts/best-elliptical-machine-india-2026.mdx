## Flywheel Weight — Why It Matters

A heavier flywheel creates smoother, more natural motion — comparable to how a heavier bicycle wheel feels more stable. Under 8kg feels jerky and "cheap." 10–16kg provides gym-quality smoothness for home use. Above 16kg is overkill for home fitness.

## Elliptical vs Treadmill for Indian Apartments

Elliptical machines create virtually no floor impact — the elliptical motion keeps feet on the pedals throughout. Treadmills create rhythmic foot-strike impact that transmits through concrete floors to downstairs neighbours. For apartment dwellers in Indian cities, **ellipticals are significantly more neighbour-friendly.**
