## What to Look For Under ₹1000

**Wired only at this price.** Wireless headphones under ₹1000 have poor battery life (3–4 hours) and noticeably weaker sound quality. Wired headphones give you better drivers and more consistent audio at this budget.

**Inline mic matters.** If you use headphones for phone calls, online classes, or video meetings, choose a model with an inline mic and remote (JBL C50HI or JBL T450). The Sony MDR-ZX110 lacks this feature.

**Driver size vs tuning.** Larger 40mm drivers (boAt) emphasise bass. Smaller 32mm drivers (JBL) can still deliver great sound with proper tuning. Driver size alone doesn't determine quality — the tuning matters more.

**Ear cushion comfort.** Budget headphones use thin foam pads that compress quickly. For sessions over two hours, consider your next upgrade to the ₹2000–3000 range where leatherette padding becomes available.
