## Gaming TV Requirements

**HDMI 2.1 (48Gbps):** Required for 4K 120Hz from PS5 and Xbox Series X. HDMI 2.0 (18Gbps) caps at 4K 60Hz.

**VRR (Variable Refresh Rate):** Eliminates screen tearing without frame rate penalties. PS5 supports VRR; Xbox supports FreeSync Premium Pro.

**Low input lag:** Target under 15ms in Game Mode. High input lag creates perceptible delay between controller input and on-screen response.

**4K 120Hz panel:** Smooth motion at 120 frames per second — visible difference in fast action games (Gran Turismo, FIFA, Call of Duty).
