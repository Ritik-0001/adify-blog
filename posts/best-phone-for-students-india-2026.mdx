## What Students Actually Need

**Battery life:** College days run 8–10 hours away from chargers. A 5000mAh minimum battery handles this. Larger (6000mAh) or fast charging is better.

**Camera for notes:** 50MP minimum for scanning handwritten notes. 100MP (Redmi Note 13) gives the best detail for zooming into handwriting. OIS is not required for note-scanning.

**Storage:** 128GB minimum. Download offline notes (Notion, OneNote), lecture recordings, and apps for multiple subjects. 256GB preferred for medical students (large study material).

**5G:** Indian college campuses are rapidly getting 5G. Jio and Airtel 5G is free — choose 5G now to avoid upgrading in 2–3 years.

**Durability:** Cases are essential. Students frequently drop phones. Gorilla Glass 5 or 6 helps. A ₹499 TPU case prevents most damage.

---

## Engineering Students — Special Considerations

**Arduino / Raspberry Pi projects:** Some engineering projects require phones for Bluetooth/USB interfacing. Most Android phones support USB OTG — useful for data logging and IoT projects.

**CAD files, large PDFs:** 8GB RAM + 256GB storage recommended for engineering students working with large project files.

**Video calls for group projects:** Front camera quality matters. 16MP front cameras are standard at ₹15,000+.
