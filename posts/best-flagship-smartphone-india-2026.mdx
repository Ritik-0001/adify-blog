## Indian-Specific Considerations

**Dual SIM:** All four support dual SIM (or SIM + eSIM). For Indian buyers using Jio + Airtel dual SIM, check eSIM support for your specific operator.

**5G bands:** All four support 5G in Indian frequency bands (sub-6GHz, n78, n28).

**Fast charging:** OnePlus 13 charges fastest (100W). Samsung S25 Ultra charges at 45W. iPhone 16 Pro Max at 27W (slowest).

**Repairability:** All four brands have service centres in tier-1 Indian cities. Apple has Apple Authorised Service Providers. Samsung has Samsung Care+ centres widely available.
