## Night Photography Key Factors

**Sensor size.** Larger sensor area captures more light photons per pixel. Pixel 9 (1/1.31"), Samsung S25+ (1/1.56"), and Vivo X200 (1/1.56") lead in sensor size — directly correlating with night performance.

**OIS (Optical Image Stabilisation).** In low light, longer exposures are needed. OIS compensates for hand shake during these longer exposures — the difference between a sharp and blurry night shot. All phones above ₹20000 now include OIS.

**Night Mode processing quality.** Google's multi-frame fusion (averaging multiple rapid exposures) reduces noise while preserving detail better than any competitor's algorithm — this is Google's primary advantage even against phones with larger sensors.
