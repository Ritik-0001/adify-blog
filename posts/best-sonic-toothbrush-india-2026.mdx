## Sonic vs Oscillating — Quick Reference

| Factor | Sonic (Philips/Oclean/Mi) | Oscillating (Oral-B) |
|---|---|---|
| Cleaning feel | Gentle, fluid | Firm, mechanical |
| Gum care | Superior (fluid dynamics) | Good (physical removal) |
| Surface plaque | Very good | Excellent |
| Interdental | Superior (fluid reaches gaps) | Good |
| Best for | Sensitive gums, gum disease | Heavy surface staining |
