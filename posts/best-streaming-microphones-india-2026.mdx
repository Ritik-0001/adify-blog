## Buying Guide: Streaming Mics in 2026

**USB vs XLR:**
- **USB:** Plug-and-play, no interface needed — best for beginners and streamers
- **XLR:** Requires an audio interface (₹4,000+) — better audio quality ceiling, more upgrade flexibility

**Condenser vs Dynamic:**
- **Condenser:** More sensitive — picks up more detail but also more room noise
- **Dynamic:** Less sensitive — better for noisy environments, more forgiving of room acoustics

**Polar patterns:**
- **Cardioid:** Picks up from the front, rejects sides and back — correct for solo recording
- **Omnidirectional:** Picks up all directions — for group podcasts/roundtables
- **Bidirectional/Figure-8:** Front and back — for two people facing each other

**Room treatment tip:** A cheap foam panel behind your monitor and a mic arm (₹1,000–₹2,000) dramatically improve recording quality — as much as spending twice as much on the mic.
