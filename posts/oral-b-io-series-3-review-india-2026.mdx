## iO Magnetic Drive at the Entry Price

The iO Series 3 delivers the same iO magnetic drive technology as the Series 4, 7, and 9 — the fundamental technology improvement over standard Oral-B oscillating is present:

- Smooth, precise movement (significantly less vibration than Pro 3)
- Micro-vibrations in addition to oscillating-rotating
- Clinical improvement: up to 4x more plaque removed from hard-to-reach areas vs manual

**At ₹4,499:** The iO Series 3 is approximately ₹1,500 cheaper than the iO Series 4 for the same core technology. The differences are primarily in accessories and polish, not core cleaning.

---

## 5 Modes — More Than the Series 4

One unusual fact: the iO Series 3 has 5 cleaning modes — one more than the Series 4's 4 modes. The iO Series 3 includes:

- Daily Clean
- Sensitive
- Whitening
- **Gum Care** (not in Series 4)
- **Intense** (not in older models)

**The Series 4 missing Gum Care:** This is a genuine Series 4 limitation — if gum care is a priority, the Series 3 at ₹4,499 is arguably a better purchase than the Series 4 at ₹5,999. The Series 3 has more modes for less money.

---

## What the Series 3 Lacks vs Series 4

**Travel case:** The Series 4 includes a USB travel charging case — a significant travel-specific advantage. The Series 3 has no travel case (a separate travel case can be purchased for ₹1,500–₹2,000).

**Handle polish:** The Series 4 has a slightly more premium matte finish and better colour options. Functionally identical cleaning performance.
