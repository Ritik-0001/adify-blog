## Deep Dive: 5 Cleaning Modes

The iO Series 3 offers five cleaning modes — more than the Pro 3 3000's three and one more than the iO Series 4:

- **Daily Clean** — standard daily brushing with 30-second quadrant pauses
- **Sensitive** — lower intensity for sensitive teeth and post-dental procedures
- **Gum Care** — focuses on gumline stimulation; relevant for India where ICMR data shows 80%+ of adults have some form of gum disease
- **Whitening** — polishing mode for surface stain removal; practical for chai, coffee, and turmeric-heavy Indian diets
- **Intense** — maximum cleaning power for heavy plaque buildup

For most Indian users, Daily Clean and Gum Care will be the two most-used modes.

## LED Pressure Ring — Why It Matters

The colored LED ring around the brush handle lights up white for correct pressure, red when pressing too hard, and green for light pressure confirmation. Research shows 50–60% of toothbrush-related gum recession is caused by excessive brushing pressure. Visual feedback corrects this habit within 2–4 weeks.

Indian dentists increasingly cite the LED ring as the most practically useful feature for patients who are chronic aggressive brushers — a common problem among Indian adults who learned manual brushing habits.

## AI Brushing Feedback via App

Pair with the Oral-B app (iOS/Android, free, no Wi-Fi required) via Bluetooth and get zone-by-zone brushing feedback. The app divides your mouth into 16 zones and tracks dwell time in each. An AI coach highlights missed zones — the lower left molars are the most commonly missed zone across all populations.

**App note:** Works reliably even on mid-range Android phones. The brush functions fully without the app — AI features are additive, not required.

## Buying Guide: iO Series 3

**Key consideration — motor feel:**
The magnetic drive is genuinely quieter and smoother. If you or someone in your household finds the buzz of oscillating brushes unpleasant, the iO motor difference is noticeable from the first use.

**Key consideration — app use:**
The AI brushing guidance is most valuable in the first 2–3 months when retraining brushing habits. After habits are corrected, many users stop using the app. Factor whether this justifies the premium.

**Key consideration — replacement head cost:**
Families with 3+ users buying individual iO handles will spend more on heads than the equivalent CrossAction setup. For multi-user households, the Pro 3 3000 may be more economical long-term.
