## Choosing the Right Power Backup Type

### Option 1: UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply)

Best for: Desktop PCs, workstations, routers, and sensitive electronics.

A UPS switches to battery in under 10 milliseconds during a power cut — fast enough that your PC never knows the power went out. It is not designed for long backup times (typically 10–30 minutes) but is essential for protecting electronics from sudden shutdowns and voltage spikes. Every Indian PC user should have one.

**Recommended:** APC BX600C-IN (₹3,500–₹4,500), Microtek EM1100 (₹2,800–₹3,500)

### Option 2: Home Inverter + Battery

Best for: Running fans, lights, TVs, and basic appliances during power cuts of 2–8 hours.

A home inverter takes 50–200 milliseconds to switch to battery — noticeable as a brief flicker, which is why it is not suitable for PCs but is fine for most other home loads. Paired with a 100Ah tubular battery, it provides 4–6 hours of power for fans, lights, and a TV. A 200Ah battery doubles this.

**Recommended:** Luminous Cruze+ 2KVA (₹8,500–₹11,000 inverter) + Luminous Red Charge 150Ah battery (₹12,000–₹15,000)

### Option 3: Solar Hybrid Inverter

Best for: Reducing electricity bills while maintaining backup for power cuts.

A solar hybrid inverter uses energy from rooftop panels first, then grid power, and falls back to battery during cuts. For a 2BHK home in a sunny Indian city using 300–400 units per month, a 2kW solar system can reduce the electricity bill by ₹1,500–₹2,500 per month — payback in 4–5 years. States with net metering policies (Gujarat, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan) offer additional savings.

**Recommended:** Luminous NXG+ 1800 solar hybrid (₹8,500–₹11,000) + 2 × 330W solar panels (₹14,000–₹18,000) + 150Ah tubular battery

## Final Verdict

India's power backup needs vary enormously — a student in a Delhi hostel needs a ₹1,500 router UPS, while a family in rural Bihar might need a ₹25,000 inverter-battery system for 8-hour daily cuts. Start by calculating your load and cut duration, then match the solution to the need. For electronics protection, a **UPS is non-negotiable**. For whole-home backup, **Luminous and Microtek inverters** paired with a quality tubular battery are the gold standard. For long-term savings and environmental benefit, a **solar hybrid system** offers the best return on investment in India's sun-rich climate.
