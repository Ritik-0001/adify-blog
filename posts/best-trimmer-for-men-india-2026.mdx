## Beard Trimmer vs Body Groomer — Key Difference

**Beard trimmers:** Precision blades optimised for facial hair length maintenance (0.5mm–35mm). Narrower head for jawline definition and mustache detailing.

**Body groomers:** Wider head, skin-safe blades (no nicks on flat body skin), and often waterproof for shower use. Not ideal for beard precision.

**All-body trimmers:** Multi-head trimmers that include both beard precision attachments and body grooming attachments — most practical for Indian men who want one device.
