India's humid climate, hard water conditions, and diverse hair types (thick straight, wavy, coiled) create specific hair dryer requirements. A good hair dryer for Indian conditions balances fast drying power (critical in humidity) with heat protection for hair health.

---

## What Indian Buyers Need in a Hair Dryer

**Wattage:** 1,800W minimum for effective drying — lower wattage dryers take too long in India's humid conditions and may leave hair semi-damp, worsening frizz. 2,000W is ideal.

**Ionic technology:** Negative ions neutralise positive charges in wet hair, reducing static and frizz. Critical for India's high-humidity climates (Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata) where frizz is a major concern.

**Cool shot button:** Locks in style after drying — important for maintaining volume in India's heat.

**Multiple heat and speed settings:** Low heat for fine hair (common in South India), high heat for thick North Indian hair types.

**Concentrator nozzle:** For directional drying — included with all decent dryers. Diffuser attachment for curly/wavy hair is a significant bonus.
