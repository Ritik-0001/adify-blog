## What to Expect Under ₹1,000

**Battery-powered (AA cell):** Almost all electric toothbrushes in this price range use AA batteries. Expect 4–8 weeks per pair of batteries. Ongoing battery cost: approximately ₹40–₹60 every 1–2 months.

**Technology:** Oscillating or sonic vibration — not as powerful as rechargeable models but still 2–5x more effective at plaque removal than manual brushing according to clinical literature.

**No pressure sensor, no timer in basic models.** Some mid-range models in this bracket have a 2-minute timer.

**Replacement heads:** At this price, replacement heads are often ₹150–₹300 — check availability before buying.
