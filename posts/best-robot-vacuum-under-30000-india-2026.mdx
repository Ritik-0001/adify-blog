## Buying Guide

### Key Features to Look For

**Suction Power:** At ₹25,000–₹30,000, you should expect 4,000–6,500 Pa. Indian homes have fine dust that requires strong suction to lift from tile and marble grout lines. Do not accept anything below 4,000 Pa in this range.

**Navigation Type:** All models here use LiDAR — this is now a baseline. Look at the quality of the mapping software rather than just the presence of LiDAR. Roborock and Ecovacs have the most mature mapping systems.

**Mopping:** At this price, mopping should be a real feature, not just a wet pad dragged behind the robot. Look for models with vibrating or rotating mop pads. Dreame L10 Prime's dual rotating pads are the gold standard in this segment. Indian marble floors respond noticeably better to scrubbing action than passive mopping.

**Dustbin Size:** Look for 400 ml or larger. In Indian conditions, a 400 ml bin on a 3 BHK robot may fill in a single cleaning run.

**App and Wi-Fi:** Multi-floor map storage, room-specific settings, and reliable scheduling are now expected. Also check whether the app works well on Indian Android devices — most of your household members will use Android, and some apps behave differently on non-Google Pixel Android builds.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the ₹25,000–₹30,000 range worth it over ₹15,000–₹20,000 LiDAR models?**
A: Yes, meaningfully so. The upgrade in suction (4,000–6,500 Pa vs 2,000–3,000 Pa), mopping quality (rotating pads vs passive cloth), and battery life (150–260 min vs 100–150 min) makes these models significantly more capable for large Indian apartments.

**Q: Can these robots clean multiple floors of a duplex house?**
A: Yes — all models here support multi-map storage. You carry the robot to each floor, let it map, and save each map separately. The robot will remember the layout for each floor on future cleanings.

**Q: How do these handle the transition between marble floors and an area rug?**
A: Models like the Dreame L10 Prime automatically lift the mop pad when detecting carpet or rugs. The Roborock Q5 Pro and others can be set with virtual no-go zones around rugs if auto-detection is imperfect.

**Q: Do robot vacuums work well for pet hair in Indian apartments?**
A: Very well. The combination of 4,000+ Pa suction and LiDAR systematic cleaning ensures pet hair is thoroughly picked up from tile and marble. Brush roll maintenance is needed weekly to prevent tangling.
