## Display — AMOLED vs Liquid Retina

This is the most significant hardware difference between the two tablets.

**Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 — Dynamic AMOLED 120Hz:**
- True blacks (OLED can turn pixels off completely)
- 120Hz ProMotion for smooth scrolling
- Exceptional contrast ratio (1,000,000:1)
- HDR10+ certified
- Vibrant colours preferred for media consumption

**iPad Air M2 — Liquid Retina IPS 60Hz:**
- More accurate colour reproduction (better for photo/video editing work)
- No PWM dimming (better for users sensitive to screen flicker)
- True Tone colour adjustment to ambient light
- Anti-reflective coating reduces glare
- 60Hz is the weakest iPad Air spec — notably behind 120Hz competition

**Verdict:** Samsung Tab S9 wins the display comparison — AMOLED at 120Hz is objectively better for media consumption. The iPad Air's display advantage is accuracy and True Tone, which matter for creative professionals.

---

## Processor — M2 vs Snapdragon 8 Gen 2

**Apple M2:**
- Single-core performance: 40–50% faster than Snapdragon 8 Gen 2
- Multi-core: Comparable to Snapdragon 8 Gen 2
- Neural engine: Best-in-class AI processing
- Optimised for iPadOS — efficiency is exceptional
- Power consumption: Remarkably efficient for performance delivered

**Snapdragon 8 Gen 2:**
- Multi-core performance: Comparable to M2 in Android-optimised tasks
- Gaming: Adreno 740 GPU — excellent Android gaming performance
- Runs warmer under sustained load than M2
- Better suited for Android's memory management patterns

**Verdict:** M2 is faster for single-core (creative apps, professional software). Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 is more efficient for gaming at sustained loads. For professional creative work → iPad Air. For gaming → Tab S9.

## Stylus — Apple Pencil vs S Pen

**Apple Pencil 2nd Gen (₹11,900 additional):**
- Magnetic charging (attaches to iPad side)
- 4,096 pressure levels
- Double-tap gesture
- Best stylus for professional illustration (Procreate integration)

**Samsung S Pen (included with Tab S9):**
- Included in box — no additional cost
- 4,096 pressure levels
- Bluetooth button (remote shutter, presentation control)
- Samsung Notes deep integration

**Value verdict:** S Pen is included. Apple Pencil costs ₹11,900 extra — pushing the iPad Air's total cost to ₹71,800 for stylus use vs ₹65,999 for Tab S9 with S Pen. Samsung wins the value comparison significantly.

## App Ecosystem — The Decisive Factor

**iPad Air advantages:**
- Procreate (₹999) — the best drawing/illustration app on any mobile platform. No Android equivalent.
- Final Cut Pro for iPad — professional video editing. iPad-exclusive.
- GoodNotes 5 — the best handwritten note app. Android version is significantly inferior.
- Logic Pro for iPad — professional music production. iPad-exclusive.
- 3D scanning and AR apps — better ecosystem on iPad
- Microsoft 365 full features — Word, Excel, PowerPoint with full desktop functionality

**Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 advantages:**
- Full file system access (Android)
- Sideloading APKs (apps not on Play Store)
- Samsung DeX (desktop mode)
- Google Play Store — more free apps
- Better gaming titles available on Android
- USB-C full OTG support (connect external hard drives, USB cameras)

**The honest verdict:** For professional creative workflows — Procreate, video editing, music production — iPad Air is the only choice. For productivity flexibility, gaming, and Android ecosystem → Tab S9.
