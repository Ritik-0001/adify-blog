## Buying Guide: Budget Gaming Headsets

**Wired vs wireless:** Under ₹3,000, choose wired. Budget wireless headsets compromise on audio quality and battery reliability. Save up for HyperX Cloud Alpha Wireless (₹12,000+) if you want wireless.

**7.1 surround:** Virtual 7.1 is software-simulated — it adds immersion in open-world games but doesn't provide the same directional precision as natural binaural audio in good stereo headsets. For FPS competitive play, good stereo drivers > virtual 7.1.

**Driver size:**
- 40mm: Standard — balanced performance for gaming
- 50mm: More bass-heavy — better for immersive gaming, slightly worse for precise audio positioning

**Connection:**
- 3.5mm: Works on PC, consoles, phones — universal
- USB: PC only — enables virtual surround via software, often RGB
