India has one of the world's highest road accident rates — 1.5 lakh deaths annually, with hit-and-run incidents and disputed liability claims a daily occurrence. Dash cameras have become essential for Indian drivers navigating aggressive traffic, providing evidence for insurance claims and legal disputes.

---

## Why Dash Cameras Are Essential in India

**Hit-and-run documentation:** India's hit-and-run rates are among the world's highest. Dash cam footage provides definitive evidence of vehicle number plates and incident circumstances.

**Insurance claim disputes:** Indian insurance companies routinely dispute liability in multi-vehicle accidents. Video evidence reduces claim processing time from months to weeks and prevents fraudulent claims against you.

**Police documentation:** In many Indian cities, dash cam footage is accepted as evidence at police stations — increasingly important for accident FIR filing.

**Parking damage:** Parking lot scratches and minor collisions without noted damage are extremely common in India's congested urban parking. Parking mode (continuous recording with motion detection) captures these incidents.
