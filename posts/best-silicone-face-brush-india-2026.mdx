## Why Silicone Face Brushes for India

**Humidity and bacteria:** India's relative humidity (60–90% in monsoon) creates ideal bacterial growth conditions in porous materials. Bristle face brushes — even when dried — retain moisture in bristle bases where bacteria multiply. Silicone's non-porous surface eliminates this.

**Indian skin sensitivity:** Silicone bristles are 35× softer than standard nylon bristles — appropriate for India's darker skin tones (Fitzpatrick III–V) that are more prone to micro-irritation damage than lighter skin.

**Easy cleaning:** Silicone heads wash clean completely — no residual shampoo, face wash, or makeup. Critical for Indian skincare routines where product residue on brushes can cause breakouts.
