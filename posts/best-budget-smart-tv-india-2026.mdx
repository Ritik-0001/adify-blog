## Budget Smart TV Buying Guide

**Android TV vs Google TV.** Google TV is Android TV with a redesigned homescreen that adds personalised content recommendations across apps. Both run the same apps. Google TV is slightly more modern; Android TV is more stable on budget hardware.

**HD Ready vs Full HD.** At 32 inches, HD Ready (1366x768) is adequate for 6-10 feet viewing distance. Full HD (1920x1080) is a meaningful upgrade if you sit closer or watch a lot of detailed sports content. Most TVs under ₹15000 are HD Ready — Full HD at this price point is a genuine differentiator.

**Sound quality matters.** Indian households typically watch TV without a soundbar. 20W+ stereo output provides adequate bedroom volume. 10W mono speakers (common in budget TVs) are notably thin and fatiguing over long sessions.
