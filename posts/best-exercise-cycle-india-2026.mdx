Exercise cycles (stationary bikes) are one of the best home fitness investments for Indian apartments — quieter than treadmills (no floor impact), smaller footprint, and effective for cardiovascular fitness and lower-body strength. Here are the best picks in 2026.

---

## Types of Exercise Cycles

**Upright bike:** Traditional bicycle position, most compact, best for general cardio. Suitable for small apartments.

**Recumbent bike:** Seat with backrest, rider reclines slightly — less joint stress on knees and back. Best for older users or those with knee pain.

**Spin bike:** High-resistance flywheel, clip-in pedals, adjustable resistance — simulates road cycling. Best for intense cardio workouts.

## Exercise Cycle vs Treadmill for Indian Homes

| Feature | Exercise Cycle | Treadmill |
|---|---|---|
| Noise | Very low (magnetic) | Moderate-high |
| Floor impact | None | Significant |
| Space | Smaller | Larger |
| Apartment suitability | Excellent | Needs mat, consideration |
| Calories burned | 400–600/hr | 500–700/hr |
| Joint stress | Low | Moderate |
| Price | ₹8,000–₹25,000 | ₹10,000–₹50,000 |

For Indian apartment buildings with downstairs neighbours, **exercise cycles are significantly more neighbour-friendly** than treadmills.
