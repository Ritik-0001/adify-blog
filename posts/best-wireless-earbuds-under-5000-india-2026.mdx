## Buying Guide: Budget TWS Earbuds in 2026

**What ₹5,000 gets you in 2026:**
- Active noise cancellation (35–50dB) — meaningfully reduces background noise
- 10–12mm drivers — decent sound quality and bass response
- 30–50 hour total battery (buds + case)
- IPX4–IPX5 water resistance for sweat and rain
- Fast charging (10 min = 1–2 hours playback)

**What you won't get:**
- LDAC Hi-Res Wireless (requires ₹15,000+ earbuds)
- Transparency mode as natural as AirPods Pro or Sony XM5
- Wear detection sensors (auto-pause)
- Reliable multipoint on budget earbuds

**ANC at this price:** Budget ANC reduces steady low-frequency noise (office hum, AC, transport rumble) well. High-frequency sounds (conversations, sudden noises) pass through more. It's a genuine improvement over no ANC, even if it's not Sony/Apple quality.

**Codec tip:** Most budget earbuds support SBC and AAC. Nothing Ear (a) supports LHDC 5.0 — if your Android phone supports it, the audio quality jump is noticeable.
