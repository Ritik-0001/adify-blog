## Indian Tile Cleaning Challenges

**Vitrified tile maintenance:** Premium vitrified tiles in Indian living rooms and kitchens develop micro-scratches from abrasive manual cleaning. Electric brushes with appropriate soft pads clean without scratching.

**Grout blackening:** Indian bathroom grout blackens rapidly from soap, water, and humidity. Narrow grout cleaning brushes with motorised action clean grout lines that standard mops cannot reach.

**Hard water white deposits:** Indian water leaves calcium carbonate deposits on bathroom tiles. Acidic cleaners (vinegar or commercial) + electric scrubbing removes mineral deposits chemically and mechanically.
