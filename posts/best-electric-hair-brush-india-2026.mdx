Electric hair brushes combine the styling motion of a brush with heat — smoothing and straightening Indian hair in one pass without the technique required by flat irons. Here are the best electric hair brushes for Indian buyers in 2026.

---

## Electric Hair Brush Types

**Straightening brush:** Bristles embedded with heated plates — brush through hair and it straightens. Faster than flat iron for everyday smoothing. Best for Indian women who want straight results without salon technique.

**Hot air brush:** Combines blow dryer airflow with a barrel brush — dries and styles simultaneously. Best for Indian women who currently blow-dry with a round brush (replaces two tools with one).

**Heated paddle brush:** Flat paddled heated surface — smooths and detangles without straightening as aggressively as a straightening iron. Best for everyday frizz control.
