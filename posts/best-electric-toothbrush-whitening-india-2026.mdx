## Buying Guide: Whitening Electric Toothbrushes India

**What electric toothbrushes can whiten:**
- Tea, coffee, and food surface stains (extrinsic staining)
- Tobacco surface stains (partial reduction)
- General surface discolouration

**What electric toothbrushes cannot whiten:**
- Intrinsic staining (tetracycline antibiotics, fluorosis — yellow-white patches common in Indian groundwater areas)
- Natural tooth colour below surface stains
- Old fillings or crowns

**For intrinsic staining:** Professional bleaching (₹5,000–₹20,000 per treatment) or veneers are required — toothbrushes cannot address this.

**Whitening toothpaste combinations:** Pairing a whitening mode electric brush with Colgate Optic White or Oral-B 3D White toothpaste accelerates visible results — abrasive toothpaste + mechanical polishing action work synergistically.

**Expectation management:** Electric toothbrush whitening removes existing surface stains — teeth will appear whiter (lighter shade) by returning to their natural baseline. They will not go beyond natural tooth colour without chemical bleaching.
