## India 5G Network Status 2026

**Jio 5G (True 5G SA — Standalone):** Fastest 5G in India. Jio's standalone 5G architecture doesn't rely on 4G fallback — delivers lower latency. Available in 500+ cities. Sub-6GHz (n28, n78) and mmWave in select locations.

**Airtel 5G (NSA — Non-Standalone):** Uses existing 4G infrastructure. Slightly higher latency than Jio. Available in 500+ cities. Sub-6GHz only.

**Both Jio and Airtel 5G are free upgrades** on existing postpaid/prepaid plans (no 5G surcharge).

**5G Band Compatibility:** Ensure your phone supports SA mode 5G for Jio, and NSA mode for Airtel. All phones in this list support both.

## 5G Battery Reality Check

5G connectivity uses more battery than 4G. If you're not in 5G coverage area, set the network to 4G manually — saves 10–15% battery life.

6000mAh batteries (iQOO Z9, POCO F6) are recommended for users who will stay on 5G throughout the day.
