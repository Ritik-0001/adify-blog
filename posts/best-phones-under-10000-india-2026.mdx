## Buying Guide: Phones Under ₹10,000 in India 2026

**5G vs 4G at this price:**
5G phones under ₹10,000 use MediaTek Dimensity 6100+ — a decent but not powerful chipset. 4G phones at the same price often have better chipsets (Helio G85, Snapdragon 680) for day-to-day performance. If 5G coverage is available in your city, choose 5G. If not, a 4G phone will perform better.

**What to prioritise:**
1. Battery (5000mAh+ is standard at this price)
2. Chipset (Snapdragon 680 or Helio G85 for best performance)
3. RAM (6GB minimum — look for virtual RAM expansion)
4. Camera (50MP doesn't always mean best photos — check samples)

**Avoid phones with:**
- Less than 4GB RAM
- eMMC 5.1 storage (slow app loading) — look for UFS 2.1+
- Only 1 year of software updates
