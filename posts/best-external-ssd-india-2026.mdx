## External SSD vs Portable HDD for India

**External SSD advantages:** 5–10× faster transfers, shock-proof (no moving parts), lighter, more compact. Critical for video creators, photographers, and professionals.

**Portable HDD advantages:** 3–5× lower price per GB — a 4TB HDD costs less than a 1TB SSD. Better for cold storage of large archives.

**Verdict:** For active work (video editing, frequent file transfers), SSD. For archive storage, HDD remains cost-effective.
