Air fryers have become India's most-gifted kitchen appliance — combining the cultural love of fried foods (samosa, pakoda, cutlet, puri) with growing health consciousness. Here are the best air fryers for Indian cooking in 2026.

## Why Air Fryers Work Well for Indian Cooking

**Indian fried food portfolio:** Samosa, pakoda, kachori, aloo tikki, murukku, banana chips, paneer tikka, tandoori chicken — all Indian fried and grilled foods that air fryers handle well. 80% less oil than deep frying with comparable crispiness.

**Snack culture:** India's chai-time snack culture makes an air fryer a daily-use appliance — frozen samosas, bread pakoda, and leftover roti revival take 5–8 minutes vs 15–20 minutes oven reheating.

**Oil savings:** At ₹150–₹200/litre for refined oil and Indian households deep-frying multiple times weekly, oil savings from air fryer use can recoup the purchase price within 6–12 months.
