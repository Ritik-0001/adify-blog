## Ergonomics Checklist

**The right setup:** Laptop screen at eye level + external keyboard at elbow height + mouse beside keyboard. This is the ergonomic holy trinity that eliminates neck and wrist strain.

**Cost to achieve it:**
- Laptop stand: ₹2,500
- External keyboard: ₹7,000–₹9,000
- Wireless mouse: ₹3,000–₹9,000
- Total: ₹12,500–₹20,000 for a proper ergonomic setup

This investment pays back in productivity and prevents the physiotherapy bills that come from 3 years of bad posture.
