**3. 30-day battery:** Most toothbrushes need weekly charging. The X Pro Elite charges once per month — an advantage that sounds minor but becomes significant over years of use.

## AI Brushing Analysis — How It Actually Works

The Oclean app receives motion data from the brush via Bluetooth. After each brushing session:

- **Coverage map:** Shows which zones (upper left, lower right, etc.) received adequate brushing time and which were rushed or missed
- **Pressure feedback:** Flags if excess pressure was applied during brushing
- **Duration tracking:** Records total brush time and whether 2 minutes was reached
- **Trend analysis:** Shows improvement or decline in brushing habits over weeks

**Honest assessment of the AI:** The coverage feedback is the most useful feature — most people consistently miss the same zones (typically back molars, lower front inner surface). Identifying these patterns and correcting them is clinically valuable. The AI doesn't do magic — it's motion-sensor data with smart visualisation, but that's genuinely useful.

## 12 Cleaning Modes — Which Are Actually Useful

Oclean markets 12 modes, but in practice, most users will use 2–3:

**Most useful modes:**
- **Clean:** Standard daily brushing (most users)
- **Whitening:** Higher frequency for surface stain removal (tea/coffee drinkers)
- **Sensitive:** Reduced power for inflamed gums or sensitive days
- **Massage:** Gum stimulation — a gentler, more massage-like motion

**Less useful modes:** The remaining 8 are combinations and variations that most users won't notice a difference between. The 4 modes above cover all practical needs.

## Build Quality and Durability

The X Pro Elite is manufactured to a high standard — the metal-look finish is premium for the price. The touchscreen is responsive and bright. The magnetic charging connection is reliable.

**Concern:** Oclean's after-sales service in India is limited. If the brush develops a fault after the warranty period, replacement parts and service centres are harder to access than Philips or Oral-B. This is a genuine long-term consideration.

**Brush head availability:** Oclean brush heads are available on Amazon India — less convenient than Oral-B heads (available in most Indian pharmacies) but accessible. Price: ₹300–₹500 per head.
