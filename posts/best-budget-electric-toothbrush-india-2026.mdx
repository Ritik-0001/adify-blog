## Buying Guide: Budget Electric Toothbrushes India 2026

**Non-negotiables at any price:**
- **2-minute timer** — if a budget brush lacks this, don't buy it
- **Rechargeable battery** (preferred over disposable AA/AAA for regular use)
- **BIS certification** — confirms safe electrical standards

**What to skip at budget pricing:**
- Multiple cleaning modes (nice to have, not essential)
- Pressure sensor (available from ₹1,499+ only)
- App connectivity (available from ₹699 with Xiaomi)

**Replacement heads at budget pricing:** Oral-B heads are ₹300–₹500. Generic Oral-B-compatible heads from Indian brands are ₹150–₹250 — work fine for budget users. Avoid unbranded heads from unknown sources.

**Budget rule:** Spend the minimum needed to get a 2-minute timer and rechargeable battery. Everything else is a bonus. Even the cheapest timed electric toothbrush outperforms an untimed manual brush for most users.
