## 6kg vs 7kg — Which to Choose?

At similar prices, a 7kg machine is almost always the better buy — the capacity difference allows washing a full set of bedsheets in one cycle. Choose 6kg only if:
- Space is severely limited (6kg front-loads are typically 3–5cm shallower)
- Household is reliably 2 people
- Price difference between 6kg and 7kg is more than ₹2,000
