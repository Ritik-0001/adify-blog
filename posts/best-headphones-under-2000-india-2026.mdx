## What to Look For Under ₹2000

**Battery life:** Look for 12+ hours minimum. Under this threshold, you'll be charging every day. boAt and Noise both exceed this; JBL hits 16 hours.

**Charging port:** USB-C is preferable for convenience; micro-USB still dominates this price range. Check before buying if this matters to you.

**Sound signature:** boAt = heavy bass. JBL = balanced V-curve. Noise = cleaner midrange. Choose based on your primary music genre — bass-heavy for Bollywood/EDM, balanced for pop/rock/classical.

**Build quality:** At under ₹2000, expect plastic construction. Foldable designs are more portable but have a hinge that's a long-term weak point. If you're buying for daily commute use, factor in durability.
