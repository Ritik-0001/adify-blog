## Why Do Gaming Laptops Overheat in India?

India's climate creates a uniquely challenging environment for gaming laptops. During summer months (April to June), ambient temperatures in most Indian cities range from 30°C to 42°C. In non-air-conditioned rooms, temperatures can exceed 38°C.

Laptop cooling systems are engineered around an ambient temperature of 25°C (the global standard). When ambient temperature rises by 10°C, the internal components run roughly 8–12°C hotter even before you start gaming. Add gaming load on top of that, and a gaming laptop running at 78°C in a Delhi winter might hit 90–95°C on a Mumbai summer afternoon in the same room.

At 95°C and above, Intel and AMD processors begin throttling — reducing clock speeds to lower heat output. This directly causes FPS drops, stutters, and in severe cases, the laptop shuts down entirely to protect the hardware.

A cooling pad addresses the problem at the intake. Most gaming laptops pull cool air through bottom vents. A cooling pad forces additional cool air through those vents, reducing inlet temperature and therefore reducing component temperatures under load.

## Which Gaming Laptops Benefit Most?

Cooling pads deliver the biggest benefit to these categories:

**Thin gaming laptops** (Asus TUF A15, Lenovo IdeaPad Gaming 3): Thin chassis have limited internal airflow. The cooling pad adds airflow that the thin design cannot accommodate internally.

**Entry-level gaming laptops** (HP Pavilion Gaming, MSI Thin GF63): These have smaller cooling systems designed for cost reduction. They thermal throttle earlier than premium gaming laptops, so external cooling helps more.

**Laptops in non-AC rooms**: As shown in the data above, the benefit of a cooling pad increases proportionally with ambient temperature.

**Laptops used for longer gaming sessions**: Temperature buildup is cumulative. After 90 minutes of gaming, a laptop that starts at 80°C will creep toward 88°C. A cooling pad that keeps the starting point at 74°C keeps the 90-minute temperature below the throttle threshold.

## What About Premium Gaming Laptops (Asus ROG, Razer Blade)?

Premium gaming laptops with advanced vapor chamber cooling already perform well thermally. The Asus ROG Strix Scar or Razer Blade 15 with factory-tuned fan curves might only see a 3–5°C improvement from a cooling pad.
