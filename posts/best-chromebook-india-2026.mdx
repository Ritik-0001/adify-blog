Chromebooks run ChromeOS — Google's lightweight operating system designed for web-based computing, Google Workspace, and Android app compatibility. In India, Chromebooks are increasingly relevant for educational institutions adopting Google Classroom and for users whose computing needs are primarily online.

---

## Who Should Consider a Chromebook in India?

**Ideal for:**
- Students using Google Classroom and Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Meet)
- Users whose primary needs are: web browsing, YouTube, email, video calls
- Educational institutions replacing tablets with low-cost computing
- Secondary laptops for travel where the primary machine stays home

**Not suitable for:**
- Office software requiring Windows-native apps (specific accounting software, tally, Windows ERP)
- Video editing or photo editing (limited native app support)
- Gaming (beyond basic Android games from Play Store)
- Programming requiring native Linux development environments (WSL2, Visual Studio)
