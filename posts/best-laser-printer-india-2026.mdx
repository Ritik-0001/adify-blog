## Why Choose a Laser Printer in India?

In a market dominated by ink tank printers, laser printers still hold a specific and important niche. Here's when laser is the right answer:

**Laser wins when:**
- You print primarily text documents (reports, invoices, legal documents)
- Your printer sits idle for weeks between uses — toner never dries out
- You need high-volume printing (500+ pages/month) at consistent quality
- Print speed matters — 20 ppm vs 10 ipm on inkjet is a real difference
- You need sharp, smudge-proof text output every time

**Laser loses when:**
- You print colour photos or colour-heavy graphics
- Your print volumes are low (under 100 pages/month) — toner is expensive per cartridge
- You need a budget below ₹7,000

The core laser advantage for India specifically: **toner doesn't clog.** India's frequent power cuts and irregular printing schedules mean printers often sit unused for days or weeks. Inkjet heads can dry and clog in that time. Laser printers simply resume printing when you need them, no maintenance required.
