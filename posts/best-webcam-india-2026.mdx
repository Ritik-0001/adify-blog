## Webcam Considerations for India

**Low-light in Indian rooms:** Indian home offices commonly have overhead lighting positioned behind the user (creating silhouette), or single-bulb rooms with directional lighting. Webcams with HDR and light correction software handle these scenarios better than standard webcams.

**Video call quality:** Post-COVID India has normalised video calls for client meetings, interviews, and team calls. A ₹500 laptop webcam creates poor first impressions in professional contexts — an upgrade to even a ₹3,000 webcam is visually significant.

**Content creation:** Indian YouTube and social media creators increasingly use webcams for face-cam recording, talking-head videos, and live streaming.
