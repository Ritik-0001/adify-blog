## Video Doorbells in the Indian Context

Video doorbells are still an emerging category in India compared to the US or UK market. Indian homes typically use a separate doorbell chime and a standalone security camera, but the combination device is gaining traction — particularly in apartments, gated communities, and urban homes where knowing who is at the door before opening it adds significant security. Most Indian apartments have existing doorbell wiring that can power a video doorbell, making installation simpler than expected.
