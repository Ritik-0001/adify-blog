CA students and professionals in India have very specific laptop needs — Tally Prime, Microsoft Excel (heavy models), PDF annotation, virtual classrooms, and occasionally Zoho Books or SAP. The good news: you don't need an expensive gaming laptop. You need reliability, battery life, and a display good enough for 10-hour study sessions.

---

## What CA Students Actually Need

- **Tally Prime / Tally ERP:** Windows-only software — eliminates MacBook for CA students on budget
- **MS Excel with large workbooks:** Needs at least 8GB RAM; 16GB for complex models
- **PDF tools (Adobe Acrobat, Smallpdf):** Any modern laptop handles this
- **Virtual classes (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet):** Needs a good webcam and mic
- **Battery life:** 6+ hours for long study sessions and classes
