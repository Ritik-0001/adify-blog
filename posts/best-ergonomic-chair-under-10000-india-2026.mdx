## What Separates a ₹10,000 Chair from a ₹5,000 Chair

The jump from ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 brings meaningful ergonomic improvements:

**Adjustable armrests:** Fixed or 1D armrests are common under ₹5,000. At ₹7,000–₹10,000, you start getting 2D and 4D options that let you customize positioning for your desk height and typing style.

**Adjustable lumbar:** Budget chairs have a fixed curve. Mid-range chairs let you move the lumbar support up or down to match your spine, which is a genuine ergonomic benefit.

**Better mesh longevity:** Cheaper mesh stretches and sags within 12–18 months of daily use. The mesh on ₹8,000+ chairs maintains its tension for 3–5 years.

**Tilt mechanisms:** Synchronized tilt (where seat and back move together) is a comfort upgrade that reduces spinal compression during reclining.
