## Buying Guide: Laptop Bags in 2026

**Size guide:**
- 13"–14" laptop: Most bags fit comfortably; look for 14" rating to avoid excess slack
- 15.6" laptop: Specify 15.6" compatible — many bags labelled "laptop bags" only fit up to 14"
- 17" gaming laptop: Confirm 17" compatibility explicitly — most bags don't fit

**Padding types:**
- Neoprene sleeve: Soft, shock-absorbing — best for drops and bumps
- Hardshell panel: Rigid protection — best for side impacts
- Air-cushion system: Found in premium bags — absorbs shock from drops

**Water resistance vs waterproof:**
- Water-resistant: Handles light rain and splashes — adequate for most Indian use
- Waterproof: Sealed construction for heavy rain — usually more expensive
- Note: No bag is fully waterproof unless rated IPX6+ or higher

**What matters most:**
1. Dedicated padded laptop compartment (prevents laptop from touching other items)
2. Comfortable straps for your commute distance
3. Enough pockets for your daily carry
