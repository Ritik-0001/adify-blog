## Understanding CCTV Options for Indian Homes

Before buying, decide between two approaches. **Wired CCTV** (DVR plus coaxial cameras) requires professional installation but offers the most reliable 24/7 recording with no WiFi dependency — ideal for standalone homes and bungalows. **WiFi cameras** require no cabling, set up in minutes, and suit renters, apartments, and anyone who wants to add cameras quickly without an electrician.
