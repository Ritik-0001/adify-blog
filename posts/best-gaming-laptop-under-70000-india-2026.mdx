## Buying Tips: Gaming Laptops Under ₹70,000

**RAM matters:** 16GB DDR5 is the minimum for 2026 gaming. Avoid 8GB RAM variants — they will limit performance in modern titles.

**TGP (Total Graphics Power):** RTX 4060 in a ₹70K laptop runs at 80W–140W TGP depending on the model. Higher TGP = better FPS. Check the spec sheet — manufacturers don't always advertise TGP prominently.

**Cooling is critical:** Gaming laptops throttle when hot. ASUS TUF and MSI have consistently better cooling than Acer in this budget. Check gaming benchmarks under sustained loads, not just Cinebench bursts.

**Display refresh rate:** Buy at minimum 144Hz. 60Hz gaming displays are wasteful with a gaming GPU.
