## How to Use a Hair Dryer Brush on Indian Hair

1. Wash hair and towel-dry to 70–80% dry (not fully wet — reduces drying time and heat exposure).
2. Apply heat protectant spray evenly through hair.
3. Section hair into 4–6 parts.
4. Glide dryer brush from roots to tips in 3–4 smooth passes per section.
5. For inward curl: rotate brush inward on the last pass and hold for 3–5 seconds.

Indian hair typically takes 20–30 minutes for a complete blowout with a dryer brush vs 45+ minutes with separate hairdryer and brush technique.
