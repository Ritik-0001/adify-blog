## Electric Dish Scrubber for Indian Cooking Residue

**Indian cooking produces intense residue:** Turmeric, red chilli, mustard seed, and ghee leave stubborn stains on Indian cookware. Electric spinning brushes with the correct detergent remove this 3–4× faster than manual scrubbing.

**Water usage:** Electric scrubbers with soap-dispensing handles use less water than traditional Indian vessel-soaking and scrubbing — relevant for water-scarce Indian cities.
