## When to Choose 8kg vs 7kg

**Choose 8kg if:**
- Family of 4–5 people
- You wash bedsheets, blankets, and curtains regularly
- You prefer 1–2 laundry sessions per week over more frequent smaller loads
- You have large Indian garments (salwar suits, sarees, dhotis)

**Stick with 7kg if:**
- Family of 3 people or less
- Budget is tight (7kg is ₹1,500–₹3,000 cheaper in the same model line)
- Laundry area space is limited
