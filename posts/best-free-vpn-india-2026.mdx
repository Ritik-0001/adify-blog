## Why Free VPNs Should Be Approached Carefully

**The product risk.** Many free VPNs fund their service by selling user browsing data to advertisers — the opposite of privacy. Stick to free tiers of established paid providers (ProtonVPN, Windscribe) with audited no-logs policies and transparent business models.

**Speed limitations.** Free VPN servers are shared with many users, resulting in congestion during peak hours. For streaming and video calls, expect 30–50% speed reduction versus no VPN. Paid VPNs typically deliver 90%+ of your native connection speed.

**Data caps.** 2–10GB/month is adequate for basic privacy browsing. For streaming, torrenting, or full-time VPN use, a paid plan at ₹149–199/month is necessary.
