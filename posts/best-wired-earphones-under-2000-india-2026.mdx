## Buying Guide: Wired IEM Earphones in 2026

**Why choose wired over TWS in 2026:**
- Zero audio latency — important for gaming and video editing
- No charging required — always ready
- Better sound quality per rupee — ₹1,500 wired IEM > ₹1,500 TWS earbuds for audio quality
- More reliable connection — no Bluetooth drops

**Sound signatures explained:**
- **V-shaped** (KZ, boAt): Boosted bass and treble, recessed mids — energetic and fun
- **Neutral/balanced** (Sony, Sennheiser): Flat frequency response — accurate to original recording
- **Harman target** (Moondrop): Slight bass lift over neutral — the scientifically preferred signature

**Driver types:**
- **Dynamic Driver (DD):** Single driver — warm sound, good bass, most common
- **Balanced Armature (BA):** More detail, less bass — used in hearing aids and professional monitors
- **Hybrid (DD+BA):** Best of both — detail + bass, usually ₹1,000+

**3.5mm vs USB-C:** Most modern phones removed the 3.5mm jack — check your phone. USB-C to 3.5mm adapters work but add latency. For phones without 3.5mm, TWS earbuds or USB-C native IEMs are more convenient.
